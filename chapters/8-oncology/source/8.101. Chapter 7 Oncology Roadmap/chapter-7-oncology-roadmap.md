## 2. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Topics (Priority Order)

### Must Know (Core)
1. **Cancer Biology & Hallmarks** — carcinogenesis, hallmarks, TME
2. **TNM Staging & Prognostication** — TNM 8th ed, biomarkers, MDT
3. **Oncologic Emergencies** — MSCC, SVCO, TLS, Hypercalcaemia, Neutropenic Sepsis
3. **Lung Cancer** — NSCLC molecular subtypes, SCLC, staging
4. **Breast Cancer** — molecular subtypes, adjuvant/metastatic treatment
5. **Colorectal Cancer** — MSI/MMR, adjuvant/neoadjuvant, screening
6. **Haematological Malignancies** — leukaemias, lymphomas, myeloma, MPN
7. **Oncologic Emergencies** — MSCC, SVCO, TLS, Neutropenic Sepsis
8. **Palliative Care** — pain ladder, symptom control, EOL

### Should Know (High Yield)
9. **Breast Cancer Molecular Subtypes** — ER/HER2, CDK4/6, PARPi
10. **Lung Cancer Molecular** — EGFR, ALK, ROS1, KRAS, immunotherapy
11. **Colorectal MSI/MMR** — Lynch, immunotherapy
12. **Haematology** — AML/APL, CLL, CML, DLBCL, Myeloma
13. **Upper GI** — Oesophageal/Gastric, HER2, CLDN18.2
11. **Hepatobiliary** — HCC, Cholangiocarcinoma, Pancreatic
12. **Urological** — RCC, Bladder, Prostate, Testicular
13. **Gynae** — Ovarian, Endometrial, Cervical
14. **Head/Neck/Thyroid** — HPV+ OPSCC, Thyroid
15. **Skin** — Melanoma, NMSC
16. **CNS** — Gliomas, Mets
17. **Sarcomas** — STS, Bone
18. **Paediatric** — Leukaemia, Solid tumours

### Nice to Know (Advanced)
19. **Upper GI** — Small bowel, GIST
19. **Hepatobiliary** — Cholangiocarcinoma, Ampullary
20. **Urological** — Bladder, Penile
21. **Haematology** — MDS, CMML, MPN
21. **Gynae** — Vulval, GTN
22. **Head/Neck** — Nasopharyngeal, Salivary
22. **Skin** — NMSC, Merkel, Kaposi
23. **CNS** — Meningioma, PCNSL
24. **Sarcomas** — STS, Bone
24. **Paediatric** — Neuroblastoma, Wilms, Rhabdo

26. **Paraneoplastic** — Endocrine, Neuro, Derm, Haeme
27. **Palliative** — Pain, EOL, Ethics
28. **Survivorship** — Cardiotoxicity, Second cancers, Fertility

## 4. Study Sequence (Recommended)

### Phase 1: Foundations (Week 1-2)
- Cancer Biology & Hallmarks
- TNM Staging & Prognostication
- Cancer Genetics & Genomics
- Tumour Immunology
- TNM Staging & Prognostication

### Phase 2: Core Solid Tumours (Week 3-5)
- Lung Cancer (NSCLC, SCLC, Mesothelioma)
- Breast Cancer (Early, Advanced, Molecular Subtypes)
- Colorectal Cancer (Colon, Rectal, Anal, Hereditary)
- Upper GI Cancers (Oesophageal, Gastric, Small Bowel)

### Phase 3: GI & Hepatobiliary (Week 6)
- Hepatobiliary & Pancreatic Cancers
- Colorectal Cancer (completed)
- Upper GI (completed)

### Phase 4: Haematology & Urology (Week 7-8)
- Haematological Malignancies (Leukaemias, Lymphomas, Myeloma, MPN)
- Urological Cancers (RCC, Bladder, Prostate, Testicular)

### Phase 5: Gynae, Head/Neck, Skin, CNS (Week 9)
- Gynaecological Cancers
- Head, Neck & Thyroid
- Skin Cancer
- CNS Tumours

### Phase 6: Special Topics (Week 10)
- Sarcomas & Paediatric Cancers
- Oncologic Emergencies (detail)
- Paraneoplastic Syndromes
- Palliative Care
- Survivorship & Late Effects

### Phase 7: Integration & Revision (Week 11-12)
- Mixed cases, Viva practice, SBA banks
- Staging practice (TNM)
- Biomarker interpretation
- Emergency management drills

## 5. Exam Pattern Notes

### FCPS Specific
- Heavy emphasis on clinical vignettes
- Staging (TNM) application
- Emergency management (MSCC, SVCO, TLS)
- Chemotherapy regimens (names, doses, toxicities)
- Radiotherapy indications
- Local guidelines (NCCN, ESMO, local)

### MRCP Specific
- Pathophysiology depth
- Trial evidence (key trials: KEYNOTE, CheckMate, etc.)
- Biomarker interpretation
- Paraneoplastic syndromes
- Palliative care ethics
- Survivorship issues
