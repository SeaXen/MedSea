Must Know (Core)
  1. Cancer Biology & Hallmarks — carcinogenesis, hallmarks, TME
  2. TNM Staging & Prognostication — TNM 8th ed, biomarkers, MDT
  3. Oncologic Emergencies — MSCC, SVCO, TLS, Hypercalcaemia, Neutropenic Sepsis
  4. Lung Cancer — NSCLC molecular subtypes, SCLC, staging
  5. Breast Cancer — molecular subtypes, adjuvant/metastatic treatment
  6. Colorectal Cancer — MSI/MMR, adjuvant/neoadjuvant, screening
  7. Haematological Malignancies — leukaemias, lymphomas, myeloma, MPN
  8. Oncologic Emergencies — MSCC, SVCO, TLS, Neutropenic Sepsis
  9. Palliative Care — pain ladder, symptom control, EOL
Should Know (High Yield)
  1. Breast Cancer Molecular Subtypes — ER/HER2, CDK4/6, PARPi

  4. Haematology — AML/APL, CLL, CML, DLBCL, Myeloma

  6. Hepatobiliary — HCC, Cholangiocarcinoma, Pancreatic
  7. Urological — RCC, Bladder, Prostate, Testicular

  11. CNS — Gliomas, Mets
  12. Sarcomas — STS, Bone
  13. Paediatric — Leukaemia, Solid tumours

5 Exam Pattern Notes
FCPS Specific
  • Heavy emphasis on clinical vignettes
  • Staging (TNM) application
  • Emergency management (MSCC, SVCO, TLS)
  • Chemotherapy regimens (names, doses, toxicities)
  • Radiotherapy indications
  • Local guidelines (NCCN, ESMO, local)
MRCP Specific
  • Pathophysiology depth
  • Trial evidence (key trials: KEYNOTE, CheckMate, etc.)
  • Biomarker interpretation
  • Paraneoplastic syndromes
  • Palliative care ethics
  • Survivorship issues
