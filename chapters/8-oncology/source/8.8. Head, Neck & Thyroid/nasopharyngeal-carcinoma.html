CHAPTER 8 ONCOLOGY / 8.8. HEAD, NECK & THYROID
Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma
1 Learning Objectives

By the end of this note you should be able to:

  • [ ] Describe EBV association and WHO Histological Types (I, II, III)
  • [ ] Apply AJCC 8th Staging for NPC
  • [ ] Prescribe RT (IMRT) for Stage I; CCRT (Cisplatin) for Stage II-IVA
  • [ ] Evaluate Induction Chemotherapy (TPF/GemCis) for Locally Advanced
  • [ ] Understand Adjuvant Chemo Controversy (Intergroup 0099 vs Modern RT)
  • [ ] Sequence Metastatic Therapy: Gem/Cis 1L → ICI (PD-1) 2L
  • [ ] Use Plasma EBV DNA for Diagnosis, Staging, Response Assessment, Surveillance
  • [ ] Manage RT Toxicities: Xerostomia, Trismus, Cranial Neuropathies, Hearing Loss, Hypothyroidism
2 Definition & Epidemiology
FeatureDetail
-----------------
**Definition**Malignant tumour of nasopharyngeal epithelium; **Strong EBV Association** (Type II/III)
**Incidence****Endemic**: SE Asia (Southern China, Hong Kong), North Africa, Arctic; **UK: ~250/year** (Rare, migrant populations)
**Prevalence**5-year OS: Stage I ~90%, Stage II ~80%, Stage III ~70%, Stage IV ~50%
**Peak Age****Bimodal**: 15-25 (EBV-driven), 50-60 (Environmental)
**Sex Ratio****M:F = 2-3:1**
**Risk Factors****EBV Infection** (Necessary but not sufficient), **Genetic** (HLA-A*0207, GWAS loci), **Diet** (Salted fish, Nitrosamines), **Smoking**, **Occupational** (Formaldehyde, Wood dust)
3 Aetiology & Pathophysiology

``mermaid flowchart LR A[EBV Infection (Latency II)] --> B[EBV Proteins Expressed] B --> B1[LMP1 (Latent Membrane Protein 1) — Constitutive CD40 Signaling → NF-κB, JAK/STAT, PI3K/AKT] B --> B2[LMP2A — BCR Mimic → PI3K/AKT, Survival] B --> B3[EBNA1 — Episome Maintenance, Immune Evasion] B1 --> C[Oncogenic Pathways] B2 --> C B3 --> C C --> D1[Cell Proliferation, Anti-Apoptosis] C --> D2[Angiogenesis (VEGF)] C --> D3[Invasion, Metastasis (MMPs)] C --> D4[Immune Evasion (PD-L1↑, HLA↓)] ``

TypeDescriptionEBVFrequency (Endemic)Prognosis
--------------------------------------------------------

EBV DNA Monitoring
TimepointClinical Utility
----------------------------
**Pre-treatment****Prognostic**: High EBV DNA → Worse OS; **Staging**: Detects Occult Mets
**Mid-RT****Early Response**: ↓EBV DNA predicts CR
**Post-RT (3mo)****Remission Confirmation**: Undetectable = CR
**Surveillance****q3mo ×2yr, q6mo ×3yr**; **Rise → Early Relapse Detection**
**Post-Salvage****Monitor Response**

8 Viva Questions (MRCP PACES / FCPS)
QuestionExpected Answer
---------------------------
**40M, Nasopharyngeal mass, Type III Undifferentiated, EBV+. T2 N1. Stage?****Stage II** (T2 N1 M0).
**Stage I NPC — Treatment?****RT Alone (IMRT) 66-70Gy to Primary + 50-54Gy Elective Neck** — **No Chemo**.
**Locally Advanced NPC — Standard CCRT?****Cisplatin 100mg/m² q3w ×3 (D1, 22, 43) + IMRT 70Gy/35fx** — **RTOG 0225 / Standard**.
**Induction Chemo — When, Regimens?****High Volume / N3 / T4**; **TPF (Doc/Cis/5-FU) ×2-3 cycles** OR **GemCis ×3 cycles** — **NARCTEC, ZHONGSHAN: Induction+CCRT > CCRT Alone**.
**Adjuvant Chemo — Intergroup 0099 vs Modern RT?****Intergroup 0099 (2D/3D RT): CCRT + Adj Cis/5-FU → OS Benefit**; **RTOG 0225 / NPC-9902 (IMRT): Adj Chemo NO OS Benefit** — **Controversial, Often Omitted**.
**Metastatic NPC 1L?****Gemcitabine 1000mg/m² d1,8 + Cisplatin 80mg/m² d1 q3w** — **Standard**.
**2L Metastatic NPC — ICI Options?****Pembrolizumab, Nivolumab, Tislelizumab, Toripalimab** — **PD-1 Inhibitors, EBV+ High Response**.
**EBV DNA Monitoring — When, Utility?****Pre: Prognostic/Staging; Mid-RT: Early Response; Post-RT 3mo: Remission; Surveillance: q3mo×2yr, q6mo×3yr — Rise = Relapse**.
**RT Toxicities NPC — Major?****Xerostomia (Parotid Sparing IMRT)**, **Trismus**, **Cranial Neuropathies (CN IX-XII)**, **Hearing Loss (Cisplatin + RT)**, **Hypothyroidism**, **Osteoradionecrosis** (Dental).
**WHO Type I vs II/III — Prognosis?****Type I (Keratinising, EBV-): Radioresistant, Worse**; **Type II/III (Non-keratinising, EBV+): Radiosensitive, Better**.
9 Confusions & Mnemonics
ConfusionClarification
--------------------------
**Adjuvant Chemo — Give or Not?****Intergroup 0099 (Old RT): YES**; **Modern IMRT Trials (RTOG 0225, NPC-9902): NO clear benefit**; **Current Practice: Often Omitted or Individualised**
**Induction Chemo — TPF vs GemCis****TPF: More Toxic (Neutropenia, Diarrhoea)**; **GemCis: Better Tolerated, Similar Efficacy** — **GemCis Preferred in Many Centres**
**EBV DNA vs Clinical Staging****EBV DNA Detects Occult Mets** (Upstages ~10-15%); **Used for Prognosis, Not Formal Staging (AJCC)**
**Type I vs II/III RT Sensitivity****Type I: Keratinising, EBV-, Radioresistant, Higher Dose/Boost Needed**; **Type II/III: Non-keratinising, EBV+, Highly Radiosensitive**
**Cisplatin Schedule in CCRT****100mg/m² q3w ×3 (Days 1, 22, 43)** — **Standard**; **Weekly Cisplatin 40mg/m²** used in some protocols (less nephrotoxicity)
**Parotid Sparing IMRT****Mean Dose <26Gy** → **Significantly Reduces Xerostomia** — **Critical Quality Metric**

Mnemonic: NPC-EBV

  • Nasopharyngeal: EBV-Associated (Latency II: LMP1/2A, EBNA1)
  • Pathology: WHO I (Keratinising, EBV-), II (Non-keratinising, EBV+), III (Undifferentiated, EBV+)
  • CCRT: Cisplatin 100mg/m² q3w ×3 + IMRT 70Gy (Stage II-IVA)
  • Early Stage I: RT Alone (IMRT) — No Chemo
  • Biopsy: EBER ISH Confirms EBV (Type II/III)
  • Viral DNA: Plasma EBV DNA — Dx, Staging, Response, Surveillance
  • Induction: TPF / GemCis for High Volume/N3/T4 (NARCTEC)
  • District: Endemic SE Asia, N Africa
  • Adjuvant Chemo: Controversial (Intergroup 0099 YES, IMRT NO)
  • Metastatic: Gem/Cis 1L → PD-1 Inhibitors 2L
  • RT Technique: IMRT Parotid Sparing (<26Gy Mean)
  • Toxicities: Xerostomia, Trismus, Cranial Neuropathies, Hearing, Hypothyroidism
