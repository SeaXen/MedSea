SEA KNOWLEDGE STUDY NOTES
CHAPTER 8 ONCOLOGY / 8.6. GYNAECOLOGICAL CANCERS
Gestational Trophoblastic Neoplasia
1 Learning Objectives

By the end of this note you should be able to:

  • [ ] Classify GTN spectrum: Complete/Partial Mole, Invasive Mole, Choriocarcinoma, PSTT, ETT
  • [ ] Apply FIGO 2000 Staging (I-IV) + WHO Risk Score (0-25)
  • [ ] Interpret hCG kinetics for diagnosis, monitoring, remission definition
  • [ ] Prescribe Low-risk: Methotrexate (weekly/biweekly) or Actinomycin D (5-day/biweekly)
  • [ ] Prescribe High-risk: EMA-CO (Etoposide, Methotrexate, Actinomycin D, Cyclophosphamide, Oncovin) — Standard
  • [ ] Manage resistance: EMA-EP (Etoposide, Methotrexate, Actinomycin D, Etoposide, Cisplatin) / TEP
  • [ ] Define remission (hCG normal ×3 weekly) and surveillance (monthly ×1yr, then q3mo ×1yr)
  • [ ] Recognise PSTT/ETT: Low hCG, Surgery primary, Chemo limited role

4 Diagnosis & hCG Monitoring
Diagnostic Criteria for GTN (Post-Molar)

Any ONE of:

  1. hCG plateau (≤10% change ×4 weekly)
  2. hCG rise (≥10% ×3 weekly or ×2 weekly over 2 weeks)
  3. hCG persistence (>6 months post-evacuation)
  4. Histological diagnosis (Choriocarcinoma, PSTT, ETT)
  5. Metastases on imaging
hCG Monitoring Schedule
PhaseFrequency
------------------
**Post-evacuation**Weekly until normal ×3
**During Chemo****Weekly** (or biweekly for Actinomycin D 5-day)
**Post-chemo (Remission)****Weekly ×3** → **Monthly ×1 year** → **Quarterly ×1 year**
**Pregnancy after GTN****hCG at 6-8 weeks** (exclude recurrence)
5 Management
Low-Risk GTN (WHO Score ≤6)

``mermaid flowchart TD A[Low-Risk GTN (Score ≤6)] --> B{First-Line Agent} B -->|Methotrexate (MTX)| C[Weekly IM/IV MTX 30mg/m²<br/>OR Biweekly MTX 100mg/m² + Leucovorin 15mg q6h×4<br/>Response: hCG ↓10% weekly<br/>Cycles: Until hCG normal ×3 + 1-2 consolidation] B -->|Actinomycin D (Act-D)| D[5-day IV Act-D 1.25mg/m²/d q2wk<br/>OR Biweekly Act-D 0.5mg/m² q2wk<br/>Preferred if MTX contraindicated (renal, hepatic, pleural effusion)<br/>Faster response, less cycles] C --> E[Remission: hCG normal ×3 weekly] D --> E E --> F[Consolidation: 1-2 cycles post-normal hCG] F --> G[Surveillance: Monthly ×1yr, q3mo ×1yr] ``

High-Risk GTN (WHO Score ≥7)

``mermaid flowchart TD A[High-Risk GTN (Score ≥7)] --> B[EMA-CO (Standard Worldwide)] B --> B1[Week 1 (Day 1-2): Etoposide 100mg/m² + Actinomycin D 0.5mg/m²] B --> B2[Week 1 (Day 2): Methotrexate 300mg/m² IV + Leucovorin Rescue] B --> B3[Week 2 (Day 8): Cyclophosphamide 600mg/m² + Vincristine 1.4mg/m² (max 2mg)] B --> B4[Repeat q2wk (14-day cycles)] B1 --> C[Response: hCG ↓10% per cycle (Week 2 hCG)] C --> D[Cycles: Until hCG normal ×3 + 3 consolidation cycles] D --> E[Remission → Surveillance] ``

7 Viva Questions (MRCP PACES / FCPS)
QuestionExpected Answer
---------------------------
**25F, post-molar evacuation, hCG: 100,000 → 95,000 → 92,000 (weekly ×4). Diagnosis?****GTN (Post-molar)** — **hCG Plateau (≤10% change ×4 weekly)** = Diagnostic criteria met.
**Same patient, WHO Score: Age 25, Mole, Interval 2mo, hCG 100,000, Tumour 4cm, Lung mets (3). Risk score?****Age<40 (0) + Mole (0) + Interval<4 (0) + hCG 10⁴-10⁵ (2) + Tumour 3-5cm (1) + Lung mets (1) + Mets 1-4 (1) = 5 → LOW RISK (≤6)**.
**Low-risk GTN — first-line chemo options?****1) Weekly MTX 30mg/m² IM/IV** OR **2) Biweekly MTX 100mg/m² + Leucovorin** OR **3) Actinomycin D 5-day 1.25mg/m² q2wk** (preferred if MTX contraindicated).
**EMA-CO regimen — day-by-day?****Day 1-2: Etoposide 100mg/m² + Act-D 0.5mg/m²** → **Day 2: MTX 300mg/m² + Leucovorin Rescue** → **Day 8: Cyclophosphamide 600mg/m² + Vincristine 1.4mg/m²** → **Repeat q2wk**.
**High-risk GTN resistant to EMA-CO — next?****EMA-EP** (Etoposide, MTX, Act-D, Etoposide, Cisplatin) **OR TEP** (Paclitaxel, Etoposide, Cisplatin).
**PSTT — hCG level, management?****Low hCG (not reliable marker)**; **Primary: Surgery (Hysterectomy + PLND)**; **Chemo: EMA-CO/EMA-EP if metastatic**.
**Remission definition — hCG?****hCG Normal ×3 consecutive weekly measurements** → **Then 1-2 consolidation cycles** → **Surveillance**.
**Surveillance post-GTN remission?****Monthly ×1 year** → **Quarterly ×1 year**; **Contraception during + 1yr post**; **hCG at 6-8wk if pregnant**.
**Choriocarcinoma vs PSTT — hCG difference?****Choriocarcinoma: Very High hCG (>100,000)**; **PSTT: Low hCG** (intermediate trophoblast, minimal syncytiotrophoblast).
**Complete vs Partial Mole — genetics?****CHM: 46,XX (Androgenetic, paternal only)**; **PHM: 69,XXY (Diandric, 2 paternal + 1 maternal)**; **CHM 15-20%→GTN, PHM 1-5%→GTN**.
8 Confusions & Mnemonics
ConfusionClarification
--------------------------
**WHO Score vs FIGO Stage****FIGO = Anatomic (I-IV)**; **WHO = Prognostic (0-25)**; **BOTH used together** (e.g., Stage III, Score 8 = High-risk)
**Low-risk vs High-risk Chemo****Low: Single-agent (MTX/Act-D)**; **High: Multi-agent (EMA-CO)**; **Score ≤6 vs ≥7**
**MTX Weekly vs Biweekly****Weekly 30mg/m²** (traditional); **Biweekly 100mg/m² + Leucovorin** (less visits, similar efficacy)
**Actinomycin D 5-day vs Biweekly****5-day 1.25mg/m² q2wk** (standard); **Biweekly 0.5mg/m²** (less myelosuppression)
**EMA-CO vs EMA-EP****EMA-CO: Cyclo + Vincristine** (Standard High-risk); **EMA-EP: Cisplatin + Extra Etoposide** (Salvage for EMA-CO failure)
**PSTT vs ETT****PSTT: Intermediate trophoblast, chorionic gonadotrophin+, better**; **ETT: Epithelioid, EMA+, worse**
**hCG in PSTT****Low / Not proportional to tumour burden** — **Do not use for response monitoring**

Mnemonic: GTN-HCG

  • GTN Spectrum: Mole → Invasive → Chorio → PSTT/ETT
  • Trophoblastic: hCG = Marker (Except PSTT/ETT low)
  • New Mole: CHM 46,XX, PHM 69,XXY
  • HCG Kinetics: Plateau ×4wk, Rise ×3wk, Persist >6mo = GTN
  • Chemo Low-risk: MTX (Weekly/Biweekly) / Act-D (5-day)
  • Onco High-risk: EMA-CO (Etopo+ActD → MTX+Leuco → Cyclo+Vinci) q2wk
  • Risk Score: WHO ≤6 Low (Single), ≥7 High (EMA-CO)
  • NOTable: PSTT/ETT — Surgery Primary, Low hCG, Chemo Limited
  • EMA-CO Failure: EMA-EP / TEP
  • Cure Rate: >95% (Low ~100%, High ~85-90%)
  • Surveillance: Monthly ×1yr → q3mo ×1yr
  • Pregnancy After: Wait 1yr, hCG at 6-8wk
