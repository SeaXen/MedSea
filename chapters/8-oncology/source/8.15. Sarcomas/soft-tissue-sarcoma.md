# Soft Tissue Sarcoma (STS)

> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP Priority: HIGH**
> **STS = Rare (1% Adult Cancers)**; **>50 Histologies**; **Common: UPS (25%), Liposarcoma (20%), Leiomyosarcoma (15%), Synovial Sarcoma (10%), MPNST (5%)**; **FNCLCC Grading (1-3)**: Differentiation, Mitoses, Necrosis; **Surgery: R0 Resection (Compartmental, Margins)**; **Adjuvant RT: Pre-op (50Gy/25fx) vs Post-op (60-66Gy/30-33fx)** for High-Grade/Size>5cm/Deep; **Adjuvant Chemo: Doxorubicin+Ifosfamide Controversial** (EORTC 62931: RFS Benefit, No OS); **Metastatic 1L: Doxorubicin 75mg/m²**; **2L: Pazopanib (PALETTE), Trabectedin (Lipo/Leiomyo), Gem/Doc (Leiomyo), Ifosfamide (Synovial)**; **Histology-Directed Essential**.

---

## 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Classify **Common STS Histologies** (UPS, Liposarcoma, Leiomyosarcoma, Synovial, MPNST)
- [ ] Apply **FNCLCC Grading** (Differentiation, Mitoses, Necrosis → Grade 1-3)
- [ ] Plan **Surgical Resection** (R0, Compartmental, Margins)
- [ ] Indicate **Adjuvant RT** (Pre-op 50Gy vs Post-op 60-66Gy) for High-Grade/Deep/>5cm
- [ ] Evaluate **Adjuvant Chemo Controversy** (EORTC 62931: RFS Benefit, No OS)
- [ ] Sequence **Metastatic Therapy**: **Doxorubicin 1L → Pazopanib 2L → Histology-Directed**
- [ ] Select **Histology-Specific Agents**: Trabectedin (Lipo/Leiomyo), Gem/Doc (Leiomyo), Ifosfamide (Synovial)
- [ ] Recognise **MPNST in NF1** (Lifetime Risk 10%)

## 2. Definition & Epidemiology

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Definition** | Malignant mesenchymal tumours; **>50 Histological Subtypes** |
| **Incidence** | **~1% of Adult Cancers**; ~5,000/year UK; **More Common in Children/Young Adults** (but diverse) |
| **Prevalence** | 5-yr OS: **Localised ~65-70%**, **Metastatic ~15-20%** |
| **Peak Age** | **Bimodal**: Young Adults (Rhabdo, Synovial), Older Adults (UPS, Lipo, Leiomyo) |
| **Sex Ratio** | Slight Female Predominance Overall (Leiomyo, Lipo) |
| **Risk Factors** | **Prior RT (Radiation-Induced Sarcoma, Latency 10-20yr)**, **NF1 (MPNST 10% Lifetime)**, **Li-Fraumeni (TP53)**, **RB1 (Hereditary Retinoblastoma → Osteosarcoma/STS)**, **Chemical (Vinyl Chloride → Angiosarcoma)**, **Chronic Lymphoedema (Stewart-Treves → Angiosarcoma)** |

### FNCLCC Grading System

| Parameter | Score 1 | Score 2 | Score 3 |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------|
| **Differentiation** | **Well-Differentiated** (Lipoma-like, WDLS) | **Certain Type** (Leiomyo, Synovial, MLPS) | **Poor/Undifferentiated** (UPS, Rhabdo, Embryonal) |
| **Mitoses / 10 HPF** | **0-9** | **10-19** | **≥20** |
| **Necrosis** | **None** | **<50%** | **≥50%** |

| Total Score | Grade |
|-------------|-------|
| 2-3 | **Grade 1 (Low)** |
| 4-5 | **Grade 2 (Intermediate)** |
| 6-8 | **Grade 3 (High)** |

> **Critical:** Grade Determines Adjuvant RT/Chemo Decisions

## 4. Clinical Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Painless Mass** | **Most Common**; Deep, Slow-Growing |
| **Pain** | **Late** (Nerve Compression, Size) |
| **Size at Presentation** | **Often Large (>5-10cm)** — Deep Location Delays Detection |
| **Location** | **Extremities (60%)**, **Trunk/Retroperitoneum (30%)**, **Head/Neck (10%)** |
| **Retroperitoneal** | **Late Symptoms** (Mass Effect, Hydronephrosis, Vascular Compression) |

## 5. Diagnosis & Investigations

| Investigation | Role | Key Details |
|---------------|------|-------------|
| **MRI (Gold Standard)** | **Local Staging, Surgical Planning** | **T1: Hypointense**, **T2/STIR: Hyperintense**, **Contrast: Heterogeneous Enhancement**, **Perilesional Oedema**, **Neurovascular Relationship** |
| **CT Chest/Abd/Pelvis** | **Systemic Staging** (Lung Most Common Met Site) | Lung Mets > Liver/Bone |
| **Core Needle Biopsy (Image-Guided)** | **Diagnosis + Grade + Molecular** | **Adequate Tissue for IHC + FISH/NGS** |
| **IHC Panel** | **Lineage Assignment** | **MDM2/CDK4 (WDLS/DDLS)**, **STAT6 (SFT)**, **DOG1 (GIST)**, **S100/SOX10 (MPNST/Schwannoma)**, **TLE1 (Synovial)**, **Desmin/h-Caldesmon/SMA (Leiomyo)** |
| **Molecular/FISH** | **Definitive Subtyping** | **MDM2 Amp (12q)**, **FUS-DDIT3 (MLPS)**, **SS18-SSX (Synovial)**, **NF1 (MPNST)** |
| **PET-CT** | **Metabolic Activity, Occult Mets** | Not Routine |

## 6. Management

### Localised Disease

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Localised STS] --> B{Grade / Size / Depth}
    B -->|**Grade 1, Superficial, <5cm**| C[**Surgery Alone (R0 Wide Excision)**<br/>**Margins: 1-2cm or Fascial Boundary**<br/>**No RT, No Chemo**]
    B -->|**Grade 1, Deep OR >5cm**| D[**Surgery + Adjuvant RT**<br/>**Pre-op 50Gy/25fx (Preferred: Less Toxicity)**<br/>**OR Post-op 60-66Gy/30-33fx**]
    B -->|**Grade 2-3, Any Size/Depth**| E[**Surgery + Adjuvant RT**<br/>**Pre-op 50Gy/25fx (Standard)**<br/>**Function Preservation (Limb Salvage)**]
    B -->|**High-Grade (2-3), >5cm, Deep**| F[**Consider Adjuvant Chemo**<br/>**Doxorubicin + Ifosfamide** (EORTC 62931)<br/>**RFS Benefit, No OS Benefit**<br/>**Selected Patients (Young, High Risk)**]
    C --> G[**Surveillance: MRI q3-6mo×2yr, q6mo×3yr; CT Chest q6mo×5yr**]
    D --> G
    E --> G
    F --> G
```

### Radiation Therapy

| Setting | Technique | Dose |
|---------|-----------|------|
| **Pre-operative** | **IMRT/VMAT** | **50Gy/25fx** (RTOG 0630: Less Wound Complications, Better Function) |
| **Post-operative** | **IMRT/VMAT** | **60-66Gy/30-33fx** (High-Grade, Positive/Close Margins) |
| **Brachytherapy** | **Intraoperative/Post-op** | **Alternative for Selected** |

> **Pre-op RT Preferred for Extremity STS** (Less Fibrosis, Better Function, Same Local Control)

## 7. Systemic Therapy

### Adjuvant Chemotherapy (Controversial)

| Trial | Regimen | Population | Outcome |
|-------|---------|------------|---------|
| **EORTC 62931** | **Doxorubicin 50 + Ifosfamide 5g/m² (with Mesna) q3w ×3-5 cycles** | **High-Grade, >5cm, Deep** | **RFS HR 0.72 (p=0.007); OS NS (HR 0.90)** |
| **Meta-Analyses** | Doxorubicin-based | Mixed | **RFS Benefit ~10-15%; No Consistent OS Benefit** |
| **Current Practice** | **Selected Young, High-Risk, Grade 3** | Individualised | **Not Routine Standard** |

### Metastatic / Unresectable

#### First-Line

| Agent | Dose/Schedule | Key Trial |
|-------|---------------|-----------|
| **Doxorubicin** | **75mg/m² q3w** (Bolus) | **Standard 1L**; **Response Rate 20-25%** |
| **Doxorubicin + Ifosfamide** | **Dox 50 + Ifos 5-10g/m² (Mesna) q3w** | **Higher RR (30-40%), More Toxic**; **No OS Benefit** |
| **Gemcitabine + Docetaxel** | **Gem 900 d1,8 + Doc 100 d8 q3w** | **Leiomyosarcoma Preferred** (GeDDiS) |

#### Second-Line & Beyond

| Agent | Indication | Key Trial |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| **Pazopanib** | **2L+ (Non-Adipocytic)** | **PALETTE: PFS 4.6 vs 1.6mo** |
| **Trabectedin** | **Liposarcoma / Leiomyosarcoma (2L+)** | **Cisplatin-Related DNA Damage**; **Lipo/Leiomyo Benefit** |
| **Eribulin** | **Liposarcoma (2L+)** | **PFS Benefit** |
| **Ifosfamide** | **Synovial Sarcoma / High-Grade** | **Single-Agent Activity** |
| **Olaratumab + Doxorubicin** | **Failed (ANNOUNCE: No OS Benefit)** | **Withdrawn** |
| **Immunotherapy (Pembrolizumab)** | **Selected (UPS, High TMB, MSI-H)** | **SARC028: Modest Activity** |

## 8. Histology-Specific Management

| Histology | Key Features | Preferred Systemic |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| **UPS** | **No Lineage**, Complex Genetics | **Doxorubicin**, **Pazopanib**, **Immunotherapy (High TMB)** |
| **WDLS/DDLS** | **MDM2/CDK4 Amp (12q13-15)**, **12q Ring Chromosomes** | **CDK4/6 Inhibitors (Palbociclib/Ribociclib — Trials)**, **Trabectedin**, **Pazopanib** |
| **MLPS** | **FUS-DDIT3 / EWSR1-DDIT3**, **Round Cell = High Grade** | **Trabectedin (High Activity)**, **Eribulin**, **Doxorubicin** |
| **PLS** | **MYC Amplification**, **Aggressive** | **Doxorubicin**, **Clinical Trials** |
| **Leiomyosarcoma** | **Smooth Muscle Markers**, **Complex Karyotype** | **Gemcitabine + Docetaxel (GeDDiS)**, **Trabectedin**, **Doxorubicin** |
| **Synovial Sarcoma** | **SS18-SSX Fusion (t(X;18))**, **TLE1+** | **Ifosfamide (High Activity)**, **Doxorubicin**, **TEC (Trials)** |
| **MPNST** | **NF1 Loss (50%), SUZ12/EED Loss**, **H3K27me3 Loss** | **Doxorubicin**, **MEK Inhibitors (Trametinib — Trials)**, **Immunotherapy** |
| **Angiosarcoma** | **Vascular Markers (CD31, ERG, FLI1)**, **MYC Amp (Radiation-Associated)** | **Paclitaxel (Weekly)**, **Doxorubicin**, **Immunotherapy (High TMB)** |

## 11. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Pre-op vs Post-op RT** | **Pre-op: 50Gy/25fx (Less Toxicity, Better Function)**; **Post-op: 60-66Gy/30-33fx (If Pre-op Not Done, Close/Positive Margins)** |
| **Adjuvant Chemo — Yes or No?** | **EORTC 62931: RFS Benefit, No OS** — **Not Routine**, **Selected Young/High-Risk** |
| **Leiomyosarcoma Chemo** | **Gem/Doc Superior to Doxorubicin** (GeDDiS: PFS 6.2 vs 3.8mo) — **Preferred 1L** |
| **Synovial Sarcoma — Ifosfamide** | **High Single-Agent Activity** (Response Rates 30-50%) — **Preferred 2L/Neoadjuvant** |
| **WDLS vs DDLS vs MLPS vs PLS** | **WDLS: Benign-Intermediate, MDM2+**; **DDLS: Dedifferentiated, MDM2+**; **MLPS: FUS-DDIT3, Round Cell**; **PLS: MYC Amp, Aggressive** |
| **MPNST Diagnosis** | **NF1 Context**, **H3K27me3 Loss (IHC)** = **Specific for MPNST** (vs Schwannoma/Other) |
| **Trabectedin Indication** | **Liposarcoma (MLPS) & Leiomyosarcoma** — **DNA Minor Groove Binder** |

**Mnemonic: STS-MANAGEMENT**
- **S**TS: **>50 Histologies**, **UPS 25%, Lipo 20%, Leiomyo 15%, Synovial 10%, MPNST 5%**
- **T**NM Not Used — **FNCLCC Grade** (Diff + Mitoses + Necrosis) **Gr1/2/3**
- **S**urgery: **R0 Wide Excision**, Compartmental, Limb Salvage
- **M**argins: **1-2cm or Fascial Boundary**
- **A**djuvant RT: **Gr2-3 / Deep / >5cm** → **Pre-op 50Gy/25fx (Preferred)**
- **N**eo/Adjuvant Chemo: **EORTC 62931: Dox+Ifo → RFS Benefit, NO OS** — **Selective**
- **A**gent 1L Met: **Doxorubicin 75mg/m²**
- **G**em/Doc: **Leiomyosarcoma Preferred**
- **E**ribulin/Trabectedin: **Liposarcoma (MLPS)** / **Leiomyo**
- **M**PNST: **NF1 (10% Lifetime)**, **H3K27me3 Loss**
- **E**nvironment: **Prior RT (10-20yr Latency)**, **Chemicals (Vinyl Chloride→Angio)**
- **N**ext Line: **Pazopanib (PALETTE)**, **Ifosfamide (Synovial)**, **Clinical Trials**
- **T**rabectedin: **Lipo (MLPS) & Leiomyo**
