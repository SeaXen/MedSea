SEA KNOWLEDGE STUDY NOTES
CHAPTER 8 ONCOLOGY / 8.15. SARCOMAS
Soft Tissue Sarcoma
1 Learning Objectives

By the end of this note you should be able to:

  • [ ] Classify Common STS Histologies (UPS, Liposarcoma, Leiomyosarcoma, Synovial, MPNST)
  • [ ] Apply FNCLCC Grading (Differentiation, Mitoses, Necrosis → Grade 1-3)
  • [ ] Plan Surgical Resection (R0, Compartmental, Margins)
  • [ ] Indicate Adjuvant RT (Pre-op 50Gy vs Post-op 60-66Gy) for High-Grade/Deep/>5cm
  • [ ] Evaluate Adjuvant Chemo Controversy (EORTC 62931: RFS Benefit, No OS)
  • [ ] Sequence Metastatic Therapy: Doxorubicin 1L → Pazopanib 2L → Histology-Directed
  • [ ] Select Histology-Specific Agents: Trabectedin (Lipo/Leiomyo), Gem/Doc (Leiomyo), Ifosfamide (Synovial)
  • [ ] Recognise MPNST in NF1 (Lifetime Risk 10%)
2 Definition & Epidemiology
FeatureDetail
-----------------
**Definition**Malignant mesenchymal tumours; **>50 Histological Subtypes**
**Incidence****~1% of Adult Cancers**; ~5,000/year UK; **More Common in Children/Young Adults** (but diverse)
**Prevalence**5-yr OS: **Localised ~65-70%**, **Metastatic ~15-20%**
**Peak Age****Bimodal**: Young Adults (Rhabdo, Synovial), Older Adults (UPS, Lipo, Leiomyo)
**Sex Ratio**Slight Female Predominance Overall (Leiomyo, Lipo)
**Risk Factors****Prior RT (Radiation-Induced Sarcoma, Latency 10-20yr)**, **NF1 (MPNST 10% Lifetime)**, **Li-Fraumeni (TP53)**, **RB1 (Hereditary Retinoblastoma → Osteosarcoma/STS)**, **Chemical (Vinyl Chloride → Angiosarcoma)**, **Chronic Lymphoedema (Stewart-Treves → Angiosarcoma)**

4 Clinical Features
FeatureDescription
----------------------
**Painless Mass****Most Common**; Deep, Slow-Growing
**Pain****Late** (Nerve Compression, Size)
**Size at Presentation****Often Large (>5-10cm)** — Deep Location Delays Detection
**Location****Extremities (60%)**, **Trunk/Retroperitoneum (30%)**, **Head/Neck (10%)**
**Retroperitoneal****Late Symptoms** (Mass Effect, Hydronephrosis, Vascular Compression)
5 Diagnosis & Investigations
InvestigationRoleKey Details
----------------------------------
**MRI (Gold Standard)****Local Staging, Surgical Planning****T1: Hypointense**, **T2/STIR: Hyperintense**, **Contrast: Heterogeneous Enhancement**, **Perilesional Oedema**, **Neurovascular Relationship**
**CT Chest/Abd/Pelvis****Systemic Staging** (Lung Most Common Met Site)Lung Mets > Liver/Bone
**Core Needle Biopsy (Image-Guided)****Diagnosis + Grade + Molecular****Adequate Tissue for IHC + FISH/NGS**
**IHC Panel****Lineage Assignment****MDM2/CDK4 (WDLS/DDLS)**, **STAT6 (SFT)**, **DOG1 (GIST)**, **S100/SOX10 (MPNST/Schwannoma)**, **TLE1 (Synovial)**, **Desmin/h-Caldesmon/SMA (Leiomyo)**
**Molecular/FISH****Definitive Subtyping****MDM2 Amp (12q)**, **FUS-DDIT3 (MLPS)**, **SS18-SSX (Synovial)**, **NF1 (MPNST)**
**PET-CT****Metabolic Activity, Occult Mets**Not Routine

11 Confusions & Mnemonics
ConfusionClarification
--------------------------
**Pre-op vs Post-op RT****Pre-op: 50Gy/25fx (Less Toxicity, Better Function)**; **Post-op: 60-66Gy/30-33fx (If Pre-op Not Done, Close/Positive Margins)**
**Adjuvant Chemo — Yes or No?****EORTC 62931: RFS Benefit, No OS** — **Not Routine**, **Selected Young/High-Risk**
**Leiomyosarcoma Chemo****Gem/Doc Superior to Doxorubicin** (GeDDiS: PFS 6.2 vs 3.8mo) — **Preferred 1L**
**Synovial Sarcoma — Ifosfamide****High Single-Agent Activity** (Response Rates 30-50%) — **Preferred 2L/Neoadjuvant**
**WDLS vs DDLS vs MLPS vs PLS****WDLS: Benign-Intermediate, MDM2+**; **DDLS: Dedifferentiated, MDM2+**; **MLPS: FUS-DDIT3, Round Cell**; **PLS: MYC Amp, Aggressive**
**MPNST Diagnosis****NF1 Context**, **H3K27me3 Loss (IHC)** = **Specific for MPNST** (vs Schwannoma/Other)
**Trabectedin Indication****Liposarcoma (MLPS) & Leiomyosarcoma** — **DNA Minor Groove Binder**

Mnemonic: STS-MANAGEMENT

  • STS: >50 Histologies, UPS 25%, Lipo 20%, Leiomyo 15%, Synovial 10%, MPNST 5%
  • TNM Not Used — FNCLCC Grade (Diff + Mitoses + Necrosis) Gr1/2/3
  • Surgery: R0 Wide Excision, Compartmental, Limb Salvage
  • Margins: 1-2cm or Fascial Boundary
  • Adjuvant RT: Gr2-3 / Deep / >5cm → Pre-op 50Gy/25fx (Preferred)
  • Neo/Adjuvant Chemo: EORTC 62931: Dox+Ifo → RFS Benefit, NO OS — Selective
  • Agent 1L Met: Doxorubicin 75mg/m²
  • Gem/Doc: Leiomyosarcoma Preferred
  • Eribulin/Trabectedin: Liposarcoma (MLPS) / Leiomyo
  • MPNST: NF1 (10% Lifetime), H3K27me3 Loss
  • Environment: Prior RT (10-20yr Latency), Chemicals (Vinyl Chloride→Angio)
  • Next Line: Pazopanib (PALETTE), Ifosfamide (Synovial), Clinical Trials
  • Trabectedin: Lipo (MLPS) & Leiomyo
  • Synovial: SS18-SSX Fusion, Ifosfamide Active
