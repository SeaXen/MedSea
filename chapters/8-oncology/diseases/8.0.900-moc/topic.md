## Key Themes for FCPS/MRCP

- **TNM 8th Edition staging** for all solid tumours
- **WHO 2022 / ICC 2022** classification for haematological malignancies
- **ELN 2022 risk stratification** for AML
- **Ann Arbor staging** for lymphomas; **R-IPI** for DLBCL
- **ISS / R-ISS** for multiple myeloma
- **FIGO staging** for gynaecological cancers
- **Breslow / Clark** for melanoma
- **Gleason / ISUP** for prostate cancer
- **Child-Pugh / BCLC** for HCC
- **TNM + MSKCC** for RCC

## Molecular / Biomarker Emphasis (high-yield)

- **EGFR/ALK/ROS1/BRAF/KRAS** — NSCLC
- **HER2/ER/PR** — breast
- **KRAS/NRAS/BRAF/MSS/MSI** — colorectal
- **BRAF/MEK, KIT, PD-L1** — melanoma
- **BRAF V600E** — hairy cell, melanoma, thyroid, colorectal
- **FLT3-ITD, NPM1, CEBPA, IDH1/2, TP53** — AML
- **BCR-ABL1, JAK2 V617F, CALR, MPL** — MPN/CML
- **JAK2 V617F** — PV/ET/PMF (95% PV)
- **MGMT methylation** — glioblastoma
- **1p/19q codeletion** — oligodendroglioma
- **IDH1/2 mutation** — gliomas (better prognosis)

## Targeted Therapy & Immunotherapy

- **TKIs**: imatinib, dasatinib, nilotinib (CML); erlotinib, gefitinib, osimertinib (EGFR NSCLC); crizotinib, alectinib (ALK)
- **Anti-VEGF**: bevacizumab (CRC, NSCLC, GBM, RCC, ovarian)
- **Anti-HER2**: trastuzumab, pertuzumab, T-DM1, trastuzumab deruxtecan (breast, gastric)
- **Anti-CD20**: rituximab, obinutuzumab (B-cell lymphomas, CLL)
- **Anti-PD-1/PD-L1**: pembrolizumab, nivolumab (melanoma, NSCLC, RCC, HL, dMMR)
- **Anti-BCMA**: belantamab, idecabtagene (myeloma)
- **PARP inhibitors**: olaparib, niraparib (BRCA, ovarian, breast, prostate)
- **CDK4/6 inhibitors**: palbociclib, ribociclib (HR+ breast)
