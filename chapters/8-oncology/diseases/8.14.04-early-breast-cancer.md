> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP Priority: HIGH**
> **Early Breast Cancer = Stages I-IIIA**; **Surgery**: Breast-Conserving Surgery (BCS) + SLNB vs Mastectomy + SLNB/ALND; **Adjuvant Endocrine**: Premeno → Tamoxifen ± OFS (SOFT/TEXT), Postmeno → AI (ATAC, BIG 1-98, MA.17); **Adjuvant Chemo**: Oncotype DX RS 0-25 (TAILORx), RS 26-100 age ≤50 (RxPONDER), High Clinical Risk (MINDACT); **HER2+**: Trastuzumab 1yr ± Pertuzumab (APHINITY), Adjuvant T-DM1 if residual (KATHERINE); **Triple Negative**: Neoadjuvant Chemo ± Pembrolizumab (KEYNOTE-522), Adjuvant Capecitabine ± PARP Inhibitor (BRCA+ OlympiA); **Radiotherapy**: Post-BCS, Post-Mastectomy (T3/T4/N+), Regional Nodal (AMAROS, EORTC 22922); **Bisphosphonates**: Adjuvant Zoledronic Acid (Postmeno, EBCTCG); **Genetic**: BRCA1/2, PALB2 testing if <50/TNBC/FH.

## 2. 2. Surgical Management

### 1. Breast-Conserving Surgery (BCS) vs Mastectomy
| Criteria | BCS + Radiotherapy | Mastectomy |
|----------|-------------------|------------|
| **Indication** | Tumour ≤5cm, Unifocal, Favourable tumour:breast ratio, Negative margins achievable | Multifocal, Large >5cm, Inflammatory, Prior chest RT, Patient preference, Genetic mutation (BRCA) |
| **Oncological Equivalence** | **Equivalent OS/DFS** (NSABP B-06, Milan, EORTC 10801) | **Equivalent OS/DFS** |
| **Margins** | **No ink on tumour** (SSO/ASTRO consensus) | N/A |
| **Radiation Required** | **Yes** (Whole breast 40-50Gy ± boost) | **Sometimes** (See below) |

### 2. Axillary Surgery
| Procedure | Indication |
|-----------|------------|
| **SLNB** | **cN0** (Early stage, No clinical nodal involvement) |
| **ALND** | **SLNB+ (≥2 macromets)**, **cN1**, **Inflammatory**, **Neoadjuvant with residual nodes** |
| **Omission of ALND** | **Z0011 criteria**: T1-2, ≤2 SLN+, BCS + Whole Breast RT, No neoadjuvant | **IBCSG 23-01**: Micromets only (≤2mm) |
| **Targeted Axillary Dissection (TAD)** | **Post-neoadjuvant cN1 → ypN0** (Mark clip + SLNB) |

## 3. 3. Adjuvant Endocrine Therapy

### 1. Premenopausal
| Scenario | Regimen | Key Trial |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Standard** | **Tamoxifen 20mg OD ×5-10yr** | **ATLAS, aTTom** (10yr >5yr) |
| **High Risk** (Node+, High Grade, <35yr) | **Tamoxifen + OFS** (Goserelin/Leuprorelin) | **SOFT/TEXT**: **Exemestane + OFS > Tamoxifen + OFS > Tamoxifen alone** |
| **OFS Duration** | **5 years** (with Tamoxifen/Exemestane) | **SOFT/TEXT** |

### 2. Postmenopausal
| Scenario | Regimen | Key Trial |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Standard** | **AI (Anastrazole/Letrozole/Exemestane) ×5-10yr** | **ATAC, BIG 1-98, MA.17R** |
| **Sequential** | **Tamoxifen 2-3yr → AI 2-3yr (Total 5-10yr)** | **BIG 1-98, ITA, TEAM** |
| **Extended** | **Letrozole 5yr post 5yr Tamoxifen** | **MA.17R** (10yr total better) |
| **AI Intolerant** | **Tamoxifen 5-10yr** | — |

### 3. Duration: 5 vs 10 Years
| Population | Recommendation |
|------------|----------------|
| **Tamoxifen** | **10 years** (ATLAS, aTTom: DFS/OS benefit) |
| **AI** | **Consider 10yr** (MA.17R: Extended Letrozole benefit) |

## 4. 4. Adjuvant Chemotherapy - Genomic Assay Guided

### 1. Oncotype DX Recurrence Score (RS) - ER+/HER2-, Node-Neg
| RS | TAILORx/NSABP B-20 | RxPONDER (1-3 Nodes) |
|----|-------------------|----------------------|
| **0-10** | **Endocrine alone** (No chemo benefit) | **Endocrine alone** |
| **11-25** | **Endocrine alone** (≤50yr: Consider chemo) | **Endocrine alone** (Postmeno: No chemo; Premeno: Chemo benefit) |
| **26-100** | **Chemo + Endocrine** | **Chemo + Endocrine** |

### 2. MammaPrint (70-gene) - MINDACT Trial
| Clinical Risk | Genomic Risk | Chemo Benefit |
|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| **High Clinical, Low Genomic** | **No** (Distal RF 94.7% vs 96.2%, NS) |

### 3. Indications for Adjuvant Chemo (ESMO/NCCN)
- **High Clinical Risk**: Grade 3, >2cm, Node+, LVI, Young age, TNBC, HER2+
- **Oncotype RS 26-100**
- **MammaPrint High Genomic Risk**
- **Triple Negative** (Any stage >T1a)
- **HER2+** (With Trastuzumab)

### 4. Regimens
| Regimen | Schedule | Indication |
|---------|----------|------------|
| **AC → T** (Dose-Dense) | **AC q2w ×4 → Paclitaxel q2w ×4 / Docetaxel q3w ×4** | **Standard High-Risk** (CALGB 9741) |
| **TC** | **Docetaxel 75 + Cyclophosphamide 600 q3w ×4** | **Lower Risk / Older / Avoid Anthracycline** (US Oncology 9735) |
| **FEC → T** | **FEC 100 q3w ×3-4 → Docetaxel q3w ×3-4** | **European Standard** |
| **Carboplatin + Paclitaxel** | **Weekly ×12 / q3w ×4** | **TNBC (Neoadjuvant)** |
| **TCHP** | **Docetaxel + Carboplatin + Trastuzumab + Pertuzumab** | **HER2+ Neoadjuvant** |

## 5. 5. HER2+ Adjuvant Therapy

### 1. Standard
| Regimen | Duration | Key Trial |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Trastuzumab 1yr + Chemo** | **1 year** (Concurrent/Sequential) | **HERA, BCIRG 006, NSABP B-31, NCCTG N9831** |
| **Dual Blockade: Trastuzumab + Pertuzumab + Chemo** | **1 year** | **APHINITY**: Benefit in Node+ (DFS HR 0.77) |

### 2. Post-Neoadjuvant
| Scenario | Regimen | Trial |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **pCR (ypT0/is ypN0)** | **Continue Trastuzumab (±Pertuzumab) to complete 1yr** | **KRISTINE, APHINITY** |
| **Residual Disease (ypT/ypN+)** | **T-DM1 (Kadcyla) 14 cycles** | **KATHERINE**: **DFS HR 0.50** |
| **Residual + Pertuzumab naive** | **Trastuzumab + Pertuzumab** | **APHINITY** |

### 3. Duration: 6 months vs 1 year
| Trial | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **PERSEPHONE, HORG, Short-HER** | **6 months non-inferior in selected (Stage I, ER+)**; **1yr standard for Node+** |

|---------|-----------|----------|

| **Dose-Dense AC → Paclitaxel + Carboplatin** | **GeparSixto** | **pCR 53-58%** (Carbo) |

### 2. Adjuvant
| Setting | Regimen | Trial |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Post-Neoadjuvant Residual** | **Capecitabine 6-8 cycles** | **CREATE-X**: **DFS HR 0.70** |
| **BRCA1/2 Mutation** | **Olaparib 1yr** | **OlympiA**: **DFS HR 0.58** |
| **No Neoadjuvant / High Risk** | **Dose-Dense AC → Taxane** | **Standard** |

## 12. 12. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **BCS vs Mastectomy — Margins** | **BCS: No ink on tumour**; **Mastectomy: No margin assessment needed** (but margin status still prognostic) |
| **Oncotype RS 25 vs 26** | **RS 0-25: No chemo (≤50yr consider); RS 26-100: Chemo** — **Cutoff at 26** |
| **RxPONDER Pre vs Postmeno** | **Premeno: Chemo benefit**; **Postmeno: No chemo benefit** (RS 0-25) |
| **Z0011 vs IBCSG 23-01** | **Z0011: ≤2 macromets, BCS+RT**; **IBCSG 23-01: Micromets (≤2mm) only, BCS+RT** |
| **T-DM1 vs Trastuzumab** | **T-DM1 = Trastuzumab + DM1 (payload)**; **KATHERINE: Residual disease → T-DM1 superior** |
| **Adjuvant Bisphosphonate — Premeno?** | **Not routine** (Only with OFS, ABCSG-12); **Postmeno: Clear benefit** (EBCTCG) |
| **Extended AI vs Extended Tamoxifen** | **Extended AI (MA.17R) > Extended Tamoxifen** for postmeno |

**Mnemonic: BREAST-CANCER-EARLY**
- **B**reast Surgery: **BCS = Mastectomy**; **SLNB cN0**, **Z0011 ≤2 SLN+**
- **R**adiotherapy: **Post-BCS always**, **Post-Mastectomy T3/T4/N+**, **Chest Wall + Nodes if N2-3/N1+RF**
- **E**ndocrine Premeno: **Tamoxifen 5-10yr ± OFS (SOFT/TEXT)**
- **A**djuvant Endocrine Postmeno: **AI 5-10yr** (Sequential/Extended)
- **S**ystemic Chemo: **Oncotype RS 0-25 no chemo, 26-100 chemo**; **RxPONDER premeno chemo**
- **T**NBC: **Carbo/Taxane + Pembro neoadj**, **Capecitabine adjuvant (CREATE-X)**
- **C**hemo Decision: **Oncotype RS 0-25 no chemo**, **RS 26-100 chemo**
- **A**nthracycline/Taxane: **Dose-Dense AC→T (CALGB 9741)** or **TC (US Oncology)**
- **N**eoadjuvant HER2+: **TCHP or THP → Surgery → T-DM1 if residual (KATHERINE)**
- **C**hemo HER2+: **Trastuzumab 1yr + Chemo**; **APHINITY: Pertuzumab if Node+**
- **A**djuvant Bisphosphonate: **Zoledronic Acid Postmeno q6mo ×3-5yr** (EBCTCG)
- **N**on- Ink Margin: **No ink on tumour** (BCS)
- **E**arly Genetic: **<50, TNBC<60, Male, FH, Ashkenazi** → BRCA1/2, PALB2
- **R**ecurrence Score: **Oncotype DX RS 0-100**
