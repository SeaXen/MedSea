> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP Priority: HIGH**
> **Meningioma = Most Common Primary CNS Tumour (30-40%)**; **Benign (WHO Grade 1) 80-85%**, **Atypical (Grade 2) 15-18%**, **Anaplastic (Grade 3) 1-3%**; **NF2 Mutation (50%, Merin)**, **TRAF7, KLF4, AKT1, SMO, PIK3CA** (Non-NF2); **Surgery: Maximal Safe Resection (Simpson Grade I)**; **RT: Adjuvant for Grade 2/3, Subtotal Resection, Recurrent**; **SRS for Small/Residual**; **Grade 3: RT Standard**; **Systemic: Limited** (Hydroxyurea, Bevacizumab, mTOR Inhibitors, Somatostatin Analogues); **Surveillance: MRI q6-12mo ×5yr**.

## 1. 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Apply **WHO CNS5 Grading** (Grade 1, 2, 3) based on Mitoses, Brain Invasion, Criteria
- [ ] Perform **Simpson Resection Grading** and Target **Grade I (Complete + Dura/Bone)**
- [ ] Indicate **Adjuvant RT** for Grade 2/3, Subtotal Resection, Recurrence
- [ ] Choose **SRS vs FSRT** for Residual/Recurrent Small Lesions
- [ ] Recognise **Molecular Subgroups**: NF2 (Merlin), TRAF7, KLF4, AKT1, SMO, PIK3CA
- [ ] Manage **Skull Base / Optic Nerve Sheath** Meningiomas (Multidisciplinary)
- [ ] Counsel on **Surveillance** and **Recurrence Risk** by Grade/Simpson/Extent

## 2. 2. Definition & Epidemiology

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Definition** | Tumour of **Arachnoid Cap Cells** (Meninges); **Extra-axial**, **Dural-Based** |
| **Incidence** | **Most Common Primary CNS Tumour (30-40%)**: ~8-10/100,000/year |
| **Prevalence** | **Female Predominance (2:1)**; Hormone Receptor Positive (PR+) |
| **Peak Age** | **50-70 years** |
| **Risk Factors** | **Ionising Radiation** (Prior RT, Tinea Capitis), **Neurofibromatosis Type 2 (NF2)**, **Hormonal** (Oral Contraceptives, HRT, Pregnancy Growth), **Obesity** |

---

## 3. 3. WHO CNS5 Classification (Grades 1-3)

| Grade | Criteria (Any One Sufficient) | Frequency | 10-yr PFS |
|-------|-------------------------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Grade 1 (Benign)** | **<4 Mitoses/10 HPF**, **No Brain Invasion**, **None of Grade 2/3 Criteria** | **80-85%** | **>90%** |
| **Grade 2 (Atypical)** | **4-19 Mitoses/10 HPF** **OR** **Brain Invasion** **OR** **≥3 of: High Cellularity, Small Cells, Prominent Nucleoli, Sheeting, Necrosis** | **15-18%** | **~60-70%** |
| **Grade 3 (Anaplastic/Malignant)** | **≥20 Mitoses/10 HPF** **OR** **Frank Anaplasia (Carcinoma/Sarcoma/Melanoma-like)** | **1-3%** | **~30-40%** |

> **Note:** Brain Invasion = **Irregular Infiltration of Brain Parenchyma** (Not Just Abutment)

## 5. 5. Clinical Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Asymptomatic** | **Incidental (20-30%)** on Imaging |
| **Headache** | Non-Specific, Progressive |
| **Seizures** | **20-30%** (Convexity Lesions) |
| **Focal Deficits** | Motor, Sensory, Cranial Nerve (Depends on Location) |
| **Skull Base Symptoms** | Cranial Neuropathies (II, III, IV, V, VI, VII), Hearing Loss, Diplopia |
| **Spinal Meningioma** | Myelopathy, Radiculopathy, Sphincter Dysfunction |
| **Hormonal Sensitivity** | **Progesterone Receptor (PR+)**, Growth in Pregnancy |

## 6. 6. Diagnosis & Investigations

| Investigation | Role | Key Details |
|---------------|------|-------------|
| **MRI Brain (Gold Standard)** | **Diagnosis, Extent, Surgical Planning** | **T1 Post-Contrast: Homogeneous Enhancement**, **Dural Tail Sign**, **Broad Dural Base**, **Hyperostosis (Bone Invasion)**, **Perilesional Oedema (Vasogenic)** |
| **CT Brain** | Bone Involvement, Hyperostosis, Calcification | **Calcification (Psammoma Bodies)**, **Hyperostosis** |
| **Angiography / MR Angio** | Vascular Supply (Surgical Planning) | **External Carotid Supply** (MMA, Accessory Meningeal) |
| **PET-CT (DOTATATE / FDG)** | Recurrence vs Radionecrosis, Grade Assessment | **DOTATATE: SSTR2A+ (High Uptake)** |
| **Biopsy** | Rarely Needed (Imaging Diagnostic) | If Atypical Imaging / Non-Operative |

## 7. 7. Simpson Resection Grades (Extent of Resection)

| Grade | Description | 10-yr Recurrence |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| **Grade I** | **Complete Removal** of Tumour, **Dura**, **Abnormal Bone** | **9%** |
| **Grade II** | Complete Tumour Removal + **Coagulation of Dural Attachment** | **19%** |
| **Grade III** | Complete Tumour Removal **WITHOUT** Dural Coagulation/Resection | **29%** |
| **Grade IV** | **Subtotal Resection** | **40%** |
| **Grade V** | **Biopsy Only** | **~100%** |

> **Goal: Simpson Grade I (or II) for Grade 1; Grade I Mandatory for Grade 2/3**

```mermaid
**OR FSRT (50-54Gy/25-30fx)**]
    F --> H[**Long-Term Surveillance**]
    G --> H
```

### 2. Grade 2 (Atypical)

| Scenario | Management |
|----------|------------|
| **GTR (Simpson I/II)** | **Adjuvant RT (54-60Gy/30fx)** — **Standard** (Reduces Recurrence) |
| **STR (Simpson III/IV)** | **Adjuvant RT (54-60Gy)** — **Mandatory** |
| **Recurrent** | **Re-resection if Feasible** → **Re-RT (SRS/FSRT)** |

### 3. Grade 3 (Anaplastic)

| Scenario | Management |
|----------|------------|
| **Any Resection** | **Adjuvant RT (60Gy/30fx)** — **Mandatory** |
| **Recurrent** | **Re-resection + Re-RT (SRS/FSRT/Re-RT)** → **Systemic Trials** |

### 4. Radiation Techniques

| Technique | Indication | Dose |
|-----------|------------|------|
| **SRS (Single Fraction)** | **Small Residual/Recurrent (<3cm)**, Grade 1/2 | **12-16Gy** (Marginal) |
| **FSRT (Hypofractionated)** | **Larger/Adjacent to Optic Apparatus**, Grade 2 | **25-30Gy/5fx** OR **50-54Gy/25-30fx** |
| **Conventional FSRT** | **Grade 2/3 Adjuvant**, Large Volume | **54-60Gy/30fx** |

## 9. 9. Special Locations

| Location | Challenges | Management |
|----------|------------|------------|
| **Parasagittal/Falx** | **Superior Sagittal Sinus Invasion** | **Sinus Reconstruction/Repair if Invaded**; **Venous Outflow Preservation** |
| **Sphenoid Wing** | **En Plaque**, Optic Nerve, Cavernous Sinus | **Orbital Decompression**, **Cavernous Sinus Dissection** |
| **Olfactory Groove** | **Frontal Lobe, Olfactory Nerve**, CSF Leak Risk | **Bifrontal Craniotomy**, **CSF Leak Prevention** |
| **Tuberculum Sellae / Suprasellar** | **Optic Chiasm, Pituitary**, Visual Loss | **Endoscopic Endonasal vs Transcranial** |
| **Cavernous Sinus** | **Cranial Nerves III-VI, ICA** | **Radical Resection Often Impossible** → **RT Primary/SRS** |
| **Optic Nerve Sheath** | **Vision Loss**, Encasement | **SRS/FSRT Preferred** (Surgery High Morbidity) |
| **Posterior Fossa / Petroclival** | **Brainstem, Cranial Nerves, Venous Sinuses** | **Multidisciplinary**, **SRS/FSRT for Residual** |
| **Spinal** | **Thoracic (80%)**, Myelopathy | **Laminectomy + Resection**, **RT for Grade 2/3/STR** |

## 10. 10. Systemic Therapy (Limited Evidence)

| Agent | Indication | Evidence |
|-------|------------|----------|
| **Hydroxyurea** | Recurrent/Unresectable, Grade 2/3 | Phase 2: Modest Activity |
| **Bevacizumab** | Recurrent, Progressive | Phase 2: PFS Benefit (Grade 2/3) |
| **Somatostatin Analogues (Octreotide)** | SSTR2A+ (DOTATATE+) | Limited, Stabilisation |
| **mTOR Inhibitors (Everolimus)** | Recurrent | Phase 2: Modest PFS |
| **VEGFR/EGFR Inhibitors (Sunitinib, Erlotinib)** | Recurrent | Minimal Activity |
| **Immunotherapy (Pembrolizumab)** | Hypermutated/MSI-H | Case Reports |

## 11. 11. Surveillance & Recurrence

| Grade | Post-GTR Surveillance | Post-STR/RT Surveillance |
|-------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| **Grade 1** | **MRI q6-12mo ×5yr**, then **q1-2yr** (Lifelong) | **MRI q6mo ×3yr**, then q12mo |
| **Grade 2** | **MRI q6mo ×3yr**, then q12mo ×2yr | **MRI q6mo ×5yr** |
| **Grade 3** | **MRI q3-6mo ×3yr**, then q6mo | **MRI q3-6mo ×5yr** |

**Recurrence Risk Factors:** Higher Grade, Simpson Grade III-V, Younger Age, Male Sex, Progesterone Receptor Negative, **TERT Promoter Mutation**.

## 14. 14. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Grade 1 vs 2 — Mitoses Threshold** | **Grade 1: <4/10 HPF**; **Grade 2: 4-19/10 HPF**; **Grade 3: ≥20/10 HPF** |
| **Brain Invasion vs Abutment** | **Invasion = Irregular Infiltration into Parenchyma (Grade 2)**; **Abutment = Clear Plane (Grade 1)** |
| **Simpson Grade 0?** | **Not in Original Classification**; Some Add **Grade 0 = GTR + Dura + Bone + Venous Sinus Reconstruction** |
| **SRS vs FSRT for Optic Apparatus** | **SRS Contraindicated if Within 2-3mm of Optic Nerve/Chiasm**; **FSRT (5-25fx) Spares Optic Structures** |
| **PR Status** | **PR+ (80%)**, **Hormone Sensitive** (Growth in Pregnancy); **PR- = Worse Prognosis** |
| **NF2 vs Non-NF2** | **NF2: Fibrous/Transitional, Spinal, Parasagittal**; **Non-NF2: Secretory, Meningothelial, Angiomatous, Higher Grade** |

**Mnemonic: MENINGIOMA**
- **M**ost Common Primary CNS Tumour (30-40%)
- **E**pithelial (Meningothelial), **F**ibrous, **T**ransitional, **P**sammoma Bodies
- **N**F2 Mutation (50%, Merlin, 22q), **TRAF7** (20%)
- **I**ncidental 20-30%, **Female 2:1**, **PR+ (Hormone Sensitive)**
- **N**ew WHO Grades: **Gr1 (<4 Mitoses), Gr2 (4-19/Brain Invasion), Gr3 (≥20/Anaplasia)**
- **G**rade 1: Obs/Surgery; **Gr2: RT Adjuvant**; **Gr3: Mandatory RT**
- **I**nvasion: **Brain Invasion = Gr2** (NOT Abutment)
- **O**peration: **Simpson Grade I (Best)**: Tumour + Dura + Bone
- **M**RI: **Homogeneous Enhancement, Dural Tail, Broad Base, Hyperostosis**
- **O**ptic Sheath: **SRS/FSRT** (Surgery = Blindness)
- **A**ngiomatous/Clear Cell/Chordoid: **Higher Grade Potential**
