## 2. 2. Young Women (<40 Years)

### 1. Epidemiology & Biology
| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Incidence** | **~7% of breast cancers** (<40yr) |
| **Biology** | **More aggressive**: Higher grade, HER2+, TNBC, LVI+, Node+ |
| **Genetics** | **Higher BRCA1/2 mutation rate** (~15-20%) |
| **Outcome** | **Worse prognosis** vs older (Stage-adjusted) |
| **Fertility Concerns** | **Premature ovarian insufficiency** (Chemo-induced) |

### 2. Management Priorities
| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1. Fertility Preservation** | **Refer before treatment**: Oocyte/Embryo cryo, Ovarian tissue cryo, GnRHa |
| **2. Genetic Testing** | **BRCA1/2, PALB2, TP53** (All <40, regardless of FH) |
| **3. Aggressive Treatment** | **Standard protocols** (No de-escalation based on age alone) |
| **4. Contraception** | **Non-hormonal IUD** (Cu-IUD) during treatment |
| **5. Pregnancy After Treatment** | **Wait 2-5 years** (Individualised, ER+ wait longer) |

## 3. 3. Pregnancy-Associated Breast Cancer (PABC)

> **Definition**: Breast cancer diagnosed during pregnancy or within 1 year postpartum.
> **Incidence**: ~1:3000 pregnancies; **Most common malignancy in pregnancy**.

### 1. Diagnosis
| Modality | Safety |
|-----------|--------|
| **Ultrasound** | **First-line** (No radiation) |
| **Mammogram** | **Abdominal shielding**, Low dose (Safe) |
| **MRI** | **Gadolinium contraindicated** (Fetal risk) |
| **Core Biopsy** | **Safe** (Local anaesthesia) |
| **Staging** | **Chest X-ray (Shielded), US Liver, MRI Spine/Brain** (No CT/PET) |

### 2. Treatment by Trimester
| Trimester | Surgery | Chemotherapy | Radiotherapy | Endocrine |
|-----------|---------|--------------|--------------|-----------|
| **1st (0-12wk)** | **Safe** (Mastectomy/BCS + SLNB) | **CONTRAINDICATED** (Teratogenic) | **CONTRAINDICATED** | **CONTRAINDICATED** |
| **2nd (13-26wk)** | **Safe** | **SAFE** (AC/FAC/EC) | **CONTRAINDICATED** | **CONTRAINDICATED** |
| **3rd (27wk+)** | **Safe** | **SAFE** (AC/FAC/EC) | **CONTRAINDICATED** | **CONTRAINDICATED** |
| **Postpartum** | — | **Continue/Complete** | **SAFE** (Postpartum) | **Tamoxifen/AI** (After delivery) |

### 3. Key Points
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Chemo Regimen** | **AC (Doxorubicin + Cyclophosphamide) q3w ×4** or **FAC/EC** |
| **Anthracyclines** | **Safe 2nd/3rd trimester** (No teratogenicity) |
| **Taxanes** | **Limited data** (Generally avoided in pregnancy) |
| **Trastuzumab/Pertuzumab** | **CONTRAINDICATED** (Fetal renal toxicity - Oligohydramnios) |
| **Endocrine Therapy** | **Tamoxifen/AI CONTRAINDICATED** (Teratogenic) |
| **Radiotherapy** | **Postpartum** (Delay if possible to avoid fetal exposure) |
| **Delivery** | **Delay until ≥37 weeks** if possible; **Induce after chemo completion** |

### 4. Prognosis
| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Stage-matched** | **Similar to non-pregnant** (When treated appropriately) |
| **Delay in diagnosis** | **Common** (Breast changes masked by pregnancy) |
| **Termination** | **Not required** for outcomes (Personal choice) |

## 4. 4. Male Breast Cancer

### 1. Epidemiology
| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Incidence** | **<1% of all breast cancers** (~350/yr UK) |
| **Peak Age** | **60-70 years** |
| **Risk Factors** | **BRCA2 (15-20%)**, **Klinefelter (XXY)**, **Liver cirrhosis**, **Testicular disorders**, **Radiation exposure** |
| **Genetics** | **BRCA2 (15-20%)** > BRCA1; **CHEK2, PALB2, CHEK2** |

### 2. Clinical Presentation
| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Presentation** | **Painless lump** (Retroareolar, firm, fixed) |
| **Gynaecomastia** | **Common differential** (Bilateral, tender, soft) |
| **Nipple Discharge** | **Bloody/Serous** (Suspicious) |
| **Stage at Diagnosis** | **Often later** (Lack of awareness, no screening) |

### 3. Treatment
| Modality | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| **Surgery** | **Mastectomy + SLNB/ALND** (BCS rarely feasible - small breast) |
| **Adjuvant Endocrine** | **Tamoxifen 20mg ×5-10yr** (Standard; AI contraindicated without OFS) |
| **Adjuvant Chemo** | **Same as female** (Anthracycline/Taxane based on risk) |
| **HER2+** | **Trastuzumab 1yr** (Same as female) |
| **Radiotherapy** | **Same indications as female** |
| **Genetic Testing** | **BRCA1/2, PALB2, CHEK2** (All men with breast cancer) |

### 4. Key Differences from Female
| Feature | Male | Female |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Surgery** | **Mastectomy standard** (BCS rare) | BCS common |
| **Endocrine** | **Tamoxifen only** (AI ineffective without OFS) | AI/Tamoxifen |
| **BRCA2** | **Higher penetrance** (6-8% lifetime) | 40-60% |
| **Outcome** | **Stage-matched similar** | Similar |

## 5. 5. Elderly Breast Cancer (>70 Years)

### 1. Geriatric Assessment
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **CIRS-G** (Cumulative Illness Rating Scale-Geriatric) | **Comorbidity burden** (Score >6 = High) |
| **G8 Screening** | **Quick screen** (≤14 = Impaired → Full GA) |
| **Comprehensive GA (CGA)** | **Functional, Cognitive, Nutritional, Social, Polypharmacy** |
| **ADL/IADL** | **Functional independence** |
| **Mini-Cog / MoCA** | **Cognitive screening** |

### 2. Treatment De-escalation

#### Surgery
| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| **Early Stage, ER+, Low Grade** | **Primary Endocrine Therapy** (Letrozole/Anastrozole) **vs Surgery** |
| **Frailty/High Comorbidity** | **Primary Endocrine** (Avoid surgery/anesthesia risk) |
| **Fit Elderly** | **Standard Surgery** (BCS + SLNB) |

#### Radiotherapy
| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| **Age >70, ER+, T1-2, N0, Grade 1-2** | **Omit RT after BCS** (PRIME II, CALGB 9343) |
| **High Risk (Grade 3, LVI, Node+)** | **RT as standard** |
| **Hypofractionation** | **40Gy/15fx or 26Gy/5fx** (Preferred in elderly) |

#### Chemotherapy
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **CARG Toxicity Score** | **Predict Grade 3-5 Toxicity** (Age, Hgb, CrCl, Hearing, Falls, Meds) |
| **CRASH Score** | **Chemo toxicity prediction** |
| **Genomic Assay (Oncotype/MammaPrint)** | **Avoid chemo if low genomic risk** |
| **Regimen Choice** | **TC (Docetaxel/Cyclophosphamide) preferred over AC** (Less cardiotoxicity) |
| **Dose Reduction** | **Consider 75-80% dose** if frail |

#### Endocrine Therapy
| Agent | Elderly Considerations |
|-------|------------------------|
| **Aromatase Inhibitors** | **Bone loss risk** (Dexa q1-2yr, Calcium/Vit D, Zoledronic if high risk) |
| **Tamoxifen** | **VTE/Stroke risk** (Caution if prior VTE, Atrial fibrillation) |
| **Duration** | **5 years standard**; **Consider 5yr vs 10yr** based on life expectancy |

## 6. 6. Survivorship Issues in Special Populations

| Issue | Young Women | Pregnancy | Elderly |
|-------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Fertility** | **Preserve pre-treatment** (Oocyte/Embryo cryo) | **Contraception during tx (Cu-IUD)** | N/A |
| **Premature Menopause** | **Chemo-induced POI** (HRT if ER-) | **Temporary ovarian suppression** | **Natural menopause** |
| **Bone Health** | **Dexa q1-2yr, Ca/Vit D, Zoledronic if AI** | **Ca/Vit D, Dexa postpartum** | **Dexa q1-2yr, AI → Bone loss** |
| **Cardiotoxicity** | **Anthracycline/Trastuzumab monitoring** | **Anthracycline monitoring** | **Baseline Echo, q6-12mo** |
| **Psychosocial** | **Fertility distress, Body image, Young kids** | **Parenting with cancer, Postpartum depression** | **Isolation, Caregiver burden, Cognitive decline** |

## 8. 8. Viva Questions (MRCP PACES / FCPS)

| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Young Woman <40, Breast Cancer — First Step?** | **Fertility Preservation Referral** (Oocyte/Embryo Cryopreservation) **BEFORE Chemo**. |
| **BRCA Testing in Young Women — When?** | **All women <40 with Breast Cancer** (Regardless of family history). |
| **PABC 1st Trimester — Chemo?** | **CONTRAINDICATED** (Teratogenic); **Surgery Safe**. |
| **PABC 2nd Trimester — Safe Chemo?** | **AC (Doxorubicin + Cyclophosphamide) or FAC/EC**; **NO Taxanes (Limited data), NO Trastuzumab**. |
| **Trastuzumab in Pregnancy — Why Contraindicated?** | **Fetal Renal Toxicity** (Oligohydramnios, Pulmonary Hypoplasia). |
| **Male Breast Cancer — Endocrine Adjuvant?** | **Tamoxifen 20mg ×5-10yr** (AI only with OFS, which is not standard in males). |
| **Male Breast Cancer — BRCA2 Frequency?** | **15-20%** (Higher than female BRCA1). |
| **Elderly >70, ER+ T1N0 Grade 1 — RT after BCS?** | **OMIT RT** (PRIME II, CALGB 9343: No survival benefit, Low local recurrence). |
| **CGA in Elderly — Tools?** | **G8 Screen (≤14 = Full CGA)**, **CIRS-G (Comorbidity)**, **ADL/IADL, Mini-Cog**. |
| **CARG Score — Purpose?** | **Predict Grade 3-5 Chemo Toxicity** (Age, Hgb, CrCl, Hearing, Falls, Meds). |
| **Primary Endocrine Therapy Elderly — Indication?** | **ER+, T1-2, N0, Grade 1-2, Fit for surgery but prefer non-surgical / High surgical risk**. |
| **Chemo in Elderly — Preferred Regimen?** | **TC (Docetaxel/Cyclophosphamide) over AC** (Less cardiotoxicity, similar efficacy). |

## 9. 9. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **PABC Chemo 1st Trimester** | **Absolutely Contraindicated** (Organogenesis period) |
| **Taxanes in Pregnancy** | **Limited Safety Data** — **Avoid if possible** (Animal teratogenicity) |
| **Trastuzumab in Pregnancy** | **Contraindicated** (Fetal Renal Toxicity - Oligohydramnios) |
| **Tamoxifen in Pregnancy** | **Contraindicated** (Teratogenic - Goldenhar Syndrome) |
| **AI in Males** | **Ineffective without OFS** (Testicular testosterone production continues) |
| **Primary Endocrine vs Surgery** | **Primary Endocrine = Letrozole/Anastrozole instead of surgery** (Elderly, ER+, Low Risk) |
| **CARG vs CRASH** | **CARG: Predicts Grade 3-5 Toxicity**; **CRASH: Predicts Grade 3-5 Haematologic/Non-haem** |
| **Primary Endocrine Duration** | **Indefinite until progression** (Not fixed 5-10yr like adjuvant) |

**Mnemonic: SPECIAL-POP-BREAST**
- **S**pecial Populations: **Young, Pregnant, Male, Elderly**
- **P**regnancy: **PABC** (1:3000), **Trimester-specific**
- **E**arly Age: **<40 = Aggressive, Fertility, BRCA, Cu-IUD**
- **C**hemo Pregnancy: **1st Trim NO, 2nd/3rd YES (AC/FAC/EC)**
- **I**mmunotherapy: **Trastuzumab NO (Fetal Renal)**
- **A**rthritis/Endocrine: **Tamoxifen NO (Teratogenic)**
- **L**actation: **RT Postpartum**
- **P**ostpartum: **Complete Chemo, Start Endocrine**
- **O**lder: **CGA (G8, CIRS-G), De-escalate**
- **P**rimary Endocrine: **Letrozole > Surgery (Frail ER+ T1-2)**
- **O**mit RT: **>70, ER+, T1-2 N0 Gr1-2** (PRIME II)
- **P**harmacology: **TC > AC (Elderly), CARG/CRASH Score**
- **U**nique Male: **BRCA2 20%, Tamoxifen Only, Mastectomy**
- **L**ife After: **Fertility, Menopause, Bone, Heart, Psychosocial**
- **A**dolescent: **Fertility Preservation Pre-Tx**
- **T**ransition: **Paed to Adult Survivorship**
