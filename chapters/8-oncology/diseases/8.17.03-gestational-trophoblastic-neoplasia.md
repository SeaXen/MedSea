> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP Priority: MEDIUM**
> **GTN = Spectrum of trophoblastic diseases**: **Hydatidiform Mole** (Complete/Partial), **Invasive Mole**, **Choriocarcinoma**, **Placental Site Trophoblastic Tumour (PSTT)**, **Epithelioid Trophoblastic Tumour (ETT)**; **FIGO 2000 Anatomic Staging + WHO Prognostic Risk Score**; **hCG is tumour marker — Mandatory monitoring**; **Low-risk (Score ≤6): Single-agent (Methotrexate / Actinomycin D)**; **High-risk (Score ≥7): Multi-agent (EMA-CO / EMAC)**; **Resistance/Relapse → EMA-EP / TEP**; **Cure rate >95%**; **hCG surveillance post-chemo**.

## 1. 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Classify **GTN spectrum**: Complete/Partial Mole, Invasive Mole, Choriocarcinoma, PSTT, ETT
- [ ] Apply **FIGO 2000 Staging (I-IV) + WHO Risk Score (0-25)**
- [ ] Interpret **hCG kinetics** for diagnosis, monitoring, remission definition
- [ ] Prescribe **Low-risk**: Methotrexate (weekly/biweekly) or Actinomycin D (5-day/biweekly)
- [ ] Prescribe **High-risk**: **EMA-CO** (Etoposide, Methotrexate, Actinomycin D, Cyclophosphamide, Oncovin) — **Standard**
- [ ] Manage **resistance**: **EMA-EP** (Etoposide, Methotrexate, Actinomycin D, Etoposide, Cisplatin) / **TEP**
- [ ] Define **remission** (hCG normal ×3 weekly) and **surveillance** (monthly ×1yr, then q3mo ×1yr)
- [ ] Recognise **PSTT/ETT**: Low hCG, Surgery primary, Chemo limited role

## 2. 2. Classification & Spectrum

| Entity | Features | hCG Level | Malignant Potential |
|--------|----------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Complete Hydatidiform Mole (CHM)** | **46,XX (Androgenetic)**, No fetus, Diffuse villous swelling | **Very High** (>100,000) | **15-20% → GTN** |
| **Partial Hydatidiform Mole (PHM)** | **69,XXY (Diandric)**, Fetal parts, Focal swelling | Moderate | **1-5% → GTN** |
| **Invasive Mole** | **Myometrial invasion** by molar tissue | High | **GTN** (locally invasive) |
| **Choriocarcinoma** | **Biphasic**: Cytotrophoblast + Syncytiotrophoblast, **No villi**, Haemorrhagic, Metastatic | Very High | **Highly malignant** |
| **PSTT** | **Intermediate trophoblast**, Minimal syncytiotrophoblast, **Low hCG**, Myometrial invasion | **Low** | **Low malignant potential** |
| **ETT** | **Chorionic-type intermediate trophoblast**, **Low hCG**, **Epithelioid** | **Low** | **Variable** |

## 3. 3. FIGO 2000 Staging + WHO Risk Score

### 1. Anatomic Staging

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **I** | Confined to uterus |
| **II** | Extension to genital structures (adnexa, vagina, broad ligament) |
| **III** | Lung metastases |
| **IV** | Distant mets (Brain, Liver, Kidney, GI) |

### 2. WHO Prognostic Risk Score (Total 0-25)

| Factor | Score 0 | Score 1 | Score 2 | Score 4 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| **Age** | <40 | ≥40 | — | — |
| **Antecedent Pregnancy** | Mole | Abortion | Term | — |
| **Interval (months)** | <4 | 4-6 | 7-12 | >12 |
| **hCG (IU/L)** | <10³ | 10³-10⁴ | 10⁴-10⁵ | >10⁵ |
| **Largest Tumour (cm)** | <3 | 3-5 | >5 | — |
| **Site of Mets** | Lung | Spleen/Kidney | GI/Liver | Brain |
| **Number of Mets** | 0 | 1-4 | 5-8 | >8 |
| **Prior Failed Chemo** | No | Single-agent | Multi-agent | — |

**Risk Groups:**
- **Low Risk: Score ≤6** → **Single-agent chemo**
- **High Risk: Score ≥7** → **Multi-agent chemo**

## 4. 4. Diagnosis & hCG Monitoring

### 1. Diagnostic Criteria for GTN (Post-Molar)

**Any ONE of:**
1. **hCG plateau** (≤10% change ×4 weekly)
2. **hCG rise** (≥10% ×3 weekly or ×2 weekly over 2 weeks)
3. **hCG persistence** (>6 months post-evacuation)
4. **Histological diagnosis** (Choriocarcinoma, PSTT, ETT)
5. **Metastases** on imaging

### 2. hCG Monitoring Schedule

| Phase | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|
| **Post-evacuation** | Weekly until normal ×3 |
| **During Chemo** | **Weekly** (or biweekly for Actinomycin D 5-day) |
| **Post-chemo (Remission)** | **Weekly ×3** → **Monthly ×1 year** → **Quarterly ×1 year** |
| **Pregnancy after GTN** | **hCG at 6-8 weeks** (exclude recurrence) |

## 5. 5. Management

### 1. Low-Risk GTN (WHO Score ≤6)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Low-Risk GTN (Score ≤6)] --> B{First-Line Agent}
    B -->|**Methotrexate (MTX)**| C[**Weekly IM/IV MTX 30mg/m²**
**OR Biweekly MTX 100mg/m² + Leucovorin 15mg q6h×4**
**Response: hCG ↓10% weekly**
**Cycles: Until hCG normal ×3 + 1-2 consolidation**]
    B -->|**Actinomycin D (Act-D)**| D[**5-day IV Act-D 1.25mg/m²/d q2wk**
**OR Biweekly Act-D 0.5mg/m² q2wk**
**Preferred if MTX contraindicated** (renal, hepatic, pleural effusion)
**Faster response, less cycles**]
    C --> E[**Remission: hCG normal ×3 weekly**]
    D --> E
    E --> F[**Consolidation: 1-2 cycles post-normal hCG**]
    F --> G[**Surveillance: Monthly ×1yr, q3mo ×1yr**]
```

### 2. High-Risk GTN (WHO Score ≥7)

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[High-Risk GTN (Score ≥7)] --> B[**EMA-CO (Standard Worldwide)**]
    B --> B1[**Week 1 (Day 1-2): Etoposide 100mg/m² + Actinomycin D 0.5mg/m²**]
    B --> B2[**Week 1 (Day 2): Methotrexate 300mg/m² IV + Leucovorin Rescue**]
    B --> B3[**Week 2 (Day 8): Cyclophosphamide 600mg/m² + Vincristine 1.4mg/m² (max 2mg)**]
    B --> B4[**Repeat q2wk (14-day cycles)**]
    B1 --> C[**Response: hCG ↓10% per cycle (Week 2 hCG)**]
    C --> D[**Cycles: Until hCG normal ×3 + 3 consolidation cycles**]
    D --> E[**Remission → Surveillance**]
```

### 3. Resistance / Relapse

| Setting | Regimen |
|---------|---------|
| **Low-risk resistant to MTX** | **Switch to Actinomycin D** (or vice versa) |
| **Low-risk resistant to both** | **EMA-CO** |
| **High-risk resistant to EMA-CO** | **EMA-EP** (Etoposide, MTX, Act-D, Etoposide, Cisplatin) q2wk
**OR TEP** (Paclitaxel, Etoposide, Cisplatin) |
| **Brain Mets** | **EMA-CO + Whole Brain RT** (or SRS for oligo) |
| **Liver Mets** | **EMA-CO / EMA-EP** (Aggressive) |

### 4. PSTT / ETT (Special)

| Feature | Management |
|---------|------------|
| **Primary** | **Surgery (Hysterectomy ± BSO + PLND)** — **Chemo limited role** |
| **Metastatic** | **EMA-CO / EMA-EP** (variable response) |
| **hCG** | **Low / Not reliable marker** — **Clinical/Imaging follow-up** |
| **Prognosis** | **PSTT: Stage I excellent**; **ETT: Worse** |

## 8. 8. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **WHO Score vs FIGO Stage** | **FIGO = Anatomic (I-IV)**; **WHO = Prognostic (0-25)**; **BOTH used together** (e.g., Stage III, Score 8 = High-risk) |
| **Low-risk vs High-risk Chemo** | **Low: Single-agent (MTX/Act-D)**; **High: Multi-agent (EMA-CO)**; **Score ≤6 vs ≥7** |
| **MTX Weekly vs Biweekly** | **Weekly 30mg/m²** (traditional); **Biweekly 100mg/m² + Leucovorin** (less visits, similar efficacy) |
| **Actinomycin D 5-day vs Biweekly** | **5-day 1.25mg/m² q2wk** (standard); **Biweekly 0.5mg/m²** (less myelosuppression) |
| **EMA-CO vs EMA-EP** | **EMA-CO: Cyclo + Vincristine** (Standard High-risk); **EMA-EP: Cisplatin + Extra Etoposide** (Salvage for EMA-CO failure) |
| **PSTT vs ETT** | **PSTT: Intermediate trophoblast, chorionic gonadotrophin+, better**; **ETT: Epithelioid, EMA+, worse** |
| **hCG in PSTT** | **Low / Not proportional to tumour burden** — **Do not use for response monitoring** |

**Mnemonic: GTN-HCG**
- **G**TN Spectrum: **Mole → Invasive → Chorio → PSTT/ETT**
- **T**rophoblastic: **hCG = Marker** (Except PSTT/ETT low)
- **N**ew Mole: **CHM 46,XX**, **PHM 69,XXY**
- **H**CG Kinetics: **Plateau ×4wk, Rise ×3wk, Persist >6mo** = GTN
- **C**hemo Low-risk: **MTX (Weekly/Biweekly) / Act-D (5-day)**
- **O**nco High-risk: **EMA-CO** (Etopo+ActD → MTX+Leuco → Cyclo+Vinci) q2wk
- **Risk Score**: **WHO ≤6 Low (Single), ≥7 High (EMA-CO)**
- **NOTable**: **PSTT/ETT** — **Surgery Primary, Low hCG, Chemo Limited**
- **E**MA-CO Failure: **EMA-EP / TEP**
- **C**ure Rate: **>95%** (Low ~100%, High ~85-90%)
- **Surveillance**: **Monthly ×1yr → q3mo ×1yr**
- **P**regnancy After: **Wait 1yr**, **hCG at 6-8wk**

**Q7:** PSTT/ETT?
**A7:** Surgery primary, Low hCG, Chemo limited; PSTT Stage I = Cure

**Q8:** Cure Rate?
**A8:** >95% overall (Low-risk ~100%, High-risk ~85-90%)
