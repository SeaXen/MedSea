## 2. 2. UK Bowel Cancer Screening Programme (BCSP)

### 1. Programme Overview
| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age Range** | **60-74 years** (Extension to **50-59** in progress) |
| **Interval** | **Every 2 years** |
| **Test** | **FIT (Faecal Immunochemical Test)** — **Quantitative, Single Sample** |
| **Threshold** | **≥120 µg Hb/g faeces** |
| **Colonoscopy Referral** | **FIT+ve → Colonoscopy** (2-week wait target) |
| **Coverage** | **~65-70%** of eligible population |
| **Positivity Rate** | **~5-7%** |
| **Cancer Detection Rate** | **~15-20 per 1000 invited** |

### 2. FIT Performance
| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Sensitivity (CRC)** | **~70-80%** | Lower for proximal/right-sided |
| **Specificity** | **~95-97%** | |
| **PPV (Cancer)** | **10-15%** | At threshold 120µg/g |
| **NPV** | **>99.9%** | High negative predictive value |

### 3. Colonoscopy Referral
| FIT Result | Action |
|------------|--------|

## 5. 5. Post-Polypectomy Surveillance (BSG / NICE / USMSTF)

| Risk Group | Findings | Surveillance Interval |
|------------|----------|-----------------------|
| **Low Risk** | 1-2 Polyps, <10mm, Tubular, LGD | **5 Years** |
| **Intermediate Risk** | 3-4 Polyps, OR ≥1 Polyp ≥10mm, OR Villous/Tubulovillous, OR HGD | **3 Years** |
| **High Risk** | ≥5 Polyps, OR ≥1 Polyp ≥20mm, OR HGD in ≥10mm Polyp, OR Serrated Polyp ≥10mm, OR Traditional Serrated Adenoma | **1 Year** |

## 9. 9. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **FIT vs FOBT** | **FIT**: Quantitative, Specific for Human Hb, Single Sample; **FOBT**: Guaiac, Qualitative, Non-Specific, 3 Samples |
| **FIT Threshold** | **Lower Threshold** = More Sensitivity, More Colonoscopies; **Higher** = More Specificity, Missed Cancers |
| **Immunosuppression — SCC Risk** | **Organ Transplant: ↑65-250x**; **SCC:BCC Ratio Reverses in Tx** |
| **SIOP vs COG Wilms** | **SIOP: Pre-op Chemo First**; **COG: Upfront Surgery First** — **Both Give Similar Outcomes** |

**Mnemonic: CRC-SCREENING**
- **C**RC Screening: **UK BCSP 60-74, FIT 2yr, 120µg/g**
- **R**ef. Colonoscopy: **FIT ≥120 → 2ww**
- **C**olorectal Surveillance: **Low 5yr, Int 3yr, High 1yr**
- **S**yndromes: **Lynch (Colonoscopy 1-2yr), FAP (Annual), IBD q1-2yr**

- **E**arly Detection: **FIT Sens 70-80%, Spec 95-97%**
- **E**arly CRC: **FIT ≥120 → 2ww Colonscopy**
- **R**esection Follow-up: **Colonoscopy 1yr → 3yr → 5yr; CEA q6mo**
