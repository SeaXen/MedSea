## 2. 2. ЁЯУЦ Definitions

| Term | Definition | Clinical Significance |

| **Polypharmacy** | Use of **тЙе5 medications** (most common definition) | Risk threshold; тЖС ADEs, falls, hospitalisation |

## 8. 8. ЁЯдп Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Polypharmacy = always bad?** | **No** тАФ appropriate polypharmacy (HF, DM, HIV) is evidence-based and beneficial |
| **Polypharmacy = drug-drug interaction risk** | тЖС Linear with drug count; **rule of thumb: each additional drug = тЖС 5тАУ10% ADE risk** |
| **Polypharmacy тЙа overprescribing** | Polypharmacy = **count of drugs**; overprescribing = **inappropriate drug count** |
| **Prescribing cascade тЙа side effect** | Cascade = **misinterpreted ADE тЖТ new drug** (the trap) |

**Mnemonics:**
- **"POLYPHARMACY = 5+ DRUGS"** = threshold for review
- **"APPROPRIATE POLYPHARMACY"** = HF GDMT, DM, HIV, Asthma тАФ evidence-based
- **"INAPPROPRIATE POLYPHARMACY"** = At least one harmful/unnecessary/duplicative
- **"ADE CASCADE"** = **A**DE тЖТ **D**isease misdiagnosis тЖТ **E**scalating drug list
- **"STOPP/START"** = **S**creening **T**ool **O**lder **P**ersons' **P**rescriptions / **S**creening **T**ool **A**lert **R**ight **T**reatment
