# Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve Replacement (TPVR)

## 1. Indications
- **Severe PR** post-TOF repair with RV dilation/dysfunction
- **Conduit dysfunction** (RV-PA) — stenosis or regurgitation
- Symptomatic or progressive RV dilation

## 2. Devices
- **Melody valve** (Medtronic) — bovine jugular vein, 16–22 mm
- **Sapien valve** (Edwards) — 20–29 mm

## 3. Procedure
- Femoral or internal jugular access
- Conduit preparation (prestenting)
- Valve deployment

## 4. Outcomes & Follow-up
- Procedural success >95%
- Endocarditis risk 1–2% per year
- Lifelong cardiology follow-up
- Re-intervention rate ~10% at 5 years

> **Pearls** — **Melody** for small conduits (≤22 mm), **Sapien** for larger. Endocarditis risk is real — prophylactic antibiotics. **CMR follow-up** is standard.
