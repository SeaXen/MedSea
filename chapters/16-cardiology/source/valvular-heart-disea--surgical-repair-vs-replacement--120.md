# Surgical Repair vs Replacement

## 1. Mitral
- **Repair preferred** for degenerative MR (90–95% repairable)
- Advantages: ↓ mortality, ↓ complications, no anticoagulation
- Replacement for rheumatic, heavily calcified, failed repair

## 2. Aortic
- **Replacement standard** for most AS/AR
- Repair for select: BAV with regurgitation, root aneurysm (David, Yacoub procedures)
- TAVI for older/high-risk AS

## 3. Tricuspid
- **Repair preferred** (annuloplasty ring)
- Replacement only if severe destruction, failed repair

## 4. Decision Factors
| Factor | Favors Repair | Favors Replacement |
|---|---|---|
| Anatomy | Localized pathology | Diffuse/calcified |
| Age | Younger | Older |
| Anticoag tolerance | Want to avoid | Can tolerate |
| Surgeon expertise | Mitral/TV expert | — |
| Etiology | Degenerative, prolapse | Rheumatic, endocarditis |

> **Pearls** — **Mitral repair > replacement** for degenerative MR (lower operative mortality, better long-term survival, avoids anticoag). Refer to **high-volume valve centers**. AS is mostly replacement territory; TAVI has revolutionized the field.
