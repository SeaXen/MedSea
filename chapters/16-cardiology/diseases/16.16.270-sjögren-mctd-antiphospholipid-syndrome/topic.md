# Sjögren, MCTD, Antiphospholipid Syndrome

## 1. Sjögren Syndrome
- Dry eyes/mouth + anti-Ro/La
- Cardiac: **congenital heart block** (neonatal, anti-Ro crossing placenta), pericarditis, Raynaud, autonomic dysfunction
- ↑ risk of **lymphoma**

## 2. Mixed Connective Tissue Disease (MCTD)
- Overlap of SLE, SSc, polymyositis; anti-U1-RNP
- Cardiac: **PAH** (worst prognostic), pericarditis, myocarditis, conduction disease
- Manage with immunosuppression + PAH Rx

## 3. Antiphospholipid Syndrome (APLS)
- Thrombosis (arterial/venous) + pregnancy morbidity + aPL antibodies (lupus anticoagulant, anti-cardiolipin, anti-β2GP1)
- Cardiac: **valvular disease** (sterile vegetations, mitral > aortic), IHD (MI in young), intracardiac thrombus, pulmonary HTN
- **CAPS** (catastrophic APLS): multi-organ thrombosis — high mortality

## 4. Management
- **APLS thrombosis**: lifelong warfarin (INR 2-3); DOACs **inferior** in arterial/venous
- **CAPS**: anticoagulation + IVIG + plasma exchange + rituximab
- Pregnancy: LMWH + low-dose aspirin

> **Pearls**: APLS = **lupus anticoagulant** despite its name prolongs PTT in vitro. **DOACs fail** in APLS — use warfarin.
