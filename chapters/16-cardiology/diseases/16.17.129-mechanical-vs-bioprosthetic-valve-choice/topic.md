# Mechanical vs Bioprosthetic Valve Choice

## 1. Definition
**Prosthetic valve choice** = decision between **mechanical** (durable, requires lifelong anticoagulation) and **bioprosthetic** (tissue, limited durability ~10–15 y, no long-term anticoagulation). Patient age, lifestyle, surgical risk, ability to anticoagulate all factor in.

## 2. Key Features
- **Mechanical valves**: bileaflet (St Jude, CarboMedics), single tilting disc (Medtronic Hall), caged ball (Starr-Edwards, historical)
  - **Durable** (essentially lifelong)
  - **Lifelong warfarin** (INR 2.5–3.5 depending on valve/position)
  - Higher bleeding risk; lower reoperation risk
  - **Thrombosis risk** higher if anticoagulation interrupted
- **Bioprosthetic (tissue) valves**: bovine pericardial, porcine
  - **Limited durability** (10–15 y; faster in younger patients)
  - **No lifelong anticoagulation** (just 3–6 mo)
  - **Reoperation** at structural valve deterioration
  - **TAVI** for failed bioprosthetic ("valve-in-valve")
- **Decision factors**:
  - **Age <50**: mechanical usually preferred (durability)
  - **Age 50–65**: shared decision-making
  - **Age >65–70**: bioprosthetic usually preferred
  - **Life expectancy**, **bleeding risk**, **occupation/lifestyle**, **ability to comply with anticoagulation**, **reoperation risk**

## 3. Management
- **Mechanical**:
  - Warfarin (target INR per position: 2.5 aortic, 3.0 mitral)
  - Bridging for surgery
  - Aspirin 75–100 mg daily (some guidelines add)
  - **DOACs contraindicated**
- **Bioprosthetic**:
  - Warfarin or DOAC for 3–6 months (per guidelines)
  - Aspirin long-term
  - **Surveillance echo**: baseline, 5 y, then every 1–3 y
- **Pregnancy**: bioprosthetic preferred (avoid warfarin teratogenicity)
- **Valve-in-valve TAVI** for failed bioprosthetic in high-risk reoperation candidates
- **Endocarditis prophylaxis**: mechanical and bioprosthetic for dental/respiratory (per guidelines)

> **Pearls**
> - **<50 y → mechanical**, **>65 y → bioprosthetic**
> - **DOACs contraindicated in mechanical** valves
> - **Mechanical** = lifelong warfarin (INR 2.5–3.5)
> - **Bioprosthetic** = degenerates ~10–15 y (faster in young)
