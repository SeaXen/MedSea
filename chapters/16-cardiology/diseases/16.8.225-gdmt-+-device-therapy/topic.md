# DCM — Guideline-Directed Medical Therapy (GDMT) + Device

## 1. Four Pillars of HFrEF GDMT (All Apply to Non-Ischaemic DCM)
1. **ARNI** (sacubitril/valsartan) or ACEi/ARB
2. **β-blocker** (bisoprolol, carvedilol, metoprolol succinate, nebivolol)
3. **MRA** (spironolactone, eplerenone)
4. **SGLT2 inhibitor** (dapagliflozin, empagliflozin)

Each reduces mortality ~25% and is additive.

## 2. Stepwise Initiation
- Start low, titrate every 2 weeks; check BP, K⁺, creatinine
- **SGLT2i** can be started early regardless of diabetes status
- **ARNI** replaces ACEi/ARB once ACEi tolerated (washout 36 h)

## 3. Additional Therapies
- **Diuretics** for congestion (loop ± thiazide) — symptom relief only
- **Ivabradine** if HR ≥70 bpm in sinus rhythm despite max β-blocker
- **Hydralazine + isosorbide dinitrate** in Black patients with NYHA III–IV (A-HeFT)
- **Verquvo** (vericiguat) — HFrEF with recent decompensation (VICTORIA)
- **Omega-3 PUFA** — small mortality benefit
- **Iron** (ferric carboxymaltose) if ferritin <100 or 100–300 with TSAT <20% (FAIR-HF, AFFIRM-AHF)

## 4. Device Therapy in DCM
### ICD for Primary Prevention
- **LVEF ≤35%** on optimal GDMT for **≥3 months** AND
- NYHA II–III (or I ischaemic), expected survival >1 year
- 1-year mortality reduction (SCD-HeFT)

### CRT
- **LVEF ≤35% + LBBB + QRS ≥150 ms** → strong benefit
- **LBBB + QRS 120–149 ms** or non-LBBB ≥150 ms → consider
- Up to 30% LVEF improvement; mortality + hospitalisation benefit

## 5. Aetiology-Specific Therapy
| Etiology | Specific |
|---|---|
| **Lamin A/C (LMNA)** | ICD at LVEF ≤45% |
| **Peripartum** | Bromocriptine, no ACEi if breastfeeding |
| **Alcoholic** | Abstinence → 50% recovery |
| **Iron overload** | Phlebotomy / chelation |
| **Tachycardia-induced** | Rate/rhythm control |
| **Amyloid** | Tafamadis (ATTR), chemo (AL) |

## 6. Monitoring
- **Echo at 3 and 12 months** after optimisation
- **Reversed remodelling** (LVEF ≥50%, LVEDDi ≤33 mm/m²) in 30–40%
- **NT-proBNP** for risk stratification
- Consider **wearable defibrillator (WCD)** as bridge if LVEF <35% early (<40 days post-MI, ischaemic; or new DCM awaiting optimisation)

## 7. Advanced Therapies
- **LVAD** (HeartMate 3) for INTERMACS 2–4
- **Heart transplant** in advanced HF
- **Cardiac sympathetic denervation** (refractory VT)

> **Pearls**
> - **Four-pillar GDMT** (ARNI/ACEi + β-blocker + MRA + SGLT2i) reduces mortality and is recommended for all HFrEF including non-ischaemic DCM.
> - **ICD** for primary prevention: LVEF ≤35% on OMT ≥3 months.
> - **CRT** for LBBB + QRS ≥150 ms + LVEF ≤35% — biggest benefit.
> - Reassess LVEF at **3 months** post-optimisation — many DCM improve.
> - Aetiology-specific therapy (alcohol abstinence, bromocriptine, chelation) can reverse disease.
