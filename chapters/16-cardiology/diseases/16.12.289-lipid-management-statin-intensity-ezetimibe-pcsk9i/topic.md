# Lipid Management

## 1. Targets
- **LDL-C**: lower is better
  - High risk: <70 mg/dL (1.8 mmol/L)
  - Very high risk: <55 mg/dL (1.4) and **>50% reduction**
  - Extreme (recurrent events): <40 mg/dL (1.0)
- **Triglycerides**: <150 mg/dL (1.7 mmol/L)

## 2. Statin Intensity
- **High-intensity** (↓LDL ≥50%): atorvastatin 40-80, rosuvastatin 20-40
- **Moderate** (30-49%): atorvastatin 10-20, rosuvastatin 5-10
- **Low** (<30%): pravastatin, fluvastatin

## 3. Add-On Therapies
- **Ezetimibe**: ↓LDL ~20%; first add-on
- **PCSK9 inhibitors** (evolocumab, alirocumab): ↓LDL ~60%; for very-high risk not at goal
- **Bempedoic acid**: ↓LDL ~20%; statin-intolerant
- **Inclisiran**: siRNA, twice-yearly SC; ↓LDL ~50%
- **Icosapent ethyl** (REDUCE-IT): ↓TG, ↓CV events in TG 135-499 on statin

## 4. Pearls
> **Pearls**: Statin first-line in all high/very-high risk. **Liver monitoring** not routinely needed. Muscle symptoms: check adherence, switch, then consider CoQ10/statin-intolerance workup.
