# CV Risk Assessment (SCORE2, ASCVD, QRISK3)

## 1. Risk Calculators
- **SCORE2/SCORE2-OP** (ESC, 40-69 / 70-89): 10-yr fatal+non-fatal CVD risk; low/moderate/high/very-high regions
- **ASCVD** (ACC/AHA, 40-79): 10-yr first atherosclerotic event; used in US
- **QRISK3** (UK, 25-84): includes ethnicity, deprivation, comorbidities

## 2. Inputs
- Age, sex, race (ASCVD), smoking, SBP, total & HDL cholesterol (ASCVD)
- SCORE2: age, sex, smoking, SBP, non-HDL cholesterol

## 3. Risk Categories
- **Low**: <1% (SCORE2), <5% (ASCVD)
- **Moderate**: 1-<5% (SCORE2)
- **High**: 5-<10% (SCORE2)
- **Very high**: ≥10% (SCORE2) or established CVD/DM/CKD

## 4. Modifiers (up/down)
- ↑ risk: FH of premature CVD, BMI, social deprivation, CKD, HIV, autoimmune, CAC >100
- ↓ risk: HDL, LVH regression, lifestyle changes

> **Pearls**: Risk drives treatment threshold. **CAC = 0** allows deferral of statins in low-intermediate risk. Always reclassify borderline cases with imaging.
