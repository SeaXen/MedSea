# ICD — Primary & Secondary Prevention Criteria


The **Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD)** is the cornerstone device therapy for sudden cardiac death (SCD) prevention.

## 1. Definition & Background

- **ICD**: subcutaneous or transvenous device that continuously monitors rhythm and delivers tiered therapy (ATP, cardioversion, defibrillation shock) for VT/VF.
- **Primary prevention** — implant in patients **at risk** of SCD but no history of sustained VT/VF arrest.
- **Secondary prevention** — implant in patients who have **already survived** sustained VT/VF or sudden cardiac arrest (SCA), where cause is non-reversible.

## 2. Secondary Prevention (Class I)

Indicated in patients surviving:
- **Cardiac arrest** due to VT/VF (not within 48 h of acute MI, not due to reversible cause — electrolyte, drug, hypoxia).
- **Sustained VT** with haemodynamic compromise.
- **Sustained VT without compromise but with reduced LVEF** (≤35%).
- Syncope + inducible sustained VT/VF on EP study.
- Exclude reversible causes (acute ischaemia, electrolyte disturbance, drug-induced, commotio cordis, sepsis).

## 3. Primary Prevention — HFrEF

Implant if **all** of:
- **LVEF ≤35%** despite ≥3 months of **optimal guideline-directed medical therapy (GDMT)** — ARNI/ACEi/ARB + BB + MRA + SGLT2i.
- **NYHA II–III** (NYHA I — consider if additional risk markers).
- **Ischaemic cardiomyopathy** (≥40 d post-MI) OR **non-ischaemic dilated cardiomyopathy** (≥3 mo on GDMT).
- Expected survival **>1 year** with good functional status.
- **MADIT-II** (post-MI LVEF ≤30%), **SCD-HeFT** (ischaemic + non-ischaemic LVEF ≤35%, NYHA II–III), **DEFINITE** (NICM).

## 4. Primary Prevention — Other Conditions

| Condition | Indication |
|---|---|
| **HCM** | ESC 5-yr SCD risk ≥6%, or prior VT/VF, or unexplained syncope with massive LVH (≥30 mm), or NSVT + family SCD + massive LVH |
| **ARVC** | Sustained VT/VF, or extensive RV dysfunction, or syncope + inducible VT |
| **LQT / SQTS** | Survivors of cardiac arrest (secondary); consider for high-risk genotype + syncope despite BB |
| **Brugada** | Survivors of arrest (secondary); spontaneous type-1 ECG + syncope + positive EP study |
| **CPVT** | Survivors of arrest; recurrent syncope/VT despite BB + flecainide |
| **Sarcoid cardiac** | Sustained VT or LVEF ≤35% on immunosuppression |
| **Congenital HD repaired** | Specific criteria (post-TOF with inducible VT, single ventricle) |

- Confirm **LVEF on recent imaging** (echo, CMR, MUGA) and document GDMT optimisation.
- **Exclude reversible causes** of LV dysfunction (revascularise, treat tachycardia, alcohol cessation, thiamine).
- **Life expectancy** >1 y, **NYHA status**, frailty, comorbidities, patient wishes (shared decision).
- Screen for **psychological impact** — device anxiety, deactivation discussions at end of life.
- **Imaging** — venous anatomy, ICD type (single/dual chamber, CRT-D, S-ICD).
- **Single-chamber VVI** — most primary prevention (AF <1% at implant).
