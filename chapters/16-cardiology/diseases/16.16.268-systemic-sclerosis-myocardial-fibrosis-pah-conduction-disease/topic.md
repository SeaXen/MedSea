# Systemic Sclerosis and the Heart

## 1. Definition
- Autoimmune fibrosis of skin/viscera — limited vs diffuse
- Cardiac disease is a **major mortality driver**

## 2. Cardiac Manifestations
- **Myocardial fibrosis**: patchy, often subendocardial → HFpEF, then HFrEF, arrhythmias
- **PAH**: esp. **limited** cutaneous — screen with echo/PFTs yearly
- **Conduction disease**: fibrosis of SA node, AV block, SVT/VT
- **Pericardial effusion** (small, usually asymptomatic)
- **Microvascular ischemia**: abnormal CFR, even with normal coronaries (RGNO)

## 3. Management
- **PAH**: same as Group 1 PAH (endothelin-R antagonists, PDE5i, prostacyclins)
- **HF**: standard GDMT, cautious with β-blockers (Raynaud)
- **CCB** (nifedipine) for Raynaud
- **Immunosuppression**: MMF, rituximab in early diffuse disease
- **Avoid**: high-dose steroids (renal crisis risk)

## 4. Pearls
> **Pearls**: SSc renal crisis can cause **malignant HTN** — start **ACEi** (captopril) immediately. Annual echo + PFTs for PAH screening.
