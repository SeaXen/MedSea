# Cardiac Rehabilitation

## 1. Definition & Indications
Comprehensive secondary prevention program post-MI, post-PCI, post-CABG, stable HF, stable angina. **Class I** (ESC 2021 CV Prevention).

## 2. Core Components
- **Exercise training** — aerobic + resistance, ≥150 min/wk
- **Risk factor modification** — BP, lipids, glucose, weight, smoking
- **Psychosocial support** — depression/anxiety screening
- **Medication adherence** education
- **Return to work/sexual activity** counseling

## 3. Phases
1. Phase 1 (inpatient): early mobilization
2. Phase 2 (early outpatient, 2–12 wk): supervised
3. Phase 3 (maintenance, lifelong): self-directed

## 4. Benefits
- 20–30% all-cause mortality reduction
- ↓ recurrent MI ~25%
- ↑ VO₂ max ~20%
- ↑ QoL, ↓ readmission

> **Pearls** — Refer **before discharge** post-MI. Women, elderly, lower-SES under-referred. Use Mediterranean diet. Combine with smoking cessation.
