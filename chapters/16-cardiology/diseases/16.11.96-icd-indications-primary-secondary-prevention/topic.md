# ICD Indications in HF

## 1. Definition
**Implantable cardioverter-defibrillator (ICD)** prevents SCD in patients at high risk. Two categories: **primary prevention** (no prior arrhythmic event, but at risk) and **secondary prevention** (after VT/VF arrest).

## 2. Key Features
- **Primary prevention** (MADIT-II, SCD-HeFT criteria):
  - LVEF **≤35%** (after ≥3 months of GDMT)
  - **NYHA II–III** symptoms
  - **Ischaemic** (post-MI ≥40 days) or **non-ischaemic** CM
  - Life expectancy >1 year
  - **Class I**
- **Secondary prevention** (AVID, CIDS, CASH):
  - **Survivors of VF or haemodynamically unstable VT**
  - **Sustained VT with LV dysfunction** (LVEF <40%)
  - **Syncope with inducible sustained VT on EP study**
  - Class I regardless of LVEF
- **Not indicated**:
  - NYHA IV awaiting transplant/CRT-D (class IIa)
  - Within 40 days post-MI
  - Within 3 months of revascularisation
  - Active inflammation (myocarditis)
  - Recovered LVEF on GDMT (re-assess!)

## 3. Management
- **Single-chamber** (VVI) for most primary prevention
- **Dual-chamber** (DDD) for AV block/sinus node dysfunction indication
- **CRT-D** if LBBB + QRS ≥150 ms + LVEF ≤35% + NYHA II–IV
- **Generator change** every 5–9 years
- **Pre-implant**: hold anticoagulation periprocedurally; antibiotic prophylaxis
- **Post-implant**: monitor, programme (avoid inappropriate shocks, optimise detection zones)
- **Reassess LVEF at 3 months post-GDMT** before implant (recovery occurs)

> **Pearls**
> - **Reassess LVEF after 3 months GDMT** before implant
> - **Wait 40 days post-MI** (early ICD no benefit)
> - **CRT-D** if LBBB + QRS ≥150 ms + LVEF ≤35%
> - **Secondary prevention** ICD regardless of LVEF
