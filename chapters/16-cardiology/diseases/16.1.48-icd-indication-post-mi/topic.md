# ICD Indication Post-MI

## 1. Primary Prevention
- **LVEF ≤35%** at **≥40 days** post-MI on optimal GDMT (Class IA)
- NYHA II–III, expected survival >1 year
- Reassess LVEF at 6–12 wk post-revascularization

## 2. Secondary Prevention
- Documented VF or hemodynamically significant VT **>48 h** post-MI
- Cardiac arrest survivors with reversible cause treated

## 3. Bridge
- **WCD (LifeVest)** — for 40-day waiting period or pending reassessment

## 4. Key Trials
- **MADIT-II** (2002): LVEF ≤30% → ↓ mortality 31%
- **SCD-HeFT** (2005): LVEF ≤35% ischemic CM → ↓ mortality 23%

> **Pearls** — **Don't** implant <40 d post-MI (EF may recover). **Don't** in NYHA IV unless CRT-D/LVAD/HTx candidate. Optimize GDMT first.
