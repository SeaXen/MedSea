# Allogeneic Stem Cell Transplantation & GVHD

> [!info] **Davidson Ch 25 Alignment**: Haematological Malignancies / Supportive Care → Allogeneic Stem Cell Transplantation
> **FCPS/MRCP Focus**: Indications, donor sources, conditioning (MAC vs RIC), GVHD prophylaxis & treatment (acute/chronic), engraftment, complications, DLI, survival

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- [ ] Define **Allogeneic SCT**: Infusion of **Donor Haematopoietic Stem Cells** → **Immune Reconstitution + Graft-versus-Tumour (GVT) Effect**
- [ ] Select **Donor Source**: **Matched Sibling (MSD)** → **Matched Unrelated (MUD)** → **Haploidentical** → **Cord Blood** (Hierarchy of Preference)
- [ ] Apply **Conditioning Intensity**: **Myeloablative (MAC)** → **Reduced-Intensity (RIC)** → **Non-Myeloablative (NMA)** – Based on Age, Comorbidities, Disease Risk
- [ ] Prophylas & Treat **Acute GVHD (aGVHD)**: **Methotrexate + Calcineurin Inhibitor (CNI)** → **Steroids 1st Line** → **Ruxolitinib 2nd Line**
- [ ] Prophylax & Treat **Chronic GVHD (cGVHD)**: **CNI + MMF** → **Steroids 1st Line** → **Ruxolitinib/Ibrutinib/Belumosudil 2nd Line**
- [ ] Manage **Infections**: Engraftment Syndrome, CMV, EBV, Fungal, Encapsulated Organisms
- [ ] Apply **Donor Lymphocyte Infusion (DLI)**: Relapse Post-SCT, Mixed Chimerism
- [ ] Assess **Complications**: VOD/SOS, TMA, Haemorrhagic Cystitis, Engraftment Syndrome

## 📖 Indications for Allogeneic SCT

| Disease | Indication | Timing |
|---------|------------|--------|
| **AML** | **High/Int-2 Risk**, Relapsed, TP53 mut, Secondary | **CR1 (High/Int-2)**, CR2 |
| **ALL** | **High Risk (Ph+, t(4;11), Complex Karyotype, MRD+), Relapsed** | CR1 (High Risk), CR2 |
| **MDS** | **High/Very High IPSS-R**, TP53 mut, Progression on HMA | At Diagnosis (High/Very High) |
| **CML** | **TKI Failure**, Advanced Phase, T315I | Chronic Phase (TKI Fail) |
| **CLL** | **TP53/del17p**, Richter Transformation | Relapsed/Refractory |
| **NHL (Aggressive)** | **Relapsed/Refractory** (DLBCL, MCL, T-cell) | CR1 (High Risk) or Relapse |
| **Multiple Myeloma** | **High Risk*, Refractory** (Selective, Clinical Trials) | Not Standard Frontline |
| **Aplastic Anaemia** | **SAA/VSAA**, Young, Matched Donor | **1st Line if <40y + MSD** |
| **Inherited Disorders** | Thalassaemia, Sickle Cell, Fanconi, WAS, Immunodeficiencies | Early (Pre-Complications) |

> [!tip] **Age/Comorbidity Cut-offs Evolving**: **MAC ≤50-55y**, **RIC ≤65-70y**, **HCT-CI (Hematopoietic Cell Transplant-Comorbidity Index)** guides intensity.

## 👥 Donor Selection Hierarchy

| Priority | Donor Type | HLA Match | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|----------|------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **1st** | **Matched Sibling Donor (MSD)** | 10/10 or 12/12 | Best Outcomes, No GVHD Mismatch | Only ~25-30% have MSD |
| **2nd** | **Matched Unrelated Donor (MUD)** | 10/10 (12/12) | Large Registry Access | Slightly Higher GVHD, Longer Search |
| **3rd** | **Haploidentical (Half-Matched Family)** | 5/10 or 6/10 | **Immediate Availability**, **Post-Transplant Cyclophosphamide (PTCy)** GVHD Prophylaxis | Higher NRM Historically (Improving with PTCy) |
| **4th** | **Umbilical Cord Blood (UCB)** | 4-6/6 or 5-6/6 | **No Donor Risk**, **Lower GVHD** | **Slow Engraftment**, **Cell Dose Limited** (Weight Restriction) |

> [!tip] **Haploidentical + PTCy** now standard for many centres – **Outcomes Approaching MUD/MSD**.

## 🔥 Conditioning Intensity

| Category | Regimen Examples | Indications | Toxicity |
|----------|------------------|-------------|----------|
| **Myeloablative (MAC)** | **BuCy** (Busulfan + Cyclophosphamide), **CyTBI** (Cyclophosphamide + TBI), **FluBu** (Fludarabine + Busulfan) | **Young (<50-55), Fit, Low HCT-CI, High-Risk Disease** | High NRM, Mucositis, VOD, Infertility |
| **Reduced-Intensity (RIC)** | **FluMel** (Fludarabine + Melphalan), **FluBu Low-Dose**, **Treosulfan-Based** | **Older (55-70), Comorbidities (HCT-CI 1-3), Lower Disease Risk** | Lower NRM, Higher Relapse Risk |
| **Non-Myeloablative (NMA)** | **FluTBI (2-3 Gy)**, **FluCy Low-Dose** | **Elderly/Frail, High Comorbidity**, Indolent Disease | Lowest NRM, Highest Relapse, Strong GVT Required |

> [!warning] **Conditioning Choice**: Based on **Age, HCT-CI, Disease Risk, Donor Type**. **RIC Preferred for >55-60y or HCT-CI ≥3**.

## 🛡️ GVHD Prophylaxis

### Acute GVHD (aGVHD) Prophylaxis

| Regimen | Components | Used For |
|---------|------------|----------|
| **CNI + Methotrexate (MTX)** | **Tacrolimus (or Cyclosporine) + MTX** (Days 1, 3, 6, 11) | **Standard MAC (MSD/MUD)** |
| **CNI + MMF** | **Tacrolimus + Mycophenolate Mofetil** (Days 0-30) | **RIC/NMA**, **Haplo (with PTCy)** |
| **Post-Transplant Cyclophosphamide (PTCy)** | **Cyclophosphamide 50 mg/kg Days +3, +4** + **Tacrolimus + MMF** | **Haploidentical**, **Increasingly MUD** |
| **ATG / Alemtuzumab In Vivo** | **ATG (Days -3 to -1) or Alemtuzumab** | **MUD/RIC**, **Haplo** (Alternative to PTCy) |

### Chronic GVHD (cGVHD) Prophylaxis

| Strategy | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **CNI + MMF** | Continue Tacrolimus + MMF beyond Day +30 (Taper from Day +60-100) |
| **Sirolimus** | Alternative to CNI (Less Nephrotoxic, Different SE Profile) |
| **Extracorporeal Photopheresis (ECP)** | For Steroid-Refractory cGVHD (Prophylaxis Investigational) |

## 🩺 Acute GVHD (aGVHD)

### Grading (Modified Glucksberg / NIH Consensus)

| Grade | Skin | Liver (Bilirubin) | Gut (Diarrhoea Volume) |
|-------|------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **I** | Maculopapular Rash <25% BSA | – | – |
| **II** | Rash 25-50% BSA | **2-3 mg/dL** | **500-1000 mL/day** |
| **III** | Generalised Erythroderma | **3-6 mg/dL** | **1000-1500 mL/day** |
| **IV** | Generalised Erythroderma + Bullae/Desquamation | **>6 mg/dL** | **>1500 mL/day** |

**Overall Grade**: Highest Single Organ Grade

### Treatment

| Line | Treatment | Dose/Details |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| **1st Line** | **Methylprednisolone 2 mg/kg/day IV** | Taper over 4-8 weeks if Response |
| **2nd Line (Steroid-Refractory)** | **Ruxolitinib 10 mg BD** (REACH2/3 Trials) | **Preferred 2nd Line** |
| **3rd Line** | **Infliximab / Etanercept (Anti-TNF)**, **MMF**, **ETCP**, **Basiliximab**, **Alemtuzumab**, **Extracorporeal Photopheresis (ECP)** | |
| **Topical** | **Steroid Creams (Grade I Skin)**, **Budesonide/Beclomethasone (Gut)** | Adjunct |

## 🩺 Chronic GVHD (cGVHD)

### NIH Consensus Criteria (Simplified)

| Feature | Diagnostic | Distinctive |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **Skin** | Lichen Planus-like, Sclerotic, Poikiloderma, Depigmentation | Sclerotic (Fasciitis, Joint Contractures) |
| **Mouth** | Lichenoid, Ulcers, Xerostomia | Hypersalivation |
| **Eyes** | **Dry Eye Syndrome** (Keratoconjunctivitis Sicca) | Photophobia |
| **GI** | Oesophageal Web/Stricture, Anorexia, Nausea, Weight Loss | Sclerotic Oesophagitis |
| **Liver** | Cholestatic Pattern (↑ ALP, GGT) | – |
| **Lung** | **Bronchiolitis Obliterans Syndrome (BOS)** (FEV1 <75% Predicted) | **FEV1 <50% = Severe** |
| **Joints/Fascia** | **Sclerotic (Fasciitis, Joint Contractures)** | ROM Limitation |
| **Genital** | Lichen Planus, Stenosis, Ulcers | – |

### Treatment

| Line | Treatment | Details |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **1st Line** | **Prednisolone 1 mg/kg/day** (or Prednisone) + **Continue CNI** | Taper Slowly Over Months |
| **2nd Line (Steroid-Refractory/Dependent)** | **Ruxolitinib** (REACH3), **Ibrutinib** (420mg OD), **Belumosudil** (200mg OD - ROCK2 Inhibitor) | **Ruxolitinib = FDA Approved 1st 2nd Line** |
| **3rd Line** | **ECP**, **Rituximab**, **MMF**, **Tacrolimus Switch**, **Sirolimus**, **Axatilimab** (Anti-CSF1R) | |
| **Organ-Specific** | **BOS: Azithromycin + Montelukast + Inhaled Steroids**, **Ocular: Lubricants, Cyclosporine, Serum Tears**, **Sclerotic: PT/OT, UVA-1 Phototherapy** | |

## 🌲 Engraftment & Chimerism

| Milestone | Timing | Definition |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Neutrophil Engraftment** | **Day +14 to +21** | **ANC ≥0.5×10⁹/L for 3 Consecutive Days** |
| **Platelet Engraftment** | **Day +18 to +28** | **Plt ≥20×10⁹/L Untransfused for 7 Days** |
| **Full Donor Chimerism** | **Day +30 to +100** | **>95% Donor Cells** in PB/BM (Lineage-Specific) |
| **Mixed Chimerism** | **Day +30+** | **<95% Donor** → Risk of Relapse/Rejection → **DLI Consideration** |

---

## 🩸 Infections Post-SCT (Timeline)

| Period | Timeline | Key Pathogens | Prophylaxis |
|--------|----------|---------------|-------------|
| **Pre-Engraftment** | Day 0 to +30 | **Bacteria (Gram+/-), Candida, HSV/VZV** | **Fluoroquinolone, Posaconazole/Voriconazole, Aciclovir** |
| **Early Post-Engraftment** | Day +30 to +100 | **CMV, HHV-6, Adenovirus, Pneumocystis, Aspergillus** | **CMV Monitoring (q1-2wk PCR), PJP Prophylaxis (Co-trimoxazole), Aciclovir** |
| **Late Post-Engraftment** | >Day +100 | **Encapsulated Bacteria (S. pneumoniae, H. influenzae), VZV, Community Viruses** | **Vaccines (Inactivated**, **IVIG if IgG<4**), **Penicillin (If Splenectomy/HS)** |

> [!warning] **CMV Reactivation**: **Monitor Weekly PCR × 100 Days**; **Pre-emptive Therapy (Ganciclovir/Valganciclovir/Foscarnet/Leterimovir/Maribavir) at Threshold**.

---

## 🩸 Major Complications

| Complication | Timing | Key Features | Management |
|--------------|--------|--------------|------------|
| **VOD/SOS (Veno-Occlusive Disease)** | **Day +10 to +20** | **Weight Gain, Hepatomegaly, Ascites, Jaundice, RUQ Pain, Renal Impairment** | **Defibrotide 6.25 mg/kg q6h IV × 21d** (Definitive), Supportive |
| **Transplant-Associated TMA (TA-TMA)** | Day +30 to +100 | **MAHA (Schistocytes), Thrombocytopenia, Renal Dysfunction, HTN, Neurological** | **Eculizumab** (Complement Inhibition), **Plasma Exchange**, Discontinue CNI if Possible |
| **Haemorrhagic Cystitis** | **Day +10 to +30** | **BK Virus, Cyclophosphamide, CMV** | **Mesna (Prophylaxis with Cy), Hydration, Alkalinisation, Cidofovir (Refractory)** |
| **Engraftment Syndrome** | **Day +7 to +14** | **Fever, Rash, Non-Cardiogenic Pulm Oedema, Weight Gain** (Around Neutrophil Engraftment) | **Steroids (MP 1-2mg/kg)** |
| **Relapse** | Variable | **Molecular/Haematological** | **DLI**, **Second Transplant**, **Targeted Therapy**, **Clinical Trials** |

## 🔄 Donor Lymphocyte Infusion (DLI)

| Indication | Dose | Response |
|------------|------|----------|
| **Molecular Relapse (Rising Chimerism)** | **1×10⁶ CD3+/kg (Low Dose)** | **~60-70% CMR** (CML), Lower in AML |
| **Haematological Relapse** | **1×10⁷ - 1×10⁸ CD3+/kg** | Higher GVHD Risk |
| **Mixed Chimerism** | **Escalating Doses** (10⁵ → 10⁷ CD3+/kg) | **Goal: Full Donor Chimerism** |

**GVHD Prophylaxis for DLI**: **Taper CNI**, **Consider Low-Dose Steroids**, **Monitor Chimerism q1-2wk**

## ❓ Viva Questions

1. **What is the hierarchy of donor preference for Allogeneic SCT?**
   - **Matched Sibling > Matched Unrelated > Haploidentical (PTCy) > Cord Blood**

2. **What is the difference between MAC and RIC conditioning?**
   - **MAC**: High-dose (BuCy, CyTBI), Young/Fit, High Toxicity; **RIC**: Lower-dose (FluMel), Older/Comorbid, Less NRM, Higher Relapse Risk

3. **What is the standard GVHD prophylaxis for a MAC transplant from a Matched Sibling Donor?**
   - **Tacrolimus (or Cyclosporine) + Methotrexate (Days 1, 3, 6, 11)**

4. **How is Acute GVHD graded?**
   - **Skin (Rash BSA%), Liver (Bilirubin), Gut (Diarrhoea Volume)** → **Grade I-IV**; **Overall = Highest Single Organ Grade**

5. **What is the first-line and second-line treatment for Acute GVHD?**
   - **1st: Methylprednisolone 2 mg/kg/day IV**; **2nd: Ruxolitinib 10 mg BD**

6. **What are the NIH diagnostic criteria for Chronic GVHD?**
   - **Distinctive Features**: Sclerotic Skin, Sicca (Dry Eyes/Mouth), BOS (FEV1<75%), Fasciitis, Oesophageal Web/Stricture, Genital Involvement

7. **What is the treatment for Steroid-Refractory Chronic GVHD?**
   - **Ruxolitinib** (FDA Approved), **Ibrutinib**, **Belumosudil** (ROCK2 Inhibitor)

8. **When does Neutrophil and Platelet engraftment typically occur?**
   - **Neutrophil: Day +14-21 (ANC≥0.5×3d)**; **Platelet: Day +18-28 (Plt≥20 Untransfused×7d)**

9. **What is VOD/SOS and how is it treated?**
   - **Day +10-20**: Weight Gain, Hepatomegaly, Ascites, Jaundice, Renal Impairment → **Defibrotide 6.25 mg/kg q6h × 21 days**

10. **What is DLI and what are the indications?**
    - **Donor Lymphocyte Infusion**: **Molecular Relapse (Rising Chimerism) → Low Dose (10⁶ CD3+/kg)**; **Haematological Relapse → High Dose (10⁷-10⁸ CD3+/kg)**; **Mixed Chimerism → Escalating Doses**

## 🧠 Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **MAC vs RIC vs NMA** | **MAC = High Dose (Young/Fit)**; **RIC = Intermediate (Older/Comorbid)**; **NMA = Minimal (Elderly/Frail)** |
| **aGVHD vs cGVHD** | **aGVHD: <Day +100, Maculopapular, Cholestasis, Diarrhoea**; **cGVHD: >Day +100, Sclerotic, Sicca, BOS, Fasciitis** |
| **Grade I-IV aGVHD** | **I: Skin <25%**; **II: 25-50% or Bilirubin 2-3**; **III: Generalised or Bilirubin 3-6**; **IV: Bullae/Desquamation or Bilirubin >6** |
| **Steroids for aGVHD vs cGVHD** | **aGVHD: MP 2mg/kg IV**; **cGVHD: Pred 1mg/kg PO** |
| **Ruxolitinib** | **2nd Line Both aGVHD & cGVHD** (REACH2/3) |
| **DLI Dose** | **Molecular Relapse = Low (10⁶)**; **Haematological = High (10⁷-10⁸)** |

| Mnemonic | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **"MAC = Young, RIC = Old"** | Conditioning choice |
| **"aGVHD = <100d, cGVHD = >100d"** | Timing |
| **"TAC + MTX = Standard MAC Prophylaxis"** | Standard prophylaxis |
| **"Ruxo = 2nd Line Both aGVHD/cGVHD"** | Ruxolitinib |
