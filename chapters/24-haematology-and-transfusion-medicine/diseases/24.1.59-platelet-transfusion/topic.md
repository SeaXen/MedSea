# Platelet Transfusion

> [!info] **Davidson Ch 25 Alignment**: Transfusion Medicine → Platelet Transfusion
> **FCPS/MRCP Focus**: Prophylactic/therapeutic thresholds, ABO compatibility, HLA-matched for refractory, pediatric dosing, storage, complications, DDAVP for platelet dysfunction

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- [ ] Apply **Prophylactic Transfusion Thresholds**: **<10** (standard); **<20** (fever/sepsis/coagulopathy); **<50** (active bleeding/procedure/CNS)
- [ ] Know **Therapeutic Transfusion**: Active bleeding regardless of count
- [ ] Understand **ABO Compatibility**: Identical preferred; ABO-compatible acceptable; Rho(D) for females
- [ ] Manage **Refractory Thrombocytopenia**: **1-hour & 24-hour CCI**, **HLA-matched** (or crossmatched) platelets
- [ ] Apply **Pediatric Dosing**: **10-15 mL/kg** (≈ 1 unit/10kg)
- [ ] Understand **Storage**: **Room temperature (20-24°C) with agitation**, 5-7 day shelf life, bacterial risk
- [ ] Use **DDAVP** for platelet dysfunction (uraemia, VWD, drug-induced)
- [ ] Manage **Complications**: Febrile reactions, Allergic, TRALI, TACO, Bacterial sepsis, Refractoriness

## 📖 Indications & Thresholds

### Prophylactic Platelet Transfusion

| Clinical Scenario | Platelet Threshold (×10⁹/L) |
|-------------------|----------------------------|
| **Standard Prophylaxis** | **<10** (stable, no risk factors) |
| **Fever / Sepsis / DIC / Coagulopathy** | **<20** |
| **Active Bleeding (Any)** | **Transfuse regardless of count** |
| **Invasive Procedure / Surgery** | **<50** (most procedures); **<100** (Neurosurgery, Ocular, Major vascular) |
| **CNS Pathology / Bleeding** | **<100** |
| **Therapeutic Anticoagulation** | **<50** (consider holding anticoagulant) |

> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP**: **Prophylactic: <10 (standard), <20 (fever/sepsis/DIC), <50 (procedure/bleeding), <100 (CNS/neurosurgery/major vascular)**. **Therapeutic = Active bleeding regardless of count**. **1 Apheresis Unit ≈ 3×10¹¹ platelets = 20-40×10⁹/L rise in adult**.

## 🩸 Product Types & Dosing

| Product | Source | Platelet Yield | Volume | Equivalent |
|---------|--------|----------------|--------|------------|
| **Apheresis (Single Donor)** | **One donor** | **≥3.0×10¹¹** (**Standard Dose**) | ~200-400 mL | **1 Apheresis Unit = 1 "Six-Pack"** |
| **Pooled (Whole Blood Derived)** | **4-6 Donors** | **≥2.5-3.0×10¹¹** | ~300-400 mL | **4-6 Buffy Coats** |

### Dosing

| Population | Dose | Expected Increment |
|------------|------|-------------------|
| **Adult** | **1 Apheresis Unit (Standard Dose)** | **20-40 ×10⁹/L** (1 hour post) |
| **Paediatric** | **10-15 mL/kg** (≈ 1 unit/10 kg) | **50-100 ×10⁹/L** per 10 mL/kg |

> [!tip] **1 Apheresis Unit = ~3×10¹¹ platelets = "Six-Pack" equivalent**. **Expected 1h CCI = 10-25**; **24h CCI = 5-15**.

## 🩸 ABO & Rh Compatibility

| Priority | ABO Compatibility | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| **1st Choice** | **ABO Identical** | Best yield, lowest reactions |
| **2nd Choice** | **ABO Compatible (Major)** | **Platelets from Group O → Any recipient** (Anti-A/B in plasma diluted); **Group A → A/AB; Group B → B/AB; Group AB → AB only** |
| **3rd Choice** | **ABO Incompatible (Major)** | **Only if no compatible**; Risk of haemolysis (plasma anti-A/B); **Volume-reduce**; Monitor Hb |

### Rh Compatibility

| Recipient | Platelet Rh | Action |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Rh Negative Female (Childbearing)** | **Rh Negative Preferred** | Prevent Anti-D alloimmunisation; **If Rh Positive given → Anti-D Ig (250-500 IU)** |
| **Rh Positive / Male / Non-childbearing** | **Any Rh** | Acceptable |

> [!warning] **Platelets express low RhD**; **Rh Positive platelets to Rh Negative female → Anti-D risk** → **Give Anti-D Ig 250-500 IU** within 72h.

## 📊 Platelet Refractoriness

### Definition

| Criterion | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **Refractory** | **Failed 2 consecutive ABO-compatible transfusions** (1-hour or 24-hour CCI below threshold) |
| **1-hour CCI** | **<(7.5 ×10⁹/L) per (10¹¹ platelets transfused)** per L body weight |
| **24-hour CCI** | **<(4.5 ×10⁹/L) per (10¹¹ platelets transfused)** per L body weight |

**CCI Formula**: `CCI = (Post-Pre) × BSA (m²) / (Platelets transfused ×10¹¹)`

### Causes of Refractoriness

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Non-immune (Most Common ~80%)** | **Splenomegaly, Sepsis, DIC, Fever, Antibiotics, Bleeding, DIC, Amphotericin B, Heparin** |
| **Immune (~20%)** | **HLA Class I Antibodies (Anti-HLA-A/B)**, **HPA Antibodies (Anti-HPA-1a/1b/5b)**, **Autoimmune**, **Drug-dependent (Quinine, Vancomycin)** |

### Management of Refractoriness

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1. Exclude Non-immune** | Treat sepsis, DIC, fever, splenomegaly; Optimize timing |
| **2. HLA-matched Platelets** | **If immune suspected** (2 failed transfusions); **HLA-A, -B matched** (or crossmatch-compatible) |
| **3. Crossmatch-compatible** | **If no HLA-matched available**; Test donor platelets against patient serum |
| **4. HPA-matched** | **If HPA antibodies identified** (Anti-HPA-1a/1b/5b) |
| **5. Adjuncts** | **Tranexamic Acid** (fibrinolysis); **DDAVP** (uraemia); **TPO-RA** (chronic) |

## 🩸 Platelet Product & Storage

### Product Characteristics

| Parameter | Apheresis Platelets | Pooled Platelets |
|-----------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Platelet Count** | **≥3.0×10¹¹** | **≥2.5-3.0×10¹¹** |
| **Volume** | 200-400 mL | 300-400 mL |
| **Leucodepletion** | **Yes (Standard)** | **Yes (Standard)** |
| **Pathogen Reduction** | Optional (Intercept, Mirasol) | Optional |
| **Irradiation** | On demand (TA-GVHD) | On demand |

### Storage Conditions

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Temperature** | **20-24°C (Room Temperature)** |
| **Agitation** | **Continuous Gentle Agitation** (prevents clumping) |
| **Shelf Life** | **5-7 Days** (depending on bag/collection system) |
| **Bacterial Screening** | **Culture Day 1-2** or **Rapid test Day 1**, **Pathogen Reduction** optional |
| **pH Monitoring** | **>6.2** (bacterial growth indicator) |

> [!warning] **Platelets stored at Room Temperature → Highest bacterial risk**; **Max 4h out of incubator**; **Culture Day 1-2 or Rapid Test Day 1** mandatory in many jurisdictions.

## 💊 Adjunct Therapies

### Desmopressin (DDAVP)

| Indication | Dose | Mechanism |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Uraemic Platelet Dysfunction** | **0.3 µg/kg IV** (or 0.3 µg/kg SC / 300 µg IN) | **↑ vWF Release → ↑ Platelet Adhesion** |
| **VWD Type 1 & 2A/2M/2N** | Same | ↑ vWF Release |
| **Drug-induced (Aspirin/NSAIDs)** | Same | ↑ vWF (partial reversal) |
| **Contraindications** | **<2 years, VWD Type 2B, TTP/HUS, Coronary/Cerebrovascular disease** | Fluid retention, Hyponatraemia |

### Tranexamic Acid

| Indication | Dose | Mechanism |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Fibrinolysis (DIC, Trauma, Surgery)** | **1 g IV q6-8h** (± 15 mg/kg loading) | **Antifibrinolytic** (Inhibits Plasminogen Activation) |
| **Menorrhagia** | **1 g PO q6h × 4 days** | Reduces menstrual bleeding |

## 🔄 Complications

| Complication | Mechanism | Management |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| **Febrile Non-Haemolytic (FNHTR)** | Cytokines / Leukocyte antibodies | **STOP**, Antipyretics, Rule out haemolytic/sepsis |
| **Allergic / Anaphylactic** | Anti-plasma protein IgE | **STOP**, Adrenaline (anaphylaxis), Antihistamines, Steroids |
| **TRALI** | Anti-HLA/HNA in donor plasma | **STOP**, Supportive ventilation |
| **TACO** | Volume overload | **STOP**, Diuretics, Oxygen |
| **Bacterial Sepsis** | Contaminated unit (room temp) | **STOP**, Blood cultures, Broad-spectrum ABX, ICU |
| **Post-Transfusion Purpura (PTP)** | Anti-HPA antibodies | **IVIG 1g/kg × 2d**, Plasma exchange |
| **TA-GVHD** | Donor T-cells → Host | **FATAL** – **Prevent with IRRADIATION** |

## ❓ Viva Questions

1. **What are the prophylactic platelet transfusion thresholds?**
   - **<10** (Standard); **<20** (Fever/Sepsis/DIC); **<50** (Procedure/Active Bleed); **<100** (CNS/Neurosurgery)

2. **What is the expected platelet increment from 1 apheresis unit in an adult?**
   - **20-40 ×10⁹/L** (1 hour post-transfusion)

3. **How do you calculate Corrected Count Increment (CCI)?**
   - `CCI = (Post - Pre) × BSA (m²) / (Platelets transfused × 10¹¹)`

4. **What defines platelet refractoriness?**
   - **Failed 2 ABO-compatible transfusions** with **1h CCI <7.5** or **24h CCI <4.5**

5. **What is the management of platelet refractoriness?**
   - **Exclude non-immune causes** → **HLA-matched platelets** (or crossmatch-compatible) → **HPA-matched if HPA antibodies**

6. **What are the storage conditions for platelets?**
   - **20-24°C with continuous gentle agitation**, **5-7 day shelf life**, **Bacterial culture/rapid test**, **pH >6.2**

7. **When do you use DDAVP for platelet dysfunction?**
   - **0.3 µg/kg IV** for **Uraemia**, **VWD Type 1/2A/2M/2N**; **Contraindicated in Type 2B, TTP, HUS, CAD**

8. **What is the ABO compatibility rule for platelet transfusion?**
   - **Identical preferred**; **Compatible acceptable (Group O → Any, Anti-A/B diluted)**; **Incompatible only if no alternative (volume reduce, monitor Hb)**

9. **Why do Rh negative women of childbearing age need Rh negative platelets?**
   - **Platelets express low RhD**; **Anti-D alloimmunisation risk** → **Give Anti-D Ig 250-500 IU if Rh Positive platelets given**

10. **How does TACO differ from TRALI in platelet transfusion?**
    - **TACO = Cardiogenic (Volume overload, Hypertension, ↑ BNP, Diuretics help)**; **TRALI = Non-cardiogenic (Anti-HLA/HNA, Normotensive, Ventilation support)**

## 🧠 Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Platelet vs RBC Thresholds** | **Platelets: <10/20/50/100**; **RBCs: <70/80** |
| **Refractoriness CCI** | **1h <7.5, 24h <4.5** (per 10¹¹/L BSA) |
| **HLA vs HPA** | **HLA Class I (A, B)** most common; **HPA (HPA-1a/1b/5b)** less common |
| **DDAVP Contraindications** | **Type 2B, TTP, HUS, CAD, <2 years** |
| **Platelet vs RBC Storage** | **Platelets = Room Temp + Agitation**; **RBCs = 2-6°C** |

| Mnemonic | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **"<10 Standard, <20 Sick, <50 Procedure, <100 CNS"** | Prophylactic thresholds |
| **"1 Unit = 3×10¹¹ = 20-40 Rise"** | Dose calculation |
| **"CCI <7.5 = Refractory"** | 1-hour CCI threshold |
| **"HLA-matched = Immune Refractory"** | Management |
| **"DDAVP = 0.3µg/kg = Uraemia/VWD"** | DDAVP indication |
| **"Platelets = Room Temp, Agitation"** | Storage |
| **"TRALI Non-Cardio, TACO Cardio"** | Pulmonary reactions |
