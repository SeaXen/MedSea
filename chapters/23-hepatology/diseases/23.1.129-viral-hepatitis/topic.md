## 1. Hepatitis B

### Serology Interpretation (8 Key Combinations)

| HBsAg | anti-HBc IgG | anti-HBc IgM | anti-HBs | HBeAg | anti-HBe | HBV DNA | Interpretation |
|-------|--------------|--------------|----------|-------|----------|---------|----------------|
| + | + | + | – | + | – | High | **Acute HBV** (replicative) |
| + | + | – | – | + | – | High | **Chronic HBV, immune active (HBeAg+)** |
| + | + | – | – | – | + | Low/Int | **Chronic HBV, immune active (HBeAg–)** |
| + | + | – | – | – | + | <2000 IU/mL | **Inactive carrier** (HBeAg–) |
| + | + | – | – | + | – | High | **HBeAg+ chronic (immune tolerant)** |
| – | + | – | + | – | – | – | **Resolved infection** (immune) |
| – | – | – | + | – | – | – | **Vaccinated** (immune) |
| – | + | – | – | – | – | – | **Occult HBV** (anti-HBc alone) → check HBV DNA |

### Phases of Chronic HBV Infection

| Phase | HBeAg | ALT | HBV DNA | Histology | Management |
|-------|-------|-----|---------|-----------|------------|
| **Immune Tolerant** | + | Normal | Very high (>10⁷ IU/mL) | Minimal inflammation | **Monitor** (no treatment) |
| **Immune Active (HBeAg+)** | + | ↑↑ | High (>10⁷) | Active necroinflammation | **Treat** (ENT/TDF) |
| **Immune Active (HBeAg–)** | – | ↑↑ | ≥2000 IU/mL | Active necroinflammation | **Treat** (ENT/TDF) |
| **Inactive Carrier** | – | Normal | <2000 IU/mL | Minimal | **Monitor** (HCC surveillance if cirrhosis) |
| **Reactivation** | +/- | ↑↑ | ↑↑ | Active | **Treat urgently** (ENT/TDF) |

### Treatment Indications (EASL/APASL/NICE)

| Population | Treat If |
|------------|----------|
| **Immune active (HBeAg+)** | ALT >2x ULN + HBV DNA >20,000 IU/mL |
| **Immune active (HBeAg–)** | ALT >2x ULN + HBV DNA >2,000 IU/mL |
| **Cirrhosis (compensated)** | **Any detectable HBV DNA** (even <2,000) |
| **Decompensated cirrhosis** | **Any detectable HBV DNA** (urgent TDF/ENT) |
| **HCC (pre/post treatment)** | Any HBV DNA (TDF/ENT lifelong) |
| **Immunosuppression/chemo** | **Prophylaxis** if HBsAg+ or anti-HBc+ (anti-HBs+/-) |

### First-Line Nucleos(t)ide Analogues

| Drug | Dose | Resistance Barrier | Renal Adjustment | Notes |
|------|------|-------------------|------------------|-------|
| **Entecavir (ENT)** | 0.5 mg OD (1mg if decompensated/lamivudine-experienced) | **High** | eGFR <50: 0.25mg | Preferred for treatment-naïve |
| **Tenofovir disoproxil fumarate (TDF)** | 245 mg OD | **High** | eGFR <50: avoid / use TAF | Bone/renal toxicity |
| **Tenofovir alafenamide (TAF)** | 25 mg OD | **High** | Safer in CKD | Preferred if renal/bone risk |

> **Stopping rules**: HBeAg+ → 12 months after HBeAg seroconversion + undetectable DNA; HBeAg– → finite course NOT recommended (lifelong); **Cirrhosis = lifelong regardless of seroconversion**.

### HCC Surveillance in Chronic HBV

| Population | Surveillance Interval | Test |
|-------------|----------------------|------|
| **Cirrhosis (any aetiology)** | **6-monthly** | **US ± AFP** |
| **HBV (Asian males >40, females >50, African >20)** | **6-monthly** | US ± AFP |
| **HBV (family history HCC)** | **6-monthly** | US ± AFP |
| **Inactive carrier >40** | **12-monthly** | US ± AFP |

### HBV Reactivation Prophylaxis

| Risk Group | HBsAg+ | Anti-HBc+ (resolved) |
|------------|--------|----------------------|
| **High risk** (rituximab, stem cell transplant, high-dose steroids) | **ENT/TDF** (start before, continue 12mo after) | **ENT/TDF** (start before, continue 12mo after) |
| **Moderate risk** (TNF-α inhibitors, moderate chemo) | **ENT/TDF** | **Monitor ALT/HBV DNA q1-3mo** (treat if reactivation) |
| **Low risk** (conventional DMARDs, low-dose steroids) | Monitor | Monitor |

## 2. Hepatitis C

### Genotype-Based DAA Selection (Pan-Genotypic Preferred)

| Regimen | Genotypes | Duration | Cirrhosis | Renal Impairment (eGFR <30) | Retreatment |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|----------------------------|-------------|
| **SOF/VEL (Epclusa)** | **1-6** | 12 wks | +RIBAVIRIN 12 wks | **No** (SOF limited) | +RIBAVIRIN 24 wks |
| **GLE/PIB (Maviret)** | **1-6** | 8 wks (non-cirr) | 12 wks | **Yes** (safe) | 16 wks |
| **SOF/VEL/VOX (Vosevi)** | 1-6 | 12 wks | 12 wks | **No** | **Salvage** (post-DAA failure) |
| **SOF/VEL + RIBAVIRIN** | 1-6 | 12 wks | 12 wks | **No** | If PI-experienced |

> **Preferred 1L**: **GLE/PIB** (all genotypes, renal-safe, 8 wks non-cirrhotic) OR **SOF/VEL** (pan-genotypic, ribavirin if cirrhotic).

### Treatment Endpoints

| Endpoint | Definition | Clinical Significance |
|----------|------------|----------------------|
| **SVR12** | **HCV RNA undetectable at 12 weeks post-treatment** | **Cure** (>99% durable) |
| **SVR24** | HCV RNA undetectable at 24 weeks post-treatment | Historical standard (now SVR12 sufficient) |
| **Relapse** | HCV RNA detectable after EOT but was undetectable | Retreat with different class |
| **Non-response** | HCV RNA detectable at 4/12 weeks on treatment | Switch regimen / resistance testing |

### Post-SVR Follow-Up

| Population | Surveillance |
|------------|--------------|
| **Cirrhosis (pre-treatment)** | **Continue 6-monthly US ± AFP** (HCC risk persists) |
| **No cirrhosis** | Discharge (SVR = cure, minimal HCC risk) |

## 3. Hepatitis D (Delta)

| Feature | Co-infection | Superinfection |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| **Timing** | Simultaneous HBV + HDV | HDV on chronic HBV |
| **Course** | Acute self-limiting (90%) | Severe, rapid progression to cirrhosis |
| **HBsAg** | + | + |
| **Anti-HDV IgM** | + | + (high titre) |
| **Treatment** | IFN-α (poor response) | **Bulevirtide** (entry inhibitor) + PEG-IFN |
| **Outcome** | Usually clears | 70-80% progress to cirrhosis |

---

## 4. Hepatitis E

| Feature | Acute (Immunocompetent) | Chronic (Immunosuppressed) |
|---------|-------------------------|----------------------------|
| **Presentation** | Acute hepatitis, self-limiting | Chronic hepatitis, rapid fibrosis |
| **Diagnosis** | Anti-HEV IgM + HEV RNA | HEV RNA persistent >3 months |
| **Treatment** | Supportive | **Ribavirin 600-800mg/day ×3 months** (reduce immunosuppression) |
| **Mortality** | <1% (general), 10-20% (pregnant) | Low if treated |

## 3. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary

| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **HBV Serology** | 8 combinations; key: HBsAg+anti-HBcIgM = acute; HBsAg+anti-HBcIgG = chronic; anti-HBc alone = occult |
| **Phases** | Immune tolerant (HBeAg+, normal ALT, high DNA) → Immune active (HBeAg±, ALT↑) → Inactive carrier (HBeAg–, normal ALT, DNA <2000) |
| **Treat HBV if** | Immune active (ALT>2xULN+DNA threshold), Cirrhosis (any DNA), Decompensated (any DNA), HCC, Immunosuppression |

| **Reactivation** | HBsAg+: ENT/TDF prophylaxis; anti-HBc+: high risk=prophylaxis, moderate=monitor |
| **HCV 1L** | **GLE/PIB 8wks (non-cirr)** or **SOF/VEL 12wks (+RBV if cirr)**; **SVR12 = cure** |

| **HEV Chronic** | Immunosuppressed; **Ribavirin 600-800mg ×3mo** + reduce immunosuppression |

## 4. Viva Questions (MRCP PACES / FCPS)

| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| **HBV Serology — HBsAg+, anti-HBc IgG+, anti-HBs–, HBeAg+, HBV DNA high?** | **Chronic HBV, immune active (HBeAg+)**, high replicative, treat with ENT/TDF. |
| **HBV Phases — Immune tolerant vs immune active?** | **Tolerant**: HBeAg+, normal ALT, DNA >10⁷, no inflammation; **Active**: HBeAg±, ALT↑, DNA↑, necroinflammation. |
| **HBV Treatment Indication — 35M, HBsAg+, HBeAg+, ALT 120, DNA 10⁸?** | **Treat** (immune active: ALT>2xULN + DNA>20,000) → **ENT 0.5mg or TDF 245mg**. |
| **Inactive Carrier — Definition?** | HBeAg–, anti-HBe+, normal ALT, HBV DNA <2,000 IU/mL, minimal histology. |
| **Cirrhosis HBV — Any DNA detectable = treat?** | **Yes** → **TDF/ENT lifelong** regardless of HBeAg status or ALT. |
| **HBV Reactivation — Rituximab patient, HBsAg+?** | **ENT/TDF prophylaxis** (start before, continue 12mo after immunosuppression). |
| **HCV Genotype 1a, Cirrhosis, Naïve — DAA?** | **GLE/PIB 12wks** or **SOF/VEL + RBV 12wks** (non-ribavirin preferred if possible). |
| **HCV Genotype 3, Cirrhosis, Naïve — DAA?** | **GLE/PIB 12wks** (preferred) or **SOF/VEL + RBV 12wks**. |
| **HCV Treatment Failure — Which salvage?** | **SOF/VEL/VOX 12wks** (pangenotypic salvage for prior DAA failure). |
| **HDV Co-infection vs Superinfection?** | **Co-infection**: simultaneous HBV+HDV, self-limiting; **Superinfection**: HDV on chronic HBV, aggressive, cirrhosis. |
| **HEV Chronic — Immunosuppressed, persistent RNA >3mo?** | **Ribavirin 600-800mg/day ×3mo** + reduce immunosuppression. |

## 4. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **HBeAg+ vs HBeAg– chronic HBV** | HBeAg+ = high replicative, immune tolerant/active; HBeAg– = pre-core mutant, immune active (DNA ≥2000) or inactive carrier |
| **Inactive carrier vs immune active HBeAg–** | Inactive: DNA <2000 + normal ALT; Active: DNA ≥2000 + ALT>2xULN |
| **HBV DNA threshold for treatment** | HBeAg+: >20,000 IU/mL; HBeAg–: ≥2,000 IU/mL; Cirrhosis: **any detectable** |
| **SOF-based regimens in CKD** | SOF **avoid if eGFR<30**; GLE/PIB safe in all CKD stages |
| **SVR12 vs SVR24** | SVR12 = standard cure endpoint; SVR24 historical, no longer required |
| **HDV co-infection vs superinfection** | Co-infection = simultaneous, usually clears; Superinfection = HDV on chronic HBV, severe |
| **HEV chronic only in immunosuppressed** | Immunocompetent = acute self-limiting; immunocompromised (transplant/HIV) = chronic |

**Mnemonic: HBV-PHASES**
- **H**BeAg+ **Immune Tolerant**: Normal ALT, High DNA, Monitor
- **B**eAg+ **Immune Active**: ALT↑, DNA↑, Treat (ENT/TDF)
- **V**eAg– **Immune Active**: ALT↑, DNA≥2000, Treat (ENT/TDF)
- **P**hase **Inactive Carrier**: HBeAg–, Normal ALT, DNA<2000, Monitor
- **H**BV **Reactivation**: HBsAg+/anti-HBc+, Immunosuppression → Prophylax ENT/TDF
- **A**SIAN MALES >40, FEMALES >50, AFRICAN >20: **HCC Surveillance q6mo**
- **S**topping Rules: HBeAg+ → 12mo post-seroconversion; HBeAg– → Lifelong; Cirrhosis → Lifelong
