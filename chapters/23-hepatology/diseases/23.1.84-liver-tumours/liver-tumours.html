1 Learning Objectives

By the end of this note you should be able to:

  • [ ] Apply HCC surveillance criteria (US±AFP q6mo in cirrhosis)
  • [ ] Interpret LI-RADS categories (LR-1 to LR-5, LR-M, LR-TIV)
  • [ ] Apply BCLC Staging and select appropriate treatment
  • [ ] Select treatment: ablation, resection, transplant, TACE, systemic therapy
  • [ ] Workup liver metastases (primary unknown → IHC panel)
  • [ ] Distinguish benign liver tumours (haemangioma, FNH, adenoma)
1 Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC)
Surveillance (Who & How Often)
PopulationIntervalModality
--------------------------------
**Cirrhosis (all aetiologies)****6-monthly****US ± AFP**
**Chronic HBV (Asian M>40, F>50; African >20; Family history)****6-monthly**US ± AFP
**NASH F3 Fibrosis****12-monthly**US ± AFP
**Post-SVR HCV with cirrhosis****6-monthly**US ± AFP
AFP cut-off: >20 ng/mL raises suspicion; >200 ng/mL + mass = HCC diagnosis (with imaging)
2 LI-RADS (Liver Imaging Reporting and Data System)
CategoryDefinitionHCC ProbabilityManagement
---------------------------------------------------
**LR-1**Definitely benign<1%Routine surveillance
**LR-2**Probably benign<10%Routine surveillance
**LR-3**Intermediate10-50%Short-interval follow-up (3-6mo)
**LR-4**Probably HCC>50%Diagnostic; consider biopsy if uncertainty
**LR-5****Definitely HCC**>95%**Non-invasive diagnosis** (treat)
**LR-M**Probably malignant, not HCC specific—Biopsy / MDT
**LR-TIV**Tumour in vein—Stage IV (BCLC D)

3 BCLC Staging & Treatment Algorithm
BCLC StageCriteriaTreatment OptionsMedian Survival
----------------------------------------------------------
**0 (Very Early)**Single <2cm, Child-Pugh A, PS 0**Ablation** (RFA/MWA) > Resection > Transplant>5 years (>70%)
**A (Early)**Single ≤5cm or ≤3 nodules ≤3cm, Child-Pugh A-B, PS 0**Resection** (if preserved liver function) / **Transplant** (Milan criteria) / **Ablation**>5 years (50-70%)
**B (Intermediate)**Multinodular, Child-Pugh A-B, PS 0**TACE** (cTACE/DEB-TACE)20-30 months
**C (Advanced)**Vascular invasion / Extrahepatic spread / PS 1-2**Systemic Therapy** (Atezolizumab+Bevacizumab 1L; Sorafenib/Lenvatinib 2L)10-15 months
**D (Terminal)**Child-Pugh C, PS 3-4**Best Supportive Care**<3 months
Milan Criteria (Transplant Eligibility)
  • Single tumour ≤5cm OR ≤3 nodules each ≤3cm
  • No vascular invasion / extrahepatic spread
  • Child-Pugh A/B, PS 0
Up-to-Seven Criteria (Extended Transplant Criteria)
  • Number of tumours + largest tumour diameter (cm) ≤7
4 Treatment Modalities
ModalityIndicationKey Details
-----------------------------------
**Resection**Single tumour, Child-Pugh A, normal bilirubin, no portal hypertension (HVPG <10)5-yr OS 50-70%; recurrence 70% at 5yr
**Ablation (RFA/MWA)****BCLC 0/A** (≤3cm, ≤3 nodules), not resectableRFA <3cm; MWA larger; 5-yr OS 40-60%
**Liver Transplant****Milan Criteria** (or Up-to-Seven)5-yr OS 70-80%; recurrence <15%
**TACE (cTACE/DEB-TACE)****BCLC B** (multinodular, preserved liver)Repeat q4-12wks; median OS 20-30mo
**TARE (SIRT/Y-90)**BCLC B/C, bridge to transplant, portal vein thrombosisAlternative to TACE
**Systemic Therapy (BCLC C)****1L: Atezolizumab + Bevacizumab** (IMbrave150); **2L: Sorafenib / Lenvatinib**Atezolizumab/Bevacizumab: median OS 19.2mo
**Sorafenib**400mg BDFirst TKI; median OS 10.7mo
**Lenvatinib**12mg (BW≥60kg) / 8mg (BW<60kg)Non-inferior to sorafenib; median OS 13.6mo

4 Benign Liver Tumours
TumourFeaturesDiagnosisManagement
-----------------------------------------
**Haemangioma**Most common; **T2 hyperintense, flash fill** on MRI; peripheral nodular enhancementMRI (T2 bright, centripetal fill)**Observe** (asymptomatic); resect if giant >10cm + symptomatic
**FNH (Focal Nodular Hyperplasia)****Central scar, spoke-wheel arterial enhancement**, isointense on T1/T2; **Gadoxetate uptake** (hepatobiliary phase retention)MRI (central scar, hepatocyte-specific uptake)**Observe** (benign); resect if diagnostic uncertainty
**Hepatic Adenoma****Oral contraceptive association**; **Risk: haemorrhage, malignant transformation** (β-catenin mut); HNF1α, inflammatory, β-catenin subtypesMRI (fat content, arterial enhancement); biopsy if uncertain**Stop OCP**; **resect if >5cm, male, β-catenin mut, symptomatic**; surveillance if <5cm female
**Regenerative Nodules**Cirrhosis background; low T1, high T2MRI (low T1, high T2)Part of cirrhosis; not true tumour
4 FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary
TopicKey Points
-------------------
**HCC Surveillance****Cirrhosis q6mo US±AFP**; HBV high-risk groups (Asian M>40/F>50, African>20, family hx); AFP >200 + mass = HCC
**LI-RADS****LR-5 = Definite HCC (treat)**; LR-4 probable; LR-3 follow-up; LR-M = malignant non-HCC; LR-TIV = tumour in vein
**BCLC Staging****0/A: Curative** (ablate/resect/transplant); **B: TACE**; **C: Systemic (Atezo/Bev)**; **D: BSC**
**Milan Criteria**Single ≤5cm OR ≤3 nodules ≤3cm; no vascular invasion; transplant eligible
**BCLC 0/A Treatment****Ablation** (RFA/MWA <3cm) > **Resection** (Child A, no PH) > **Transplant** (Milan)
**BCLC B****TACE** (cTACE/DEB-TACE) q4-12wks
**BCLC C****Atezolizumab+Bevacizumab 1L**; Sorafenib/Lenvatinib 2L
**Metastases Workup**CT CAP, Endoscopy, IHC Panel (CK7/20, CDX2, TTF-1, etc.)
**Haemangioma**T2 bright, flash fill, observe
**FNH**Central scar, spoke-wheel enhancement, hepatobiliary retention, observe
**Adenoma**OCP association, haemorrhage risk, β-catenin = HCC risk; stop OCP, resect if >5cm/β-catenin mut
5 Viva Questions (MRCP PACES / FCPS)
QuestionExpected Answer
---------------------------
**HCC Surveillance — Who, How Often, Modality?****Cirrhosis q6mo US±AFP**; HBV high-risk (Asian M>40/F>50, African>20, family hx) q6mo.
**LI-RADS LR-5 vs LR-4 — Differences?****LR-5**: APHE + Washout + Capsule + Size≥10mm = **Definite HCC**; **LR-4**: APHE + (Washout or Capsule) + Size 10-19mm = **Probable HCC**.
**BCLC Staging — Treatment per Stage?****0/A: Ablation/Resection/Transplant (Curative); B: TACE; C: Systemic (Atezo/Bev); D: BSC**.
**Milan Criteria — Transplant Eligibility?****Single ≤5cm OR ≤3 nodules ≤3cm, no vascular invasion, no extrahepatic spread**.
**BCLC B — Treatment?****TACE** (cTACE/DEB-TACE) — repeat q4-12wks; median OS 20-30mo.
**BCLC C — 1L Systemic Therapy?****Atezolizumab + Bevacizumab** (IMbrave150); **Sorafenib/Lenvatinib 2L**.
**Metastases — Workup for Unknown Primary?****CT CAP + MRI Liver + Endoscopy + IHC Panel (CK7/20, CDX2, TTF-1, etc.)**.
**Haemangioma — MRI Features, Management?****T2 hyperintense (light bulb), flash fill, peripheral nodular enhancement**; **Observe if asymptomatic**.
**FNH — MRI Features, Central Scar?****Central scar, spoke-wheel arterial enhancement, hepatobiliary retention (Gadoxetate)**; **Observe**.
**Hepatic Adenoma — Risk Factors, Subtypes, Management?****OCP use**, **β-catenin mut = HCC risk**, **inflammatory, HNF1α**; **Stop OCP, resect if >5cm, male, β-catenin mut**.
6 Confusions & Mnemonics
ConfusionClarification
--------------------------
**LI-RADS LR-4 vs LR-5****LR-5**: APHE + Washout + Capsule (≥10mm); **LR-4**: APHE + (Washout or Capsule) 10-19mm; **LR-3**: APHE only or indeterminate
**BCLC A vs B**A: Single ≤5cm or ≤3 nodules ≤3cm (curative intent); B: Multinodular beyond Milan (TACE)
**TACE vs TARE****TACE**: Embolisation + chemo (cTACE/DEB); **TARE**: Y-90 radioembolisation (SIRT); TARE for PVT, bridge to transplant
**Ablation vs Resection****Ablation**: <3cm, not surgical candidate; **Resection**: Child A, no portal hypertension, preserved liver function
**Adenoma Subtypes****HNF1α**: steatotic, low HCC risk; **Inflammatory**: CRP↑, serum amyloid A; **β-catenin**: **High HCC risk** (resect); **Unclassified**
**LI-RADS LR-M****Malignant but not HCC** (cholangiocarcinoma, metastases); **LR-TIV**: Tumour in vein (portal/hepatic vein)
**AFP in HCC**>20 = suspicious; >200 + mass = diagnostic (with imaging); not sensitive enough alone for screening
**Up-to-Seven vs Milan**Milan: single ≤5cm or ≤3 nodules ≤3cm; Up-to-7: size + number ≤7 (expanded transplant criteria)

Mnemonic: HCC-LI-RADS-BCLC

  • HCC Surveillance: Cirrhosis q6mo US±AFP
  • CCR: Ablation/Resection/Transplant
  • LI-RADS: LR-5 Definite HCC, LR-4 Probable, LR-3 Follow-up, LR-M Non-HCC Malignant
  • Imaging: APHE + Washout + Capsule = LR-5
  • BCLC: 0/A Curative, B TACE, C Systemic (Atezo/Bev), D BSC
  • Curative: Ablation <3cm, Resection Child A, Transplant Milan
  • Liver Mets: CT CAP + Endoscopy + IHC (CK7/20, CDX2, TTF-1)
  • Benign: Haemangioma (T2 Bright), FNH (Central Scar + Retention), Adenoma (OCP, β-catenin = HCC Risk)
  • AFP: >200 + Mass = HCC Diagnosis
  • TACE: BCLC B Multinodular
  • Systemic: Atezo+Bev 1L, Sora/Lenv 2L
  • Resection: Child A, No PH, HVPG<10
  • Ablation: RFA/MWA <3cm
