## 📝 Exam Answer Templates

### Viva: "Explain the inheritance pattern and genetic testing strategy for a family with Huntington disease"
1. **Inheritance**: Autosomal Dominant, CAG repeat expansion in HTT (chr4), Anticipation (paternal > maternal)
2. **Diagnostic Testing**: Symptomatic patient — CAG repeat count (>36 = affected, 27-35 = intermediate, <27 = normal)
3. **Predictive Testing Protocol**: Pre-test counselling (3 sessions), Neurology/Psychiatry review, Informed consent, Result disclosure protocol (2 clinicians), Post-test support
4. **Family Implications**: At-risk relatives (50% risk), Cascade testing, Reproductive options (PGT-M, PND)
5. **Genetic Counselling**: Non-directive, Psychosocial support, Insurance/employment considerations (GINA/Equality Act)

### Viva: "How do you investigate a child with developmental delay and dysmorphic features?"
1. **First-tier**: **Chromosomal Microarray** (CNV, LOH, UPD) — 15-20% diagnostic yield
2. **If array negative**: **NGS Panel** (DD/ID panel) or **Whole Exome Sequencing** — 30-40% additional yield
3. **Specific phenotypes**: Fragile X (repeat-primed PCR), Angelman/Prader-Willi (methylation), Mitochondrial (mtDNA seq)
4. **Parental Testing**: Segregation analysis (de novo vs inherited) for VUS reclassification
5. **Re-analysis**: Periodic (1-2 years) for unsolved cases

### SAQ: "Calculate carrier frequency for cystic fibrosis if disease incidence = 1/2500"
- q² = 1/2500 = 0.0004 → q = 0.02
- p = 1 - q = 0.98
- Carrier freq (2pq) = 2 × 0.98 × 0.02 = **0.0392 = 1/25.5 ≈ 1 in 25**

### Viva: "Woman with family history of breast cancer — when to refer for BRCA testing?"
- **NICE CG164 / NHS GMS Criteria**: 
  - 1st-degree relative <40y, or 2+ relatives same side, or Triple-negative <60y, or Bilateral, or Male breast cancer, or Ovarian cancer, or Jewish ancestry
  - Manchester Score ≥15, CanRisk >10%
  - Refer to Clinical Genetics for pre-test counselling

## 🎓 Final Notes for Exam Success

1. **Draw Pedigrees**: Practice AD, AR, XLR, Mitochondrial — label affected/carrier/deceased
2. **Know Numbers**: H-W equilibrium, Recurrence risks (AD 50%, AR 25%, XLR 50% males), Population carrier frequencies (CF 1/25, Sickle 1/10 AA, Thal 1/20 SEA)
3. **Test Selection Logic**: Microarray 1st-tier DD/ID → NGS panel/WES → Specific tests (Fragile X, Methylation, mtDNA)
4. **Variant Language**: Pathogenic/Likely Pathogenic/VUS/Likely Benign/Benign — ACMG criteria
5. **Counselling Keywords**: Non-directive, Autonomy, Informed consent, Cascade testing, Predictive testing protocol
6. **Cancer Genetics**: Gene ↔ Syndrome ↔ Surveillance ↔ Targeted Therapy (PARPi for BRCA, Immunotherapy for MSI-H)
7. **Pharmacogenetics**: CPIC levels (A/B/C/D), NHS GMS commissioned tests (DPYD, TPMT, CYP2C19, CYP2D6)
8. **ELSI**: GINA (US), Equality Act (UK), Duty to warn vs Confidentiality, CRISPR germline moratorium
