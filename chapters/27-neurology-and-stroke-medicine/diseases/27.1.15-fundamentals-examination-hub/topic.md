## Key Algorithms & Scoring Systems (Memorise)

| System | Use Case | Key Thresholds |
|--------|----------|----------------|
| **GCS** | Coma severity | ≤8 = severe, 9-12 = moderate, 13-15 = mild |
| **FOUR Score** | Coma (intubated) | Eye, Motor, Brainstem, Respiration (0-4 each) |
| **NIHSS** | Stroke severity | ≥16 = severe, 5-15 = moderate, <5 = mild |
| **mRS** | Functional outcome | 0-2 = good, 3-5 = disability, 6 = death |
| **MRC Power** | Motor strength | 0-5 (5 = normal) |
| **ASIA Scale** | Spinal cord injury | A (complete) to E (normal) |
| **Hunt & Hess** | SAH grade | I-V (V = moribund) |
| **Fisher Scale** | SAH CT blood | 1-4 (predicts vasospasm) |
