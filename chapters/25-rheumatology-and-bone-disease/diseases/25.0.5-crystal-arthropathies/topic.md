## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **Gout**: podagra, tophi, MSU crystals (negatively birefringent, needle-shaped), acute = NSAID/colchicine/steroids, ULT = allopurinol/febuxostat, target urate <360 µmol/L
- **Pseudogout (CPPD)**: knee > wrist, chondrocalcinosis on X-ray, rhomboid crystals (weakly positively birefringent), associated with haemochromatosis, hyperparathyroidism, hypomagnesaemia
- **Basic calcium phosphate**: Milwaukee shoulder, calcific tendonitis, hydroxyapatite crystals
- **Crystal identification**: MSU = negatively birefringent needles; CPPD = positively birefringent rhomboids
- **Gout ULT**: start 2-4 weeks after flare; colchicine prophylaxis 6 months; target serum urate <360 µmol/L (<300 if tophi); start low/go slow
