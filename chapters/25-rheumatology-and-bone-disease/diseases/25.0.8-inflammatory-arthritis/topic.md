## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **RA**: 2010 ACR/EULAR criteria, RF/anti-CCP, DMARD step-up, treat-to-target, window of opportunity
- **Spondyloarthritis**: ASAS criteria, HLA-B27, sacroiliitis on MRI/X-ray, peripheral/axial domains
- **Psoriatic Arthritis**: CASPAR criteria, 6 domains (joints, skin, nail, dactylitis, enthesis, spine)
- **Reactive arthritis**: post-GU/GI, triad (arthritis, conjunctivitis, urethritis), HLA-B27
- **Enteropathic**: IBD-associated, treat inflammation → joints improve
- **Undifferentiated**: features not meeting specific criteria
- **JIA**: ILAR classification (systemic, oligoarticular, polyarticular RF+/-)
