## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **Regional soft tissue**: bursitis (trochanteric, prepatellar, olecranon), tendinopathy (rotator cuff, tennis/golfer's elbow, Achilles), trigger finger, plantar fasciitis
- **Fibromyalgia**: ACR 2010/2016 criteria (WPI ≥7 + SSS ≥5 or WPI 4-6 + SSS ≥9); chronic widespread pain ≥3 months; tender points replaced by symptom severity; central sensitisation; treat with CBT, graded exercise, duloxetine/pregabalin
- **Chronic widespread pain**: central sensitisation, central pain amplification, multidisciplinary (CBT, exercise, pharmacotherapy)
- **CRPS**: Budapest criteria (sensory, vasomotor, sudomotor/edema, motor/trophic); type I (no nerve injury) vs II (nerve injury); early PT + bisphosphonates + neuropathic pain agents
- **Regional disorders**: rotator cuff tear (painful arc, drop arm), frozen shoulder (global restriction active+passive), tennis/golfer's elbow (epicondylitis), trochanteric bursitis (lateral hip pain), Baker's cyst (popliteal swelling), plantar fasciitis (heel pain worst morning)
