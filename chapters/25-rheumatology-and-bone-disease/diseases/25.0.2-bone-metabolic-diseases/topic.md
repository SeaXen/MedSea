## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **Osteoporosis**: DEXA T-score ≤-2.5; FRAX ≥10% major/≥3% hip = treat; bisphosphonates (oral/IV), denosumab, romosozumab, teriparatide; monitor adherence, dental
- **Osteomalacia/rickets**: vitamin D deficiency → low Ca, low PO4, high ALP, high PTH; Looser zones (pseudofractures); treat vitamin D
- **Paget's**: high ALP (disproportionate), mosaic bone, "cotton wool" skull, bowed bones; bisphosphonates (zoledronic acid)
- **GIOP**: bone loss within 3-6 months on >7.5mg pred; DEXA at start; calcium/vit D + bisphosphonate; FRAX adjusted for steroids
- **Renal osteodystrophy**: CKD-MBD; low Ca, high PO4, high PTH, high FGF23; low turnover (adynamic) vs high turnover; phosphate binders, calcimimetics, vitamin D analogues
- **Hyperparathyroidism**: primary = high Ca, high PTH; bone pain, stones, abdominal groans, psychiatric moans; surgery if symptomatic
