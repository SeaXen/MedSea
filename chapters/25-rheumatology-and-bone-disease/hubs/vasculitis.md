## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **GCA**: age >50, new headache, jaw claudication, scalp tenderness, ESR/CRP >> normal, temporal artery biopsy, high-dose steroids immediately (prevent blindness), PMR overlap
- **ANCA vasculitides**: c-ANCA/PR3 = GPA (ENT, lung, kidney); p-ANCA/MPO = MPA (renal, lung) or EGPA (asthma, eosinophilia, neuropathy)
- **EGPA** (Churg-Strauss): asthma + eosinophilia + vasculitis; ANCA+ in 40-60%; cardiac involvement bad prognosis
- **PAN**: medium vessel, no ANCA, renal artery aneurysms, mononeuritis multiplex; hepatitis B association
- **Kawasaki**: children <5, fever 5d + 4/5 criteria (conjunctivitis, rash, cervical LN, mucositis, extremities), coronary aneurysms, IVIG + aspirin
- **IgA vasculitis (HSP)**: children, palpable purpura (gravity-dep), abdominal pain, arthralgia, renal (IgA nephropathy)
- **Behçet's**: oral/genital ulcers + uveitis + skin + pathergy; HLA-B51; vascular (aneurysm, thrombosis), neuro
- **Secondary**: SLE, RA, Sjögren's, hepatitis C (cryoglobulins), drugs
