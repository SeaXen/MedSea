## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **History**: inflammatory vs mechanical pain, morning stiffness duration, distribution patterns
- **GALS**: screening + focused exam (Look, Feel, Move)
- **Investigations**: autoantibody interpretation (RF, anti-CCP, ANA, ANCA, HLA-B27), DEXA, X-ray features
- **Drugs**: csDMARDs (MTX, HCQ, SSZ, LEF), biologics, JAKi, steroids, bone protection
