## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **PMR**: age >50, bilateral shoulder/pelvic girdle pain/stiffness >45 min morning, ESR/CRP >> normal, rapid steroid response (15-20mg pred), **exclude GCA**
- **GCA** (temporal arteritis): age >50, new headache, jaw claudication, scalp tenderness, visual symptoms (amaurosis fugax, diplopia), temporal artery prominence/nodule, biopsy gold standard (skip lesions), **high-dose steroids urgently to prevent blindness**, PMR overlap 40-50%
- **PMR vs GCA**: 10-20% PMR have GCA; 40-50% GCA have PMR; always screen for GCA in PMR
- **Other granulomatous**: sarcoidosis (bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy), IgG4-related disease
