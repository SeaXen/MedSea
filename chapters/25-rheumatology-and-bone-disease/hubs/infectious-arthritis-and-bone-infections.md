## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **Septic arthritis**: emergency; monoarthritis + fever + high WBC/crystals negative; aspirate before antibiotics; <24h joint destruction; Staph aureus #1; gonococcal in young sexually active
- **Rheumatic fever**: Jones criteria (major: carditis, arthritis, chorea, erythema marginatum, subcutaneous nodules; minor: fever, arthralgia, high ESR/CRP, prolonged PR); post-strep; penicillin prophylaxis
- **Osteomyelitis**: acute (Staph aureus), chronic (draining sinus), diabetic foot; MRI gold standard; prolonged antibiotics ± surgery
- **Lyme disease**: erythema migrans, Bell's palsy, migratory arthritis; Borrelia burgdorferi; doxycycline/ceftriaxone
- **TB arthritis**: Pott's spine, monoarthritis; AFB culture/PCR; anti-TB therapy
- **Viral arthritis**: parvovirus B19, hepatitis B/C, rubella, alphavirus; self-limiting
