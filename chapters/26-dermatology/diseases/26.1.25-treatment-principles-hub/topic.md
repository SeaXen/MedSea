## High-Yield Summary

| Treatment | Key Points | Monitoring |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| **Topical vehicles** | Ointment > Cream > Lotion > Gel > Solution (potency & occlusion) | Choice by site, hair, patient preference |
| **Steroid potency (Vedamurthy)** | Group I (superpotent: clobetasol) → VII (mild: hydrocortisone 1%) | Face/flexures: Group V-VII max 2w; Trunk/limbs: Group III-IV |
| **Fingertip Unit (FTU)** | 1 FTU = 0.5g = 2% BSA = 2 adult palms | Adult: Face 1, Hand 1, Foot 2, Arm 3, Leg 6, Trunk F/B 7 each |
| **Calcineurin inhibitors** | Tacrolimus 0.1%/0.03%, Pimecrolimus 1% | No atrophy, OK face/flexures, burning sensation |
| **Vitamin D analogues** | Calcipotriol, calcitriol, tacalcitol | Synergy with TCS, avoid face, max 100g/week |
| **Systemic conventional** | MTX (folate), Ciclosporin (renal/BP), Acitretin (teratogenic, lipids), Azathioprine (TPMT), MMF, Hydroxychloroquine | FBC, LFT, U&E, lipids, pregnancy test |
| **Phototherapy** | NB-UVB 311nm (1st line), PUVA (UVA+psoralen) | NB-UVB: 3x/week, incremental; PUVA: 2x/week, cataract risk |
| **Biologics - Anti-TNF** | Adalimumab, Etanercept, Infliximab, Certolizumab (pregnancy) | TB screen, Hep B/C, demyelination, heart failure |
| **Biologics - IL-17** | Secukinumab, Ixekizumab, Brodalumab | IBD flare risk, candidiasis |
| **Biologics - IL-23** | Guselkumab, Risankizumab, Tildrakizumab | TB screen, infections |
| **JAK Inhibitors** | Baricitinib, Upadacitinib, Abrocitinib, Ruxolitinib (topical) | CBC, LFT, lipids, VZV, thrombosis risk, pregnancy avoid |
| **Surgery** | Elliptical excision, punch biopsy, shave, curettage, Mohs | Margins: BCC 4mm, SCC 4-6mm, Melanoma Breslow-based |

## Key Algorithms

### Topical Steroid Selection
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Prescribe Topical Steroid] --> B{Site}
    B -->|Face/Neck/Flexures/Genital| C[Mild-Mod: Group V-VI (HC 1%, Eumovate)]
    C --> D[Max 2 weeks continuous]
    B -->|Scalp/Hair-bearing| E[Lotion/Mousse/Gel: Group III-IV]
    B -->|Trunk/Limbs (Adult)| F[Potent: Group III-IV (Betnovate, Elocon)]
    B -->|Palms/Soles/Thick Skin| G[Very Potent: Group I-II (Dermovate, Nerisone Forte)]
    B -->|Child <12| H[Mild-Mod only, avoid potent/very potent]
    H --> I[Max 1-2 weeks]
```

### Biologic Selection Algorithm (Psoriasis)
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Psoriasis - Biologic Indicated] --> B{Comorbidities}
    B -->|IBD| C[IL-23 (guselkumab/risankizumab) OR IL-17 (secukinumab - CAUTION IBD flare)]
    B -->|Psoriatic Arthritis| D[Anti-TNF (adalimumab) OR IL-17/23]
    B -->|Recurrent Infections| E[IL-23 preferred (lower infection risk)]
    B -->|Pregnancy Planning| F[Certolizumab (no Fc) OR ciclosporin short-term]
    B -->|No Specific Contraindication| G[IL-23 (convenience q8-12w) OR IL-17 (speed) OR Anti-TNF]
```

## FCPS/MRCP Viva Topics

1. **Topical steroid potency ladder** - 7 groups, examples, body site adjustments, duration limits
2. **FTU calculation** - 1 FTU = 0.5g = 2% BSA; adult body site FTUs
3. **Calcineurin inhibitors** - tacrolimus/pimecrolimus, no atrophy, face/flexures, burning, black box (theoretical lymphoma)
4. **Phototherapy** - NB-UVB vs PUVA, indications, protocols, contraindications (photosensitising drugs, XP), cataract risk (PUVA)
5. **Biologic sequencing** - TNFα → IL-17 → IL-23 → JAKi; screening (TB IGRA/CXR, Hep B/C, HIV, VZV, malignancy)
6. **Anti-TNF in pregnancy** - certolizumab (no Fc) only safe throughout; others stop 2nd/3rd trimester
7. **Methotrexate monitoring** - FBC, LFT, U&E q1-3m; folate 5mg/week; pneumonitis, fibrosis risk
8. **Ciclosporin monitoring** - Renal, BP, lipids, Mg, uric acid, drug interactions (CYP3A4), max 1-2 years
9. **Acitretin** - Teratogenic (cat X), 3-year washout, lipids, LFT, mucocutaneous toxicity
10. **JAK inhibitors** - VZV screening, CBC/LFT/lipids, thrombosis risk, avoid pregnancy

## Mnemonics

- **Steroid potency groups:** `SCARY POTENT` = **S**uperpotent (I: Clobetasol), **C**lobetasone (II: Eumovate), **A**lclometasone (III: Mod), **R**egular (IV: Betnovate), **Y**... **P**otent **O**ver **T**ime **E**nds **N**o **T**opical - Better: `I-VII`: I=Clobetasol, II=Betamethasone dipropionate, III=Betamethasone valerate, IV=Mometasone, V=Clobetasone, VI=HC butyrate, VII=HC 1%
- **FTU Adult:** `FACE HAND FOOT ARM LEG TRUNK` = **1, 1, 2, 3, 6, 7+7** (front/back)
- **Biologic Screening:** `TB HEP HIV VZV CANCER` = **TB** (IGRA/CXR), **HEP** B/C, **HIV**, **VZV** IgG, **CANCER** history
- **Pre-biologic vaccines:** `LIVE DEAD` = **LIVE** vaccines (MMR, VZV, Yellow fever) → give ≥4w before; **DEAD** (inactivated) → OK anytime
