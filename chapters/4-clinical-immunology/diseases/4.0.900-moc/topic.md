## 📝 Exam Answer Templates

### Viva: "Describe the immune response to intracellular vs extracellular pathogens"
1. **Intracellular** (Viruses, Mycobacteria, Listeria): CD8+ CTL (MHC I), Th1 (IFN-γ → Macrophage activation), NK cells, CD4+ Th1
2. **Extracellular** (Bacteria, Fungi, Helminths): Antibody (IgG opsonisation, IgA mucosal), Complement, Th2 (Eosinophils/IgE), Th17 (Neutrophils), Neutrophils
3. **Key Cytokines**: IL-12/IFN-γ (Intracellular), IL-4/IL-5/IL-13 (Helminths/Allergy), IL-17/IL-22 (Extracellular bacteria/fungi)

### Viva: "Classify Primary Immunodeficiencies (IUIS 2022)"
1. **Immunodeficiencies affecting cellular and humoral immunity** (CID/SCID)
2. **Combined immunodeficiencies with associated/syndromic features** (AT, NBS, WAS)
3. **Predominantly antibody deficiencies** (XLA, CVID, Hyper-IgM, IgA deficiency)
4. **Diseases of immune dysregulation** (IPEX, ALPS, CTLA4, LRBA)
5. **Congenital defects of phagocyte number/function** (CGD, LAD, CHS)
5. **Defects in innate immunity** (MSMD, TLR pathways, CARD9)
6. **Autoinflammatory disorders** (FMF, CAPS, TRAPS, MKD)
7. **Complement deficiencies** (Classical, Alternative, Terminal, Regulatory)
7. **Phenocopies of inborn errors of immunity** (Autoantibodies to cytokines)

### Viva: "SLE Classification & Management"
**SLICC 2012 / EULAR 2019 Criteria**: ≥4 criteria (Clinical + Immunological)
**Management**: Hydroxychloroquine (all), NSAIDs, Steroids (flare), Immunosuppressants (MMF, AZA, CYC for nephritis/CNS), Belimumab, Anifrolumab, Voclosporin
**Monitoring**: dsDNA, C3/C4, Urinalysis, BP, Lipids, Bone density, Ophthalmology (HCQ)

### Viva: "Anaphylaxis Algorithm"
1. **Recognise** (Airway, Breathing, Circulation, Disability, Exposure)
2. **Adrenaline IM** 0.5mg (1:1000) anterolateral thigh — **Repeat q5min**
3. **Position** — Supine, legs raised (if not breathless)
4. **Oxygen**, IV fluids (Crystalloid bolus)
5. **Adjuncts** — Chlorphenamine, Hydrocortisone, Salbutamol (if bronchospasm)
6. **Observe** 6-12h (Biphasic risk), Mast cell tryptase (1-2h, 24h), Referral
