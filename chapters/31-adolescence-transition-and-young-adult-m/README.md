## Key Themes for FCPS/MRCP

- **HEEADSSS assessment** - Home, Education, Activities, Drugs, Sexuality, Suicide/depression, Safety (+ Eating, Sleep, Screen)
- **Gillick competence / Fraser guidelines** - under-16 consent and confidentiality
- **Tanner staging** - B1-B5 (breast), G1-G5 (genital), PH1-PH5 (pubic hair); testicular volume >=4 mL = first sign in boys
- **Transition of care** - Ready Steady Go, STARx, NICE NG43, Got Transition 6 core elements
- **Eating disorders** - anorexia (F:M 10:1), bulimia; FBT/Maudsley first-line for adolescents
- **Substance use** - CRAFFT screening, motivational interviewing, harm reduction
- **Mental health** - depression, self-harm/suicide, anxiety, OCD, PTSD, bipolar; SSRI fluoxetine first-line; FDA black-box warning
- **LARC contraception** - IUS, IUD, implant first-line for adolescents
- **STIs** - chlamydia commonest, annual screening <25, doxycycline first-line
- **Safeguarding** - under-13 sexual activity mandates referral; multi-agency
- **CFS/ME** - PEM hallmark, multi-component management, multidisciplinary team
- **Testicular torsion** - 6-hr salvage window, surgical emergency
- **ADHD** - 5% adolescents; methylphenidate first-line; comorbidity
- **FGM** - mandatory reporting <18; deinfibulation; safeguarding
- **DSD** - multidisciplinary team; sex assignment deferred where possible
- **Tic disorders / Tourette** - 1% prevalence; CBIT; clonidine, risperidone for severe
- **Conduct disorder** - 3-5%; parent management training; comorbidity with ADHD
