# Gaming disorder and internet addiction

---

## Definition

Gaming disorder (ICD-11, 2018) is characterised by a pattern of persistent or recurrent gaming behaviour (digital or video gaming) of sufficient severity to result in significant impairment in personal, family, social, educational, occupational or other important areas of functioning, typically evident over at least 12 months. Internet addiction (broader concept) is not in ICD-11 but includes compulsive internet use affecting school, social and family life. Prevalence: 1-3% adolescents; M:F 3:1; peak 12-18 yrs. Often comorbid with ADHD, anxiety, depression, ASD. Management: CBT (often family-based), motivational interviewing, screen-time contracts, school reintegration, treat comorbidities. Consider digital detox. Prognosis: variable; responds to early intervention.
