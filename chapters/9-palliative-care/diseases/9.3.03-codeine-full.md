> Davidson 24e Ch 8 (Colvin, Fallon) — **Opioid Analgesics**

## 1. 1. Definition

Codeine is a weak opioid (μ-receptor agonist prodrug) metabolised by CYP2D6 to morphine (10% of dose). Used for mild-moderate pain (WHO step 2). Dose: 30-60mg q4h PO (max 240mg/day). Variable efficacy due to CYP2D6 polymorphism: poor metabolisers (5-10%) have no analgesia; ultrarapid metabolisers (1-2% European, 10% North African) may have toxic morphine levels (respiratory depression, death — FDA boxed warning in children, lactation). Often combined with paracetamol (co-codamol 8/500, 30/500).

## 2. 2. Clinical Features and Presentation

Codeine is a weak opioid (WHO step 2) for mild-moderate pain. Prodrug — metabolised by CYP2D6 to morphine (5-10% by O-demethylation). Equianalgesia: codeine 60mg PO = morphine 6mg PO (10:1). Side effects: constipation, N&V, sedation. CYP2D6 polymorphism: poor metabolisers (5-10% Caucasians) — no analgesia; ultra-rapid metabolisers (5% Caucasian, higher in Middle East) — morphine toxicity, life-threatening in children. Limited by ceiling effect; combination with paracetamol (co-codamol 8/500, 30/500).


## 3. 3. Investigation and Management Summary

### 1. Investigations
Pain assessment. No routine monitoring. CYP2D6 genotype not routinely tested but consider in poor/ultra-rapid metabolisers or unexplained response.

### 2. Management
Co-codamol 30/500 (codeine 30mg + paracetamol 500mg) 1-2 tabs q6h. Maximum 8 tablets in 24h (limit paracetamol). Avoid codeine in children <12 (breathing problems), post-tonsillectomy, lactating (morphine in breast milk), renal failure. Use with caution in elderly. Switch to morphine if inadequate response.

## 6. 6. Viva Questions

1. 1. Q: Codeine mechanism? A: Prodrug metabolised by CYP2D6 to morphine (~10% of dose).

2. 2. Q: CYP2D6 polymorphism? A: Poor metabolisers (5-10% Caucasians) — no analgesia; ultra-rapid metabolisers (5%) — morphine toxicity.

3. 3. Q: Why avoid in children? A: Risk of life-threatening respiratory depression (especially ultra-rapid metabolisers).

4. 4. Q: Co-codamol max dose? A: 8 tablets/24h (limits paracetamol 4g).

5. 5. Q: Equianalgesia codeine:morphine? A: 10:1 (60mg codeine = 6mg morphine).
