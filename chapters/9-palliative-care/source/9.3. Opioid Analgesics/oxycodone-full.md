# Oxycodone

Related: [[../../Pain and Palliative Care MOC]], [[../../Davidson Chapter 8 - Pain and Palliative Care Hierarchy]]

> Davidson 24e Ch 8 (Colvin, Fallon) — **Opioid Analgesics**

## 1. Definition

Oxycodone is a strong opioid (μ- and κ-receptor agonist) for moderate-severe cancer pain. Alternative to morphine. Available: IR (Oxynorm), MR (OxyContin). Dose: 5mg q4-6h IR (naïve); MR 10mg BD. Equianalgesia: oral oxycodone 20mg = oral morphine 30mg (1.5:1). Less histamine release than morphine (less pruritus). Also available combined with naloxone (Targinact) to reduce opioid-induced constipation (naloxone local gut action, not absorbed).

## 2. Clinical Features and Presentation

Oxycodone is a strong opioid (WHO step 3) for moderate-severe cancer pain. Semi-synthetic, dual μ-agonist and κ-agonist. Bioavailability 60-87% (higher than morphine). Available: IR (Oxynorm 5/10/20mg, capsule), MR (Oxycontin 5/10/20/40/80/120mg, 12-hourly). Side effects: similar to morphine — constipation, N&V, sedation, respiratory depression, pruritus; less histamine (less pruritus). Metabolites: oxymorphone (active, renally excreted), noroxycodone. Caution in renal/hepatic impairment. Potency: oral oxycodone 1.5-2x oral morphine.


## 3. Investigation and Management Summary

### Investigations
Pain assessment, renal function (active metabolites), liver function, sedation, bowel function.

### Management
Start: opioid-naïve 5mg q4h IR or 10mg 12-hourly MR. Titrate by 25-50%. Equianalgesia: oxycodone PO 20mg = morphine PO 30mg (1:1.5). Often used as first-line alternative to morphine (less histamine, fewer hallucinations). Available in IR + MR combination for breakthrough.

## 6. Viva Questions

1. 1. Q: Oxycodone vs morphine potency? A: Oxycodone ~1.5-2x more potent (PO 20mg = PO morphine 30mg).

2. 2. Q: Why prefer over morphine? A: Less histamine (less pruritus), less neuroexcitatory metabolites, dual μ + κ.

3. 3. Q: Bioavailability? A: 60-87% (higher than morphine ~30%).

4. 4. Q: Active metabolite? A: Oxymorphone.

5. 5. Q: Can convert directly to morphine? A: Yes, 1.5:1 (10mg oxycodone = 15mg morphine).
