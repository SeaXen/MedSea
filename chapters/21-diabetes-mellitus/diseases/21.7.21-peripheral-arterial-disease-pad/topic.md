## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Screening** | ABI <0.9 diagnostic · TBI if calcified vessels (ABI >1.4) · Screen if >50 or symptoms (claudication, rest pain) |
| **Classification** | Asymptomatic · Claudication (Rutherford 1-3) · Critical limb ischaemia (Rutherford 4-6) |
| **Critical Limb Ischaemia** | Rest pain >2wk, tissue loss (ulcer/gangrene), ABI <0.4, TcPO₂ <30mmHg · Urgent vascular referral |
| **Management** | Antiplatelet, statin, exercise therapy · Endovascular 1st → Surgical bypass · Amputation if unreconstructible |
