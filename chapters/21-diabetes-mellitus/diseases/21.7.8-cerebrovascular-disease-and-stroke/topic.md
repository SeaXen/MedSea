## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Stroke Risk** | Diabetes → 2-4x ischaemic stroke risk · Lacunar infarcts common · Haemorrhagic stroke also increased |
| **Risk Factors** | HTN (major), dyslipidaemia, AF (CHA₂DS₂-VASc: DM = 1 point), carotid stenosis |
| **Prevention** | BP control <130/80 · High-intensity statin · Anticoagulation if AF (DOAC preferred) · Antiplatelet if no AF (aspirin/clopidogrel) |
| **Acute Stroke** | Thrombolysis (alteplase) if <4.5h · Thrombectomy if LVO <24h · Glucose management (target 6-10 mmol/L) |
| **Post-Stroke** | Intensive risk factor modification · Statin, BP, antiplatelet/anticoagulant · Rehabilitation |
