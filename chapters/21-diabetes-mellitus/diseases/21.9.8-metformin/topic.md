## 2. Definition & Epidemiology

| Feature | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Drug Class** | Biguanide (only available agent) |
| **Mechanism** | [down]Hepatic gluconeogenesis ([up]AMPK -> [down]mTOR -> [down]gluconeogenic genes); [up]peripheral insulin sensitivity ([up]GLUT4); [down]intestinal glucose absorption; [up]GLP-1 secretion |
| **Pharmacokinetics** | Oral absorption 50-60%; no protein binding; renal excretion unchanged (t[half] 4-9h); not metabolised |
| **Dosing** | Immediate-release (IR): 500mg BD/TDS with meals -> max 3g/day; Extended-release (XR): 500mg OD with evening meal -> max 2g/day |
| **HbA1c Reduction** | Monotherapy: 1.0-1.5% (11-16 mmol/mol); Add-on: 0.5-1.0% |

## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### Renal Dosing (eGFR-based) -- **CRITICAL FOR EXAMS**

| eGFR (mL/min/1.73m2) | CKD Stage | Metformin Dose | Action |
|----------------------|-----------|----------------|--------|
| **>=45** | G1-G3a | Full dose (IR up to 3g, XR up to 2g) | Standard |
| **30-44** | G3b | **Half max dose** (IR 1g/day, XR 1g/day) | Use with caution; monitor 3-6 monthly |
| **<30** | G4-G5 | **CONTRAINDICATED** | Stop; switch to alternative |

> **UK (MHRA) / ADA / KDIGO alignment**: eGFR <30 = contraindicated; 30-45 = dose reduction; [ge]45 = full dose.
> **In hospital**: Hold if eGFR <45, contrast procedures, surgery, hypoxia, sepsis.

### Contraindications

| Absolute | Relative / Precaution |
|----------|----------------------|
| eGFR <30 mL/min | eGFR 30-45 (dose reduce) |
| Acute metabolic acidosis (DKA, lactic acidosis) | Age >80 (caution, assess renal function) |
| Severe hepatic impairment | Constrast media (hold 48h post-IV contrast if eGFR<60) |
| Hypersensitivity | Alcohol excess, malnutrition, hypoxia (sepsis, MI, HF) |
| Pregnancy (Category B but insulin preferred for GDM/pre-existing) | Surgery (hold periop) |

## 6. Differential Diagnosis

| Condition | Distinguishing Features |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| **Metformin-associated lactic acidosis (MALA)** | Metabolic acidosis (pH<7.3, lactate >5 mmol/L) + metformin accumulation (renal failure, hypoxia, sepsis); mortality 30-50%; **STOP metformin**, supportive care, CRRT |
| **GI intolerance** | Diarrhoea, nausea, abdominal pain (20-30%); [down]with XR, titration, taking with meals |
| **B12 deficiency** | Macrocytic anaemia, peripheral neuropathy; screen annually, replace if <200 pg/mL |

## 7. Management

### Initiation & Titration

| Step | Dose | Titration |
|------|------|-----------|
| **1** | 500mg OD (IR with breakfast) or 500mg XR with evening meal | -- |
| **2** | 500mg BD (IR) or 1000mg XR | After 1 week if tolerated |
| **3** | 500mg TDS (IR 1500mg) or 1500mg XR | After 2 weeks |
| **4** | Max 2550-3000mg (IR) / 2000-2500mg (XR) | As tolerated |

> **XR preferred** for GI tolerability; take with evening meal.

### GI Side Effect Mitigation

| Strategy | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **XR formulation** | [down]GI adverse effects 30-50% vs IR |
| **Gradual titration** | 500mg weekly increments |
| **With meals** | IR: with food; XR: with evening meal |
| **Switch to XR** | If IR intolerant |
| **Probiotics** | Some evidence for [down]diarrhoea |

### Special Situations

| Situation | Management |
|-----------|------------|
| **Contrast procedures** | Hold metformin at time of procedure; restart 48h post if eGFR [ge]45; if eGFR 30-44, assess risk/benefit |
| **Surgery** | Hold on day of surgery; restart when eating/drinking and renal function stable |
| **Hospital admission** | Review indication; hold if acute illness, hypoxia, sepsis, contrast planned |
| **Pregnancy** | Safe (Category B) but insulin preferred for GDM/pre-existing DM; can continue if already on for PCOS |
| **CKD G3b (eGFR 30-44)** | Half max dose; monitor eGFR 3-6 monthly; stop if <30 |
| **CKD G4-5 (eGFR <30)** | **STOP** (contraindicated) |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Metformin causes hypoglycaemia?** | NO -- minimal risk as monotherapy; only with insulin/SU |
| **Metformin contraindicated in all CKD?** | NO -- only eGFR<30; 30-44 = half dose; [ge]45 = full dose |
| **Metformin before contrast?** | Hold at time of IV contrast; restart 48h post if eGFR[ge]45; if eGFR 30-44, individualise |
| **XR vs IR efficacy?** | Equivalent HbA1c reduction; XR better GI tolerability |
