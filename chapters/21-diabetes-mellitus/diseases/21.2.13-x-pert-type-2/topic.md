## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### DESMOND
| Component | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Format** | 1-day group (6-12 participants) or 2x half-days |
| **Curriculum** | Self-management, lifestyle, medication understanding, goal setting, complications |
| **Educators** | Trained lay educators + healthcare professionals |
| **Evidence** | HbA1c ↓0.5-1.0%; weight ↓2-3kg; self-efficacy ↑ |

### X-PERT
| Component | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Format** | 6-week (2.5hr/week) group programme |
| **Curriculum** | Lifestyle, medications, monitoring, complications, psychological wellbeing |
| **Educators** | Trained X-PERT educators (dietitians/nurses) |
| **Evidence** | HbA1c ↓0.3-0.5%; weight ↓3-4kg; sustained at 14 months |

### Programme Comparison

|---------|---------|--------|

| Recommendation | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
