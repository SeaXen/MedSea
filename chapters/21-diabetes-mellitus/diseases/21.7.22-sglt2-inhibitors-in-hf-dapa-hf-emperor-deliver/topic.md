# SGLT2 Inhibitors in Heart Failure (DAPA-HF, EMPEROR, DELIVER)

> [!info]
> **SGLT2i = disease-modifying across EF spectrum** (HFrEF → HFmrEF → HFpEF); **dapagliflozin/empagliflozin** licensed for HF (eGFR ≥20); **CV death/HF hosp ↓ 20–30%**; renal protection additive; **mechanism**: metabolic (ketones), haemodynamic (preload/afterload), anti-inflammatory.

## 2. Key HF Outcome Trials

| Trial | Population | Drug | Primary Outcome | Key Result |
|-------|------------|------|-----------------|------------|
| **DAPA-HF** (2019) | HFrEF (EF ≤40%), ±T2DM | **Dapagliflozin 10mg** | CV death / HF hosp / urgent visit | **HR 0.74 (26% ↓)**; consistent ±T2DM |
| **EMPEROR-Reduced** (2020) | HFrEF (EF ≤40%), ±T2DM | **Empagliflozin 10mg** | CV death / HF hosp | **HR 0.75 (25% ↓)**; consistent ±T2DM |
| **DELIVER** (2022) | HFmrEF/pEF (EF >40%), ±T2DM | **Dapagliflozin 10mg** | CV death / HF hosp | **HR 0.82 (18% ↓)**; benefit down to EF ~60% |
| **EMPEROR-Preserved** (2021) | HFpEF (EF >40%), ±T2DM | **Empagliflozin 10mg** | CV death / HF hosp | **HR 0.79 (21% ↓)**; consistent ±T2DM |

**Meta-analysis** (5 trials, 11,000+ patients): **CV death/HF hosp ↓ 23%** across EF spectrum.

---

## 3. Indications & Dosing

| Indication | Drug | Dose | eGFR Threshold |
|------------|------|------|----------------|
| **HFrEF (EF ≤40%)** | Dapagliflozin / Empagliflozin | **10mg OD** | **≥20 mL/min** |
| **HFmrEF (EF 41–49%)** | Dapagliflozin / Empagliflozin | 10mg OD | **≥20 mL/min** |
| **HFpEF (EF ≥50%)** | Dapagliflozin / Empagliflozin | 10mg OD | **≥20 mL/min** |
| **T2DM + CKD** | Dapagliflozin / Empagliflozin | 10mg OD | **≥20 (dapa/empa)** |

**Note**: Canagliflozin **not licensed for HF**; HF license = dapagliflozin & empagliflozin only.

---

## 4. Mechanism of Benefit (Multifactorial)

| Mechanism | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Metabolic** | ↑ Ketone bodies (β-hydroxybutyrate) → efficient cardiac fuel; ↓ glucose oxidation |
| **Haemodynamic** | Osmotic diuresis → ↓ preload; natriuresis → ↓ afterload; ↓ ventricular filling pressures |
| **Anti-inflammatory/anti-fibrotic** | ↓ NLRP3 inflammasome, ↓ IL-6, TNF-α, ↓ cardiac fibrosis |
| **Ion transport** | ↑ Na+/H+ exchange inhibition; ↓ intracellular Na+/Ca2+ overload |
| **Renal** | ↑ Tubuloglomerular feedback → afferent constriction → ↓ intraglomerular pressure |

**Not dependent on glycaemic effect** — benefit identical in T2DM and non-diabetic HF.

---

## 5. EF Spectrum — Consistent Benefit

| EF Category | Trials | Benefit |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| **HFrEF (≤40%)** | DAPA-HF, EMPEROR-Reduced | **RRR 25–26%** |
| **HFmrEF (41–49%)** | DELIVER, EMPEROR-Preserved | **RRR ~20%** |
| **HFpEF (≥50%)** | DELIVER, EMPEROR-Preserved | **RRR 18–21%** |
| **Continuous EF** | DELIVER (EF up to 65%) | Benefit **persists across EF range** |

**No lower EF threshold** for benefit — works even in advanced HFrEF.

---

## 6. Adverse Effects & Monitoring

| Adverse Effect | Incidence | Management |
|----------------|-----------|------------|
| **Genital mycotic infections** | ~5–10% (women > men) | Topical antifungal; hygiene; usually continue |
| **Volume depletion / hypotension** | ~2–3% | Reduce loop diuretic; monitor weight/BP; counsel on sick days |
| **Euglycaemic DKA** | Rare (~0.1%) | **Stop SGLT2i** if acute illness/surgery/fasting; check ketones if unwell |
| **AKI** | Rare (initial eGFR dip expected) | **Initial 3–5 mL drop = haemodynamic** — do not stop; stabilises |
| **Amputation** | CANVAS (cana) signal; **not seen with dapa/empa** | Foot care; avoid in active ulcer/ischaemia |
| **Fournier's gangrene** | Very rare | Urgent surgical debridement; stop SGLT2i |

---

## 7. Dosing by eGFR (HF Indication)

| eGFR | Dapagliflozin | Empagliflozin |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| **≥20** | **10mg OD** (full dose) | **10mg OD** (full dose) |
| **15–20** | Consider 10mg OD (off-label) | Consider 10mg OD (off-label) |
| **<15** | Not recommended | Not recommended |

**Initiation**: No need to stop diuretics; monitor weight/BP 1–2w; check eGFR/K+ 4w.

---

## 8. HF Guideline Position (ESC 2021 / ACC/AHA 2022 / NICE)

| Guideline | HFrEF | HFmrEF/pEF |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| **ESC 2021** | **Class I, Level A** (with ARNI, BB, MRA) | **Class I, Level A** (dapagliflozin/empagliflozin) |
| **ACC/AHA 2022** | **Class 1, Level A** | **Class 2a, Level A** |
| **NICE TA** | TA679 (dapa), TA774 (empa) | TA959 (dapa), TA960 (empa) |

**Four pillars of HFrEF**: **ARNI (or ACEi/ARB) + Beta-blocker + MRA + SGLT2i**.
