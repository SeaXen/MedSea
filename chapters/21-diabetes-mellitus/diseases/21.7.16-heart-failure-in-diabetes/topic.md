## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **HFrEF (EF ≤40%)** | SGLT2i: DAPA-HF (dapagliflozin), EMPEROR-Reduced (empagliflozin) → ↓CV death/HF hosp |
| **HFpEF (EF ≥50%)** | SGLT2i: EMPEROR-Preserved (empagliflozin), DELIVER (dapagliflozin) → ↓HF hosp · GLP-1 RA: STEP-HFpEF (semaglutide) → ↑symptoms/exercise |
| **HFmrEF (41-49%)** | SGLT2i benefit across spectrum |
| **Diabetic cardiomyopathy** | Diastolic dysfunction, fibrosis, microvascular disease, metabolic inflexibility |
| **SGLT2i dose** | Dapagliflozin 10mg, Empagliflozin 10mg · eGFR ≥20 for HF indication |
