## 2. Definition & Epidemiology
| Feature | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Problem** | HbA1c underestimates true glycaemia in CKD/ESRD |
| **Mechanism** | ↓RBC lifespan (uraemia, ESA, blood loss); ↑carbamylation of Hb |
| **Magnitude** | HbA1c 5-15% lower than true mean glucose in ESRD |
| **ESA effect** | ESA -> reticulocytosis -> younger RBCs -> ↓HbA1c |

## 5. Diagnosis & Investigations

### Alternative Glycaemic Markers
| Marker | Reflects | Advantages | Limitations |
|--------|----------|------------|-------------|
| **Fructosamine** | Mean glucose 2-3 weeks | Unaffected by RBC lifespan; cheap | ↓ in hypoproteinaemia; no DCCT standardisation |
| **Glycated Albumin (GA)** | Mean glucose 2-3 weeks | Unaffected by RBC lifespan; standardised | ↓ in nephrotic syndrome (albumin loss) |
| **Continuous Glucose Monitoring** | Real-time | Direct glucose measurement | Cost; calibration; not universally available |

### HbA1c Interpretation Algorithm
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Diabetes + CKD] --> B{eGFR >= 45?}
    B -->|Yes| C[HbA1c reliable; target per guidelines]
    B -->|No| D{eGFR 30-44?}
    D -->|Yes| E[HbA1c + Fructosamine; interpret with caution]
    D -->|No| F[Fructosamine/GA essential; HbA1c unreliable]
    F --> G[Target GA: <17-18% (equiv HbA1c ~53)]
    F --> H[Target Fructosamine: ~230-280 umol/L]
```

## 6. Differential Diagnosis
| Condition | Distinguishing Features |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| **Iron deficiency anaemia** | HbA1c falsely HIGH (prolonged RBC survival) |
| **Haemolysis/blood loss** | HbA1c falsely LOW (shortened RBC survival) |
| **ESA therapy** | HbA1c falsely LOW (reticulocytosis) |
| **Recent transfusion** | HbA1c falsely LOW (donor RBCs) |
| **Nephrotic syndrome** | GA falsely LOW (albumin loss); fructosamine preferred |

## 7. Management

### Glycaemic Targets in CKD
| CKD Stage | HbA1c Target (if reliable) | Alternative Target (GA/Fructosamine) |
|-----------|----------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| **G1-G3a** | < 53 mmol/mol (7.0%) | GA < 18%; Fructosamine < 280 umol/L |
| **G3b-G4** | < 58 mmol/mol (7.5%) | GA < 19-20%; Fructosamine < 300 umol/L |
| **G5 / ESRD** | < 64 mmol/mol (8.0%) | GA < 20-21%; Fructosamine < 320 umol/L |
| **Dialysis** | < 64 mmol/mol (8.0%) | GA < 20-21%; Fructosamine < 320 umol/L |

### Monitoring Frequency
| Marker | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|
| **HbA1c** | 6-monthly (G1-G3); 3-monthly (G4-5) |
| **Fructosamine/GA** | 3-monthly (G3b-G5) |
| **CGM** | Continuous (if available) |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **HbA1c = gold standard always?** | NO - unreliable in CKD G3b+; use GA/Fructosamine/CGM |
| **GA vs Fructosamine?** | GA standardised; fructosamine cheaper; both 2-3 wk mean |
| **Nephrotic syndrome -> GA?** | GA falsely LOW (albumin loss); use fructosamine instead |
