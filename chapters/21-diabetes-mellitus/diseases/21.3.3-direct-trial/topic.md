## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### DiRECT Outcomes
| Timepoint | Intervention Remission | Control Remission | Odds Ratio |
|-----------|------------------------|-------------------|------------|
| **12 months** | 46% | 4% | 19.7 |
| **24 months** | 36% | 3% | — |
| **5 years** | 23% | — | — |

### Remission Definition
| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **HbA1c** | <48 mmol/mol (6.5%) |
| **Medications** | Off ALL glucose-lowering meds ≥3 months |
| **Sustained** | At 12 and 24 months |

### Weight Loss & Remission
| Weight Loss | Remission Rate |
|-------------|----------------|
| **0-5kg** | 7% |
| **5-10kg** | 34% |
| **10-15kg** | 57% |
| **≥15kg** | 86% |

## 7. Management

### DiRECT Intervention Phases
| Phase | Duration | Details |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **1. Total Diet Replacement** | 12-20 weeks | 825-853 kcal/day (soups/shakes); all meals replaced |
| **2. Food Reintroduction** | 2-8 weeks | Stepwise reintroduction; dietitian support |
| **3. Maintenance** | Long-term | Monthly visits; weight monitoring; relapse management |

### Eligibility Criteria (DiRECT)
| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **T2DM duration** | <6 years |
| **Age** | 20-65 years |
| **BMI** | 27-45 kg/m2 |
| **Insulin** | Not on insulin |
| **HbA1c** | No upper limit (but <108 if on insulin) |
