# CKD and HF Guided Therapy

> [!info]
> **Modern T2DM algorithm = comorbidity-driven** (not HbA1c-driven); **SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA first** if CKD/HF/CVD; **SGLT2i eGFR ≥20**; **GLP-1 RA eGFR ≥15**; **finerenone eGFR ≥25 A2/A3**; **avoid SU, TZD, saxagliptin/alogliptin in HF**; **metformin if eGFR ≥30**.

## 2. Comorbidity-First Algorithm (ADA 2024 / KDIGO 2024 / ESC 2023)

### Step 1: Confirm T2DM Diagnosis + Assess Comorbidities
| Comorbidity | First-Line Add-On (Beyond Metformin) |
|-------------|--------------------------------------|
| **ASCVD** (MI, stroke, PAD) | **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa) **+ GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide/dulaglutide) |
| **HF** (HFrEF or HFpEF) | **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa) **first**; GLP-1 RA if obese HFpEF |
| **CKD** (eGFR <60 or ACR ≥30) | **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa) **+ GLP-1 RA** **+ finerenone** (if A2/A3, eGFR ≥25) |
| **Obesity** (BMI ≥30) | **GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide/tirzepatide) **or** **SGLT2i** |
| **High CV risk only** | **SGLT2i** or **GLP-1 RA** (per trial evidence) |

**Metformin**: Continue if eGFR ≥30; stop if <30.

## 3. Agent Selection by eGFR & Comorbidity

### SGLT2 Inhibitors (CKD/HF Indications)

| Agent | HF Indication | CKD Indication | eGFR Threshold | Dose |
|-------|---------------|----------------|----------------|------|
| **Dapagliflozin** | HFrEF, HFpEF | T2DM + CKD, Non-diabetic CKD | **≥20** | 10mg OD |
| **Empagliflozin** | HFrEF, HFpEF | T2DM + CKD, Non-diabetic CKD | **≥20** | 10mg OD |
| **Canagliflozin** | HFrEF | T2DM + CKD | **≥30** | 100mg OD |

**Mechanism beyond glucose**: Haemodynamic, anti-inflammatory, anti-fibrotic.

### GLP-1 Receptor Agonists (CKD/HF Indications)

| Agent | CV Indication | HFpEF (obese) | eGFR Threshold | Dose |
|-------|---------------|---------------|----------------|------|
| **Semaglutide SC** | MACE ↓ (SUSTAIN-6) | **STEP-HFpEF** (2.4mg) | **≥15** (no adjust) | 0.5→1.0mg wk |
| **Dulaglutide** | MACE ↓ (REWIND) | — | **≥15** (no adjust) | 0.75–1.5mg wk |
| **Liraglutide** | MACE ↓ (LEADER) | FIGHT (neutral) | **≥15** (no adjust) | 1.2–1.8mg daily |

**No dose adjustment needed down to eGFR 15**.

### Finerenone (CKD Specific)

| Indication | eGFR | ACR | Dose |
|------------|------|-----|------|
| **T2DM + DKD (A2/A3)** | **≥25** | **A2 (3–30) or A3 (>30)** | 10mg OD (eGFR 25–60); **20mg OD** (eGFR ≥60) |

**Monitor K+ at 4 weeks, then quarterly**; hold if K+ >5.5.

## 4. Agents to AVOID in CKD/HF

| Agent | Avoid In | Reason |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Sulfonylureas** (except gliclazide) | CKD G3b–G5, HF | Hypoglycaemia; no CV benefit |
| **Thiazolidinediones (TZDs)** | **HF (any class)**, fracture risk | Fluid retention → HF exacerbation |
| **DPP-4i: Saxagliptin, Alogliptin** | **HF** | SAVOR/EXAMINE: ↑ HF hospitalisation |
| **DPP-4i: Sitagliptin, Linagliptin** | Generally safe | Neutral CV; linagliptin no renal adjust |
| **GLP-1 RA** | — | **Safe in HF** (FIGHT, LIVE neutral; STEP-HFpEF benefit) |
| **SGLT2i** | — | **Preferred in CKD/HF** |
| **Metformin** | eGFR <30 | Lactic acidosis risk |
| **Insulin** | — | Safe; dose reduce by eGFR |

## 5. Treatment Sequencing Examples

### Scenario A: T2DM + HFrEF + CKD G3a (eGFR 50, ACR 10)
1. Metformin (continue, eGFR 50)
2. **SGLT2i (dapagliflozin/empagliflozin 10mg)** — **first add-on** (HF + CKD)
3. **GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide) if HbA1c not controlled
4. Stop sulfonylurea if present

### Scenario B: T2DM + HFpEF + Obesity (BMI 35) + CKD G2
1. Metformin
2. **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa) — **HFpEF indication**
3. **Semaglutide 2.4mg** — **HFpEF + obesity** (STEP-HFpEF)
4. Finerenone if ACR ≥30 and eGFR ≥25

### Scenario C: T2DM + CKD G3b (eGFR 35, ACR 50) + No CVD/HF
1. Metformin (max 1g BD)
2. **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa — eGFR ≥20) **+ GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide/dulaglutide)
3. **Finerenone** (eGFR ≥25, A2/A3) — add after SGLT2i+GLP-1
4. Avoid TZD, SU, saxagliptin

### Scenario D: T2DM + No Comorbidities (BMI 28, eGFR 80)
1. Metformin
2. **SGLT2i** or **GLP-1 RA** (per patient preference, cost, weight)
3. DPP-4i (linagliptin) if injectable decline

## 6. Monitoring by Comorbidity

| Comorbidity | Key Monitoring | Frequency |
|-------------|----------------|-----------|
| **CKD** | eGFR, UACR, K+ | **3–6 monthly** (G3+); annual (G1–2) |
| **HF** | NT-proBNP, weight, symptoms, K+, eGFR | **3–6 monthly** |
| **CVD** | LDL, BP, HbA1c | 6–12 monthly |
| **SGLT2i** | eGFR, K+, volume status | 1–2w post-initiation; then 3m |
| **GLP-1 RA** | Weight, GI symptoms, HR | 3m |
| **Finerenone** | **K+ at 4w**, then quarterly | Per protocol |

## 7. Exam Pearls (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Point |
|-------|-----------|
| **Algorithm shift** | **Comorbidity-first** (not HbA1c-first) |
| **SGLT2i in CKD** | **eGFR ≥20** (dapa/empa); cana ≥30 |
| **SGLT2i in HF** | **HFrEF + HFpEF** — dapa/empa both |
| **GLP-1 RA in CKD** | **No dose adjust** down to eGFR 15 |
| **GLP-1 RA in HF** | **Semaglutide 2.4mg for HFpEF + obesity** (STEP-HFpEF) |
| **Finerenone** | **eGFR ≥25 + A2/A3**; monitor K+ at 4w |
| **Avoid in HF** | **TZDs (contraindicated)**, **saxagliptin/alogliptin** (↑ HF hosp) |
| **Avoid in CKD** | SU (hypo), TZD (HF), metformin <30 |
| **Metformin eGFR** | **≥30** (max 1g BD if 30–45); <30 stop |
| **Sequence** | Metformin → **SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA** (if CKD/HF/CVD) → others |
