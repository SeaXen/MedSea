# Zinc Transporter 8 (ZnT8) Autoantibodies

> [!info]
> **ZnT8 = 4th major T1DM autoantibody** (Slc30A8 gene; zinc transporter in β-cell secretory granules); **60–80% positive at diagnosis**; added to GAD65, IA-2, IAA panel → **↑ sensitivity to ~98%**; ZnT8-R/W/Q arginine/tryptophan/glutamine variants at position 325; not yet routine in all centres.

## 2. Biology

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Full name** | **Zinc Transporter 8** (ZnT8; SLC30A8) |
| **Location** | **β-cell secretory granule membrane** |
| **Function** | Transports **Zn2+ into granules** → insulin crystallization (Zn2+-insulin hexamers) |
| **Gene** | **SLC30A8** (chromosome 8q24) |
| **Polymorphism** | **rs13266634** (C/T) → **Arg325Trp (R/W)**; also **Gln (Q)** variant |
| **Antibody target** | C-terminal domain (intracellular) |

---

## 3. Diagnostic Performance

| Population | ZnT8 Sensitivity | Specificity |
|------------|------------------|-------------|
| **New-onset T1DM (children)** | **60–80%** | **>99%** |
| **New-onset T1DM (adults)** | **50–70%** | >99% |
| **LADA** | **40–60%** | >99% |
| **T2DM** | **<2%** | >99% |
| **Healthy controls** | **<1%** | — |

**Combined panel (GAD65 + IA-2 + IAA + ZnT8)**: **Sensitivity ~98%** at diagnosis.

---

## 4. ZnT8 Variants & Antibody Binding

| Variant (rs13266634) | Amino Acid 325 | Antibody Binding | Population Frequency |
|----------------------|----------------|------------------|---------------------|
| **CC** | **Arginine (R)** | **Strong** (ZnT8-R Ab) | ~50% (European) |
| **TT** | **Tryptophan (W)** | **Strong** (ZnT8-W Ab) | ~30% |
| **CT** | **Both R + W** | **Both** | ~20% |
| **Rare** | **Glutamine (Q)** | **ZnT8-Q Ab** | <1% |

**Clinical implication**: Assays should detect **both R and W variants** (or use recombinant antigen covering both) to avoid false negatives.

---

## 5. Clinical Utility

| Use Case | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Diagnosis (atypical presentation)** | Antibody-negative "T2DM" → ZnT8+ confirms autoimmune |
| **LADA** | ZnT8+ in GAD65+/IA-2- cases → confirms autoimmune |
| **Prediction (relatives)** | **Multiple antibodies** (including ZnT8) → ↑ 5y risk |
| **Clinical trials** | Stratification (e.g., teplizumab, immunotherapy) |
| **Post-transplant** | Recurrence monitoring (ZnT8 may persist) |

---

## 6. Comparison of T1DM Autoantibodies

| Antibody | Target | Sensitivity (New T1DM) | Specificity | Notes |
|----------|--------|------------------------|-------------|-------|
| **GAD65** | Glutamic acid decarboxylase | **70–80%** | 98% | Most common; persists years |
| **IA-2 (IA-2β)** | Insulinoma-associated antigen-2 | **60–70%** | 99% | More specific for progression |
| **IAA** | Insulin (autoantibody) | **40–60%** (children) | 99% | **Only valid if insulin-naive**; age-dependent |
| **ZnT8** | Zinc transporter 8 | **60–80%** | >99% | **Newest**; adds sensitivity |
| **Combined (4)** | All four | **~98%** | >99% | **Gold standard panel** |

---

## 7. Practical Considerations

| Issue | Guidance |
|-------|----------|
| **Cost** | More expensive than GAD/IA-2; not all centres offer routinely |
| **Availability** | Reference labs; specialist centres; **not universal** |
| **Timing** | Best at diagnosis; declines over years (like IA-2) |
| **Standardisation** | Improving (IDSP workshops); R/W variant coverage essential |
| **Interpretation** | **Positive = autoimmune aetiology**; negative ≠ excludes (sensitivity <100%) |

---

## 8. ZnT8 in LADA (Latent Autoimmune Diabetes in Adults)

| Scenario | ZnT8 Role |
|----------|-----------|
| **GAD65+ / IA-2-** | ZnT8+ confirms autoimmune; predicts faster insulin requirement |
| **GAD65- / IA-2-** | ZnT8+ rare but identifies autoimmune subset |
| **Prediction** | **Multiple Abs (GAD+ZnT8)** → insulin within 3–5y vs GAD alone 6–10y |
