## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### Intervention Details
| Component | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Weight loss** | ≥5% body weight |
| **Diet** | Total fat <30% kcal; saturated fat <10%; fibre ≥15g/1000kcal |
| **Exercise** | ≥30 min/day moderate intensity |
| **Counselling** | Individualised, 7 sessions year 1, quarterly thereafter |

### Key Results
| Outcome | Intervention | Control | Reduction |
|---------|--------------|---------|-----------|
| **Cumulative DM incidence (4y)** | 11% | 23% | **58%** |
| **13-year follow-up** | 23% | 38% | 43% |
| **Weight loss (1y)** | 4.2kg | 0.9kg | — |
