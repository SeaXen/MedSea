# Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT)

> [!info]
> **MNT = cornerstone of T2DM management**; **500kcal/day deficit** → 0.5kg/week weight loss; **carb 40–45%** (low GI, ≥130g/day), **protein 15–20%**, **fat <35%** (<10% saturated); **Mediterranean / DASH** patterns; **dietitian referral**; **5–15% weight loss** → remission possible.

## 2. Macronutrient Distribution (ADA / EASD / NICE)

| Nutrient | Target | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **Carbohydrate** | **40–45% total calories** (≥130g/day minimum) | Glucose control; brain fuel; fibre source |
| **Protein** | **15–20%** (1.0–1.5g/kg ideal BW) | Satiety; muscle preservation; glycaemic neutral |
| **Fat** | **<35%** (<10% saturated) | CVD risk reduction; calorie density |

**Carbohydrate Quality > Quantity**:
- **Low GI/GL** (oats, legumes, whole grains, vegetables)
- **Fibre ≥30g/day** (soluble: oats, psyllium, legumes)
- **Avoid**: SSBs, refined grains, added sugars, ultra-processed

---

## 3. Caloric Restriction & Weight Loss

| Target | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| **Weight loss** | **5–15%** (5% = metabolic benefit; 15% = remission) |
| **Caloric deficit** | **500–750 kcal/day** below TDEE |
| **Rate** | **0.5–1kg/week** |
| **Protein during weight loss** | **1.2–1.5g/kg** (preserve lean mass) |
| **Physical activity** | Combined with diet (see Physical Activity note) |

**DiRECT Trial**: **800kcal/day formula diet** → **46% remission at 1y** (15kg loss); 70% remission if >15kg loss.

---

## 4. Dietary Patterns — Evidence-Based

| Pattern | Key Features | Diabetes Evidence |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Mediterranean** | EVOO, nuts, fish, vegetables, legumes, whole grains, wine | **PREDIMED**: ↓ T2DM incidence 30–40%; ↓ CVD |
| **DASH** | Fruits, vegetables, low-fat dairy, whole grains, low sodium | ↓ BP; ↓ insulin resistance; ↓ T2DM risk |
| **Plant-based / Vegetarian** | Legumes, nuts, whole grains, minimal animal | ↓ HbA1c; ↓ weight; ↓ CVD risk |
| **Low Carb (<130g/day)** | <26% carb; higher protein/fat | ↓ HbA1c short-term; weight loss; **LDL may rise**; long-term safety unclear |
| **Very Low Calorie (VLCD)** | **800kcal/day** (formula) | **DiRECT**: 46% remission; requires medical supervision |

**NICE/ADA**: **Individualised** — no single "diabetic diet"; Mediterranean/DASH first-line.

## 4. Special Considerations

### Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
| Stage | Protein | Potassium | Phosphate | Sodium |
|-------|---------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| **G3a–G3b** | **0.8g/kg** (avoid >1.0) | Restrict if >5.5 | Restrict if elevated | <2.3g/day |
| **G4–G5** | **0.6–0.8g/kg** + ketoanalogues | Restrict | Restrict (binders) | <2g/day |

### Elderly / Frail
- **Avoid malnutrition** > strict glycaemic control
- **Protein ≥1.2g/kg** (sarcopenia prevention)
- **Liberalised diet** (quality of life)
- **Oral nutritional supplements** if intake inadequate

### Pregnancy (GDM / Pre-existing)
- **Carb 175g/day minimum** (fetal brain)
- **3 meals + 2–3 snacks** (prevent ketosis)
- **Low GI, high fibre**
- **Folic acid 5mg** (pre-conception)

## 5. Practical MNT Delivery

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1. Assessment** | Diet history (24h recall, food frequency), weight, HbA1c, lipids, renal |
| **2. Goal Setting** | SMART goals (weight, HbA1c, dietary changes) |
| **3. Education** | Carb awareness, label reading, portion sizes, cooking methods |
| **3. Monitoring** | Weight monthly; HbA1c 3–6m; dietitian review 3–6m |
| **4. Behavioural** | Self-monitoring (food diary, apps), problem-solving, relapse prevention |

**Referral**: **Dietitian at diagnosis** (NICE); annual review; more frequent if not at target.

## 6. Weight Loss & Remission — Nutrition

| Programme | Diet | Weight Loss | Remission |
|-----------|------|-------------|-----------|
| **DiRECT** | 800kcal formula (12–20w) → food reintroduction | **15kg** (mean) | **46% at 1y**, 36% at 2y |
| **DROPLET** | 810kcal formula (GP-led) | 10kg | 27% at 1y |
| **Low Carb Program** | <130g carb (digital) | 7–10kg | 25–30% |
| **Mediterranean + Exercise** | Ad lib Mediterranean + 150min/wk | 4–6kg | 15–20% |

**Remission definition** (DiRECT): **HbA1c <48 mmol/mol off all glucose medications for ≥2 months**.

## 7. Common Myths vs Evidence

| Myth | Evidence |
|------|----------|
| **"Avoid all carbs"** | Carbs essential; **quality > quantity**; <130g not needed for all |
| **"Fruit is bad"** | Whole fruit ↓ T2DM risk; **juice/dried fruit** ↑ risk |
| **"Special diabetic foods"** | No benefit; often high fat/calorie; **normal food + portions** |
| **"High protein damages kidneys"** | **Only if CKD G4–G5**; normal kidneys tolerate 1.5–2g/kg |
| **"Artificial sweeteners cause diabetes"** | **No causal evidence**; useful for SSB replacement |
