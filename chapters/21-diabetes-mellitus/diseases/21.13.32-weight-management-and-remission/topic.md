# Weight Management and Remission

> [!info]
> **Weight loss 5–15%** → metabolic benefit; **Remission = HbA1c <48 mmol/mol off glucose meds ≥2 months**; **DiRECT**: 800kcal formula 12–20w → **46% remission at 1y** (15kg loss); **70% if >15kg loss**; **maintenance critical**; **bariatric surgery >75% remission**.

---

## 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Define weight loss targets for metabolic benefit vs remission
- [ ] Apply DiRECT/VLCD protocol for remission
- [ ] Define remission criteria and monitoring
- [ ] Manage weight maintenance post-loss
- [ ] Position pharmacotherapy (GLP-1 RA, SGLT2i) and surgery

---

## 2. Weight Loss Thresholds & Benefits

| Weight Loss | Metabolic Benefit |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **3–5%** | ↓ HbA1c 3–5 mmol/mol; ↓ BP; ↑ insulin sensitivity |
| **5–10%** | **Clinically significant**: ↓ HbA1c 5–10; ↓ CVD risk; ↓ medication need |
| **10–15%** | **Remission possible**: ↓ HbA1c 10–20; ↓ CVD events |
| **>15%** | **High remission rate**: 70–80% (DiRECT); ↓ mortality |

---

## 3. Remission Definition (Consensus: ADA/EASD/Diabetes UK)

| Criteria | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **HbA1c** | **<48 mmol/mol (6.5%)** |
| **Duration** | **≥2 months** sustained |
| **Medication** | **Off ALL glucose-lowering medications** |
| **Exclusions** | Not on steroids, not pregnant, not acute illness |

**Note**: "Remission" not "cure" — relapse common if weight regained.

---

## 4. DiRECT Trial Protocol (Evidence Base for Remission)

### Phase 1: Total Diet Replacement (12–20 weeks)
- **800–850 kcal/day** formula diet (soups, shakes, bars)
- **All meals replaced** — no conventional food
- **Weekly support** (dietitian/health coach)
- **Target**: **15kg loss** (or >15% body weight)
- **Stop glucose meds** on day 1 (except metformin if CKD)
- **Stop antihypertensives** if BP normalises (monitor)

### Phase 2: Food Reintroduction (2–8 weeks)
- **Stepwise**: vegetables → lean protein → whole grains → fruit/dairy
- **Portion-controlled** meals (400–600kcal each)
- **Continue weekly support**

### Phase 3: Weight Maintenance (Long-term)
- **Monthly support** (group or individual)
- **Self-monitoring**: weight weekly, food diary, activity
- **Relapse protocol**: 2kg regain → return to formula 1 week
- **Medication re-start** if HbA1c ≥48

---

## 4. DiRECT Key Results

| Outcome | Result |
|---------|--------|
| **Mean weight loss** | **15kg** (intervention) vs 3kg (control) |
| **Remission at 1y** | **46%** (intervention) vs 4% (control) |
| **Remission if >15kg loss** | **70–80%** |
| **Remission at 2y** | **36%** (intervention) |
| **Medication cessation** | **74% off all glucose meds** at 1y |
| **BP reduction** | Mean −10/−5 mmHg; 50% off antihypertensives |
| **Quality of life** | Improved (EQ-5D) |
| **Cost-effectiveness** | **Dominant** (cost-saving per QALY) |

## 6. Weight Maintenance — Critical for Sustained Remission

| Strategy | Evidence |
|----------|----------|
| **Monthly support** (group/individual) | DiRECT: monthly → 36% remission at 2y |
| **Self-weighing** | Weekly; **2kg regain trigger** → action |
| **High physical activity** | **>300min/week** (NWCR: successful maintainers) |
| **Protein-rich breakfast** | ↓ Hunger, ↓ evening intake |
| **Continued food tracking** | Apps, food diaries |
| **Behavioural skills** | Problem-solving, relapse prevention, stress management |

**Relapse**: >50% regain some weight at 2–5y; **early intervention** prevents full relapse.

## 7. Pharmacotherapy for Weight Loss (Adjunct)

| Agent | Dose | Weight Loss | Remission Data |
|-------|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Semaglutide 2.4mg** (Wegovy) | 2.4mg weekly | **−15–17%** | STEP trials: remission in T2DM subset |
| **Tirzepatide 15mg** (Mounjaro) | 15mg weekly | **−20–22%** | SURMOUNT: high remission rates |
| **Liraglutide 3mg** (Saxenda) | 3mg daily | −5–8% | SCALE: modest remission |
| **Orlistat** | 120mg TDS | −3–5% | Low remission |
| **Naltrexone/Bupropion** | 32/360mg | −5% | Limited T2DM data |

**Positioning**: **After lifestyle failure** (BMI ≥30 or ≥27 + comorbidity); **GLP-1 RA preferred**.

---

## 8. Bariatric Surgery — Highest Remission Rates

| Procedure | Remission Rate | Mechanism |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| **RYGB** (Roux-en-Y) | **80–85%** at 1y; 50–60% at 5y | Restriction + malabsorption + incretin (GLP-1) |
| **Sleeve Gastrectomy** | **70–80%** at 1y; 40–50% at 5y | Restriction + ghrelin ↓ + GLP-1 ↑ |
| **OAGB** (One-anastomosis) | 80–85% | Similar to RYGB |

**NICE criteria**: BMI ≥35 + T2DM (or ≥30 + recent T2DM <10y); **ADA**: BMI ≥30 + T2DM.
