# GFR Categories (G1–G5)

> [!info]
> **KDIGO GFR categories (G1–G5)** based on **CKD-EPI 2021 creatinine equation** (race-free); requires **chronicity >3 months**; determines drug dosing, monitoring, referral.

---

## 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Name and define all 5 GFR categories
- [ ] Apply CKD-EPI equation and interpret eGFR
- [ ] Adjust diabetes medications by eGFR
- [ ] Identify nephrology referral thresholds

---

## 2. KDIGO GFR Categories

| Category | eGFR (mL/min/1.73m²) | Description | Clinical Implications |
|----------|----------------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **G1** | **≥90** | Normal / High | Monitor if risk factors (diabetes, HTN); drug dosing normal |
| **G2** | **60–89** | Mildly decreased | Monitor annually; screen for albuminuria; drug dosing normal |
| **G3a** | **45–59** | Mild-moderate decreased | **6-monthly monitoring**; adjust renally-cleared drugs; avoid NSAIDs |
| **G3b** | **30–44** | Moderate-severe decreased | **6-monthly**; **metformin max 1g BD** (NICE) / contraindicated (some); nephrology referral |
| **G4** | **15–29** | Severely decreased | **3–6 monthly**; **metformin contraindicated**; SGLT2i OK (dapa/empa ≥20); RRT planning; mandatory nephrology |
| **G5** | **<15** | Kidney failure | **Nephrology mandatory**; RRT (dialysis/transplant) or conservative; adjust all drugs |

---

## 3. CKD-EPI 2021 Equation (Race-Free)

**Variables**: Serum creatinine (µmol/L), age, sex
- **No race coefficient** (removed 2021)
- **Preferred over MDRD** (more accurate at higher GFR)
- **Cystatin C** equation available (eGFRcys, eGFRcr-cys) — use if creatinine unreliable (amputees, muscle wasting, high meat diet)

**Chronicity**: eGFR <60 on **≥2 occasions ≥90 days apart** = CKD.

## 4. Drug Dosing Adjustments by GFR Category (Key Diabetes Drugs)

| Drug | G1 (≥90) | G2 (60–89) | G3a (45–59) | G3b (30–44) | G4 (15–29) | G5 (<15) |
|------|----------|------------|-------------|-------------|------------|----------|
| **Metformin** | Full | Full | **Max 1g BD** | **Contraindicated** (NICE) | **Contraindicated** | **Contraindicated** |
| **Sulfonylureas** | Full | **Gliclazide preferred** | **Avoid glibenclamide/glimepiride** | **Avoid** | **Avoid** | **Avoid** |
| **DPP-4i** | Full | Full | **Dose reduce** (saxa/alo/vilda); **Linagliptin no adjust** | **Dose reduce** | **Dose reduce** | **Dose reduce** |
| **SGLT2i** | Full | Full | **Dapa/Empa OK** (≥20); Cana avoid <45 | **Dapa/Empa OK** (≥20) | **Dapa/Empa OK** (≥20) | Avoid |
| **GLP-1 RA** | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| **Finerenone** | Full | Full | Full | **eGFR ≥25** | **eGFR ≥25** | Avoid |
| **ACEi/ARB** | Full | Full | **Monitor K+/Cr 1–2w** | **Monitor K+/Cr 1–2w** | **Monitor K+/Cr 1–2w** | Specialist |
| **Insulin** | Standard | ↓ 10–20% | ↓ 20–30% | ↓ 30–50% | ↓ 50%+ | ↓ 50%+ |

## 5. Monitoring by GFR Category

| Category | UACR + eGFR | BP | HbA1c | Other |
|----------|-------------|----|-------|-------|
| **G1–2** | **Annual** | <130/80 | 3–6 monthly | Lipids annual |
| **G3a** | **6-monthly** | <130/80 | 3–6 monthly | K+, bicarbonate, phosphate, Ca, PTH annual |
| **G3b** | **6-monthly** | <130/80 | 3–6 monthly | As above + vitamin D, bone profile |
| **G4** | **3–6 monthly** | <130/80 | 3–6 monthly | As above + RRT planning, access |
| **G5** | **Monthly** (dialysis) | <130/80 | 3-monthly | Full dialysis bloods |

---

## 6. Special Considerations

| Situation | eGFR Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Acute kidney injury (AKI)** | eGFR not valid — use KDIGO AKI criteria (Cr rise, UO) |
| **Rapidly progressive** | eGFR decline >5 mL/min/yr → urgent nephrology |
| **Muscle wasting/amputee** | Creatinine-based eGFR **overestimates** — use cystatin C |
| **Pregnancy** | eGFR ↑ physiologically — use pregnancy-specific ranges |
| **Elderly/frail** | G3a may be "normal ageing" — assess trajectory, not single value |

## 7. Exam Pearls (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Point |
|-------|-----------|
| **GFR categories** | G1 ≥90, G2 60–89, G3a 45–59, G3b 30–44, G4 15–29, G5 <15 |
| **CKD-EPI 2021** | Race-free; preferred over MDRD |
| **Chronicity** | eGFR <60 on ≥2 occasions ≥90 days apart |
| **Metformin cutoff** | G3a: max 1g BD; **G3b (<45): contraindicated (NICE)** |
| **SGLT2i in CKD** | **Dapagliflozin/Empagliflozin ≥20**; Canagliflozin ≥45 |
| **Linagliptin** | **No dose adjustment** at any eGFR |
| **Finerenone** | **eGFR ≥25** + A2/A3 |
| **ACEi/ARB monitoring** | K+/Cr at 1–2w after start/change, then 6m |
| **Nephrology referral** | **eGFR <30 (G4)** or **ACR >70** or rapid decline |
| **Cystatin C** | Use if creatinine unreliable (low muscle mass, amputees) |
