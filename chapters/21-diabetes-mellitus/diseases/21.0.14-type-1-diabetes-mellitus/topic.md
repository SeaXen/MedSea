## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Facts |
|-------|-----------|
| **Classic presentation** | Polyuria, polydipsia, weight loss, fatigue → DKA in 25-30% at diagnosis |
| **LADA** | Age >30, non-obese, GAD65+, slow progression, initial non-insulin response |
| **Autoantibodies** | GAD65 (70-80%), IA-2 (50-70%), ZnT8 (60-80%), IAA (children >adults) · ≥2 = high risk |
| **Basal-bolus** | Basal 40-50% TDD (glargine U-100/U-300, degludec) · Bolus 50-60% TDD (rapid analogues) |
| **CSII** | Indications: HbA1c >58 on MDI, severe hypoglycaemia, Dawn phenomenon, lifestyle |
| **CGM metrics** | TIR 70-180mg/dL >70% · TBR <54mg/dL <4% · TAR >180mg/dL <25% · CV <36% |
| **DKA** | See Diabetic Emergencies hub |
| **Hypoglycaemia unawareness** | Impaired counter-regulation → avoid <3.9 for 2-3 weeks to restore awareness |
