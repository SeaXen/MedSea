# Diabetes Education and Structured Education Programmes

> [!info]
> **Structured education = NICE quality standard** — offer at diagnosis (T1DM: DAFNE; T2DM: DESMOND/X-PERT); evidence: HbA1c −5 to −10 mmol/mol, ↑ knowledge, ↑ QoL, cost-effective; digital options emerging.

## 2. Major Programmes

| Programme | Type | Format | Duration | Key Features |
|-----------|------|--------|----------|--------------|
| **DAFNE** (Dose Adjustment For Normal Eating) | **T1DM** | Group (6–8), 5 days (or 5× weekly) | **Carb counting + insulin dose adjustment**; skills-based; QISMET accredited |
| **DAFNE 1-day/Online** | T1DM | Shorter/digital | Refresher/remote access |
| **DESMOND** (Diabetes Education and Self-Management for Ongoing and Newly Diagnosed) | **T2DM** | Group, 1 day (6h) or 2× half-day | **Lifestyle, beliefs, self-management**; QISMET accredited |
| **X-PERT** (eXtend, Practical, Education, Research, Training) | **T2DM** | Group, 6× 2.5h weekly | **Progressive pathway**; insulin module available |
| **X-PERT Insulin** | T2DM on insulin | 6 sessions | Carb counting, dose adjustment |
| **BERTIE** | T1DM (pump/MDI) | 4 days | Similar to DAFNE, pump focus |
| **Digital** (MyDiabetes, DAFNE Online, Oviva, Liva) | T1/T2 | App/web, 12 weeks + coaching | Remote, scalable; NICE-approved some |

---

## 3. NICE Guidance (NG17, NG28, QS6)

| Recommendation | Details |
|----------------|---------|
| **Offer at diagnosis** | T1DM: DAFNE (or equivalent) at diagnosis; T2DM: DESMOND/X-PERT at diagnosis |
| **Annual review** | Assess need for refresher/education at each annual review |
| **QISMET accreditation** | Only QISMET-accredited programmes meet NICE standard |
| **Cultural adaptation** | Must be available in appropriate languages/culturally adapted |
| **Digital options** | Can supplement but **not replace** face-to-face initially |
| **Carer/family** | Include carer/family if patient wishes |

## 4. Evidence Base

| Outcome | DAFNE (T1DM) | DESMOND/X-PERT (T2DM) |
|---------|--------------|------------------------|
| **HbA1c** | **−5 to −10 mmol/mol** (0.5–1.0%) at 6–12m | **−3 to −6 mmol/mol** at 12m |
| **Severe hypoglycaemia** | ↓ 50–60% | Not primary outcome |
| **Diabetes knowledge** | ↑↑ | ↑↑ |
| **Quality of life** | ↑ (distress ↓) | ↑ |
| **Weight** | Neutral | ↓ 1–3 kg (X-PERT > DESMOND) |
| **Cost-effectiveness** | **Cost-saving** (DAFNE: £1,500/QALY) | **Cost-effective** (<£20,000/QALY) |
| **Sustained benefit** | Up to 5 years (with refreshers) | 12–24 months (booster needed) |

**Key trials**: DAFNE RCT (2002), DESMOND RCT (2008), X-PERT RCT (2007), 5-year DAFNE follow-up.

## 5. Core Curriculum Components (QISMET)

| Domain | DAFNE (T1DM) | DESMOND/X-PERT (T2DM) |
|--------|--------------|------------------------|
| **Pathophysiology** | Insulin physiology, glucose regulation | Insulin resistance, beta-cell decline |
| **Monitoring** | Capillary/CGM, pattern management | SMBG/CGM, when to test |
| **Carbohydrate** | **Carb counting (CP/10g)**, label reading | Carb awareness, portions, GI |
| **Insulin/Dose** | **ICR, ISF, correction**, basal testing | When to start, titration basics |
| **Hypoglycaemia** | Recognition, treatment, prevention | Recognition, treatment |
| **Illness/Sick days** | **Sick day rules**, ketone testing | Sick day rules |
| **Physical activity** | Exercise management, carb adjustment | Exercise prescription |
| **Complications** | Screening, prevention | Screening, prevention |
| **Psychological** | Diabetes distress, burnout | Beliefs, health behaviour change |
| **Lifestyle** | Alcohol, travel, driving | **Weight, diet, smoking, alcohol** |

---

## 6. Referral Criteria & Timing

| Patient Group | Timing | Programme |
|---------------|--------|-----------|
| **Newly diagnosed T1DM** | **Within 6–12 months** (after stabilisation) | DAFNE |
| **Newly diagnosed T2DM** | **Within 12 months** | DESMOND / X-PERT |
| **T1DM not previously attended** | Any time | DAFNE |
| **T2DM intensifying (insulin start)** | At insulin initiation | X-PERT Insulin / DAFNE principles |
| **Pump start** | Pre-pump | BERTIE / DAFNE pump module |
| **Recurrent hypoglycaemia** | Urgent | DAFNE refresher / specialist |
| **Diabetes distress/burnout** | Identified at review | Psychosocial + education |

---

## 7. Digital & Remote Education

| Platform | Target | Evidence |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **DAFNE Online** | T1DM | Non-inferior to face-to-face (DAFNE Online RCT) |
| **Oviva** | T2DM | ↓ HbA1c, weight loss (NHS DPP) |
| **Liva Healthcare** | T2DM | ↓ HbA1c, cost-effective |
| **MyDiabetes (NHS)** | T1/T2 | Structured content, tracking |
| **Low Carb Program** | T2DM/pre-diabetes | Remission outcomes (Virta/Diabetes.co.uk) |

**NICE position**: Digital can **supplement** or **replace** if face-to-face not accessible; must be QISMET/ORCHA accredited.

---

## 8. Barriers & Solutions

| Barrier | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **Non-attendance** (30–50%) | Flexible timing, digital, reminders, transport, childcare |
| **Language/culture** | Translated materials, bilingual educators, community venues |
| **Learning disabilities** | Adapted programmes (e.g., OK Diabetes) |
| **Work commitments** | Evening/weekend, digital, employer engagement |
| **Deprivation** | Community-based, link workers, incentivise |
