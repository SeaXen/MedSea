## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### Predictors of Sustained Remission
| Factor | Association |
|--------|-------------|
| **Weight maintenance** | <2kg regain from nadir |
| **T2DM duration** | <3 years at remission |
| **Baseline HbA1c** | <58 mmol/mol at remission |
| **C-peptide** | Preserved (>600 pmol/L) |
| **Age** | <50 years |
| **Weight loss** | ≥15kg at remission |

### Relapse Risk Factors
| Factor | Risk |
|--------|------|
| **Weight regain >2kg** | 3x higher relapse |
| **T2DM duration >6y** | 2x higher relapse |
| **Age >60** | Higher relapse |
| **Baseline HbA1c >75** | Higher relapse |

## 7. Management

### Maintenance Protocol
| Component | Strategy |
|-----------|----------|
| **Weight monitoring** | Weekly self-weigh; monthly clinic visit year 1 |
| **Diet** | Mediterranean/low GI; 1500-1800 kcal; high protein |
| **Exercise** | 150-300 min/week moderate + resistance 2x/week |
| **Behavioural** | Self-monitoring; problem-solving; relapse plan |
| **Medication** | None (if remission); metformin if pre-diabetes |

### Relapse Early Intervention
| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Weight regain >2kg** | Intensive lifestyle review; dietitian referral |
| **HbA1c 42-47** | Intensify lifestyle; consider metformin if persistent |
| **HbA1c ≥48 on 2 occasions** | Restart glucose-lowering medication |

### Behavioural Strategies
| Strategy | Evidence |
|----------|----------|
| **Self-monitoring** | Daily weight, food diary, activity tracker |
| **Problem-solving** | Identify triggers (stress, social, emotional eating) |
| **Relapse prevention plan** | Written plan; 3 "red flags" with actions |
| **Social support** | Peer groups, family involvement, digital apps |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Remission = permanent cure?** | NO - relapse common; ongoing monitoring required |
| **Relapse = failure?** | NO - expected; early intervention restores remission |
| **Medication during remission?** | None (by definition); if restart needed -> memo |
| **Monitoring enough?** | Monthly weight + 3-6mo HbA1c minimum |

- **T**racking: daily weight; food diary; activity
