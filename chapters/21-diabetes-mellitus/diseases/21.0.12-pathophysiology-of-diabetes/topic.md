## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Facts |
|-------|-----------|
| **T1D autoimmunity** | CD4+/CD8+ T-cell mediated β-cell destruction · Insulitis · GAD65, IA-2, ZnT8, IAA autoantibodies |
| **T1D genetics** | HLA-DR3/DR4-DQ2/DQ8 (40-50% risk) · INS VNTR · PTPN22 · CTLA4 · >50 loci |
| **T1D stages** | Stage 1: ≥2 autoantibodies, normoglycaemia · Stage 2: ≥2 autoantibodies, dysglycaemia · Stage 3: symptomatic |
| **T2D insulin resistance** | Impaired PI3K-Akt signalling in muscle/liver/adipose · ↑FFA, inflammation (TNF-α, IL-6), adipokines |
| **T2D β-cell failure** | ↑Proinsulin/insulin ratio · Amyloid (IAPP) deposition · Glucolipotoxicity · Dedifferentiation |
| **T2D incretin defect** | ↓GLP-1 secretion · ↓GIP effect on β-cell · ↑Glucagon → ↑HGP |
| **T2D genetics** | TCF7L2 (strongest) · KCNJ11 · PPARG · FTO · >400 loci (polygenic risk score) |
