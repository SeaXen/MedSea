## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Facts |
|-------|-----------|
| **GDM screening** | IADPSG: universal 75g OGTT 24-28wk (FBG≥5.1, 1h≥10.0, 2h≥8.5) · NICE: risk factors then OGTT · ADA: universal or risk-based |
| **GDM targets** | FBG <5.3, 1h <7.8, 2h <6.4 mmol/L · HbA1c not recommended for monitoring |
| **GDM management** | Diet 1st → Metformin (if fasting >5.5 or postprandial >7.0) → Insulin · Glyburide alternative |
| **Pre-existing DM pregnancy** | Pre-conception HbA1c <48mmol/mol (6.5%) · Folic acid 5mg OD to 12wk · Stop ACEi/ARB/statin/SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA |
| **Postpartum** | OGTT 6-13 weeks · Annual HbA1c · Lifetime T2DM risk 50% |
| **Elderly HbA1c targets** | Fit: <53mmol/mol (7.0%) · Mild frailty: <58mmol/mol (7.5%) · Moderate/severe: <64mmol/mol (8.0%) |
| **Deintensification** | Stop sulfonylurea/insulin if hypoglycaemia · Simplify regimens |
| **CKD HbA1c** | Falsely low in CKD/ESRD (↓RBC lifespan) · Use fructosamine or glycated albumin |
| **CKD drug dosing** | Metformin: eGFR≥45 full, 30-45 half dose, <30 contraindicated · SGLT2i: initiate eGFR≥45/30 · Insulin: ↓dose 25-50% |
| **Dialysis** | Glucose-free dialysate → hypoglycaemia risk · Insulin requirements variable · Monitor pre/post dialysis |
| **HIV/ART** | Protease inhibitors, integrase inhibitors → insulin resistance, dyslipidaemia, lipodystrophy |
