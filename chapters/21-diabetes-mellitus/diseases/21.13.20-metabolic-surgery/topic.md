## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Indications (NICE/ADA)** | BMI ≥35 (or ≥30 with comorbidities) · T2DM <10 years · C-peptide >200 pmol/L · Failed medical therapy |
| **Procedures** | RYGB (gold standard), Sleeve gastrectomy (most common), OAGB, BPD/DS |
| **Remission rates** | RYGB: 80% at 1y, 50% at 10y · SG: 60% at 1y · BPD/DS: 95% at 1y |
| **Mechanisms** | Weight loss, ↓ghrelin, ↑GLP-1/PYY, bile acid changes, microbiome, ↓caloric absorption |
| **Post-surgery** | Stop insulin/sulfonylurea immediate · Continue metformin · Monitor B12, iron, Ca, vit D · Hypoglycaemia risk (especially dumping) |
