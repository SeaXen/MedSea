# rtCGM vs isCGM (Real-Time vs Intermittently-Scanned CGM)

> [!info]
> **rtCGM = Real-time alerts + automatic upload** (Dexcom G6/G7, Guardian, FreeStyle Libre 3); **isCGM = Scan-dependent** (FreeStyle Libre 1/2). Both factory-calibrated; rtCGM superior for hypoglycaemia prevention.

## 2. Definitions & Technology

| Feature | **rtCGM** (Real-Time CGM) | **isCGM** (Intermittently-Scanned / Flash) |
|---------|---------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| **Examples** | Dexcom G6/G7, Medtronic Guardian 3/4, **FreeStyle Libre 3**, Eversense | FreeStyle Libre 1, FreeStyle Libre 2 (optional alarms) |
| **Data transmission** | **Automatic** (Bluetooth/BLE to phone/receiver) | **Manual scan** (NFC) every ‚â§8 hours |
| **Real-time alerts** | **Yes** (predictive low/high, rate-of-change) | **Libre 1: No**; **Libre 2: Optional** (Bluetooth alarms only) |
| **Data completeness** | **High** (‚â•95% auto) | **User-dependent** (scan ‚â•8-hourly for 100%) |
| **Calibration** | Factory (no fingerstick) | Factory (no fingerstick) |
| **Sensor life** | 10 days (G6/G7, Guardian); 14 days (Libre 3); 180 days (Eversense) | 14 days (Libre 1/2) |

| **Integration with pumps** | **Full** (HCL: Tandem, Omnipod 5, CamAPS, 780G) | Libre 2/3 with some (Omnipod 5, CamAPS FX) |

| Aspect | rtCGM | isCGM |

## 4. NICE Guidance (NG17, NG18, TA updates)

### T1DM ‚Äî rtCGM Recommended (NG17 2022)
- **All adults with T1DM** should be offered rtCGM
- If rtCGM not suitable/tolerated ‚Üí consider isCGM (Libre 2)
- **Criteria**: HbA1c ‚â•58 mmol/mol (7.5%) OR recurrent hypos OR impaired awareness OR pregnancy planning

### T2DM on Insulin ‚Äî isCGM First-Line (NG28)
- **Offer isCGM (Libre 2)** to adults on insulin (basal ¬± bolus) with:
  - HbA1c ‚â•58 mmol/mol (7.5%) OR
  - Recurrent/severe hypos OR
  - Impaired hypo awareness OR
  - Need for intensive monitoring (pregnancy, renal impairment)
- **rtCGM** if isCGM not suitable or specific need (e.g., pump integration, carer alerts)

### Pregnancy
- **rtCGM preferred** for T1DM (CONCEPTT trial)
- isCGM acceptable if rtCGM unavailable

## 7. Patient Selection Guide

| Patient Profile | Preferred | Rationale |
|-----------------|-----------|-----------|
| **T1DM, frequent hypos, impaired awareness** | **rtCGM** (Dexcom G7, Guardian, Libre 3) | Predictive alerts prevent severe hypos |
| **T1DM on pump / wanting HCL** | **rtCGM** (pump-compatible) | Required for AID |
| **T2DM on basal-bolus, cost-conscious** | **isCGM (Libre 2)** | Lower cost, good HbA1c benefit |
| **T2DM on basal only** | **isCGM (Libre 2)** | NICE first-line |
| **Pregnancy T1DM** | **rtCGM** | CONCEPTT evidence |
| **Paediatric / carer remote monitoring** | **rtCGM** (Dexcom Share, Guardian Connect) | Remote alerts to carer |
| **Needle-phobic / minimal burden** | **Libre 3** (small, 1h warm-up, no scanner) | rtCGM convenience |
| **Longest sensor life** | **Eversense E3** (180 days) | Fewer insertions |
