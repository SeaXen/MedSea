## 7. Management

### Lifestyle Intervention (DPP Protocol)
| Component | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **Weight loss** | **≥7% body weight** (16-session curriculum) |
| **Physical activity** | **≥150 min/week** moderate intensity |
| **Diet** | Low-fat (<25% kcal), calorie restriction |
| **Behavioural** | Self-monitoring, stimulus control, problem-solving |

### Metformin Protocol
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Dose** | 850mg BD |
| **Adherence** | 72% at study end |
| **GI side effects** | 25% (vs 15% placebo) |

## 8. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary
| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Lifestyle** | 58% reduction; 7% weight loss; 150min/week exercise |
| **Metformin** | 31% reduction; less effective than lifestyle |
| **Age ≥60** | Lifestyle 71% (best), Metformin minimal |
| **BMI ≥35** | Metformin effective (53%) |
| **NNT** | Lifestyle 7, Metformin 14 (3 years) |

|-----------|---------------|
| **Metformin = lifestyle?** | NO - lifestyle 58%, metformin 31% |
