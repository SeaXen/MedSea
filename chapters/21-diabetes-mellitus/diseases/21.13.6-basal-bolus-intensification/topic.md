# Basal-Bolus Intensification

> [!info]
> **Basal-bolus = basal insulin + rapid-acting bolus at meals**; escalate when basal optimised (fbst 4–7) but HbA1c >58; **carb counting** essential; **ICR ~1u/10g**, **ISF ~2mmol/L per 2u**; pre-bolus 15min; QDS regimen; avoid fixed-dose regimens unless carb counting not possible.

## 2. Indications for Basal-Bolus

| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Basal optimised** | Fasting glucose **4–7 mmol/L** consistently |
| **HbA1c above target** | **>58 mmol/mol (7.5%)** despite basal + orals |
| **Post-prandial hyperglycaemia** | 2h PP >8.5 mmol/L consistently |
| **Variable eating pattern** | Irregular meals, shift work, carb variability |
| **High insulin requirement** | Basal >0.5u/kg or >50u/day |
| **Steroid-induced hyperglycaemia** | High post-prandial excursions |

---

## 3. Rapid-Acting Insulin Options

| Insulin | Onset | Peak | Duration | Notes |
|---------|-------|------|----------|-------|
| **Aspart** (NovoRapid) | 10–20 min | 1–3h | 3–5h | Standard |
| **Lispro** (Humalog) | 10–20 min | 1–2h | 3–5h | Standard |
| **Faster Aspart** (Fiasp) | **5–10 min** | 30–60 min | 3–5h | Niacinamide + arginine; better PP control |
| **Lispro-aabc** (Lyumjev) | **5–10 min** | 30–45 min | 3–5h | Citrate + treprostinil |
| **Human regular** (Actrapid) | 30–60 min | 2–4h | 6–8h | **Not preferred** (delayed peak) |

**Preferred**: Aspart, Lispro, **Fiasp/Lyumjev** for tighter PP control.

---

## 4. Initial Bolus Dose Calculation

### Carbohydrate Ratio (ICR — Insulin-to-Carb Ratio)
| Method | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| **Rule of 500** | **ICR = 500 / TDD** (total daily dose) → grams carb per 1 unit |
| **Rule of 300** (some use) | **ICR = 300 / TDD** → more aggressive |
| **Weight-based** | **1 unit per 10g carb** (starting point) |

**Starting ICR**: **1 unit per 10–15g carb** (adjust based on 2h PP glucose).

### Insulin Sensitivity Factor (ISF — Correction Factor)
| Method | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| **Rule of 100** (mmol/L) | **ISF = 100 / TDD** → mmol/L drop per 1 unit |
| **Rule of 1800** (mg/dL) | **ISF = 1800 / TDD** → mg/dL drop per 1 unit |

**Starting ISF**: **2 mmol/L drop per 2 units** (1u per 1mmol/L approximate).

---

## 5. Practical Initiation Protocol

### Step 1: Basal Optimisation (Already Done)
- Basal dose stable, fasting 4–7 mmol/L

### Step 2: Add First Bolus (Usually Largest Meal)
| Meal | Initial Bolus |
|------|---------------|
| **Breakfast** | Often largest; start here if PP highest |
| **Dinner** | If post-dinner PP highest |

**Dose**: ICR 1u/10g carb; pre-bolus 15min.

### Step 3: Add Second Bolus (After 1–2 Weeks)
- If HbA1c still > target, add bolus at next problematic meal

### Step 4: Full Basal-Bolus (QDS)
- Pre-breakfast, pre-lunch, pre-dinner, bedtime basal
- **Bedtime snack** if basal peak overnight

---

## 6. Titration Rules

| Parameter | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| **Bolus (if 2h PP >8.5)** | ↑ 1 unit per meal (or tighten ICR: 1u per 8g) |
| **Bolus (if 2h PP <4.0)** | ↓ 1 unit (or widen ICR: 1u per 12g) |
| **Basal (if fasting >7)** | ↑ 2–4u weekly |
| **Basal (if fasting <4)** | ↓ 2–4u weekly |
| **Correction (if CBG >10)** | Add ISF dose (e.g., 2u per 2mmol/L >10) |

**Avoid stacking**: Wait 3–4h between bolus corrections.

---

## 7. Pre-Mixed Insulin vs Basal-Bolus

| Scenario | Pre-Mixed (BD) | Basal-Bolus (QDS) |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Regular meals, fixed carbs** | Acceptable | Better flexibility |
| **Variable meals/carbs** | **Poor fit** | **Preferred** |
| **Carb counting ability** | Not needed | **Essential** |
| **Injection burden** | 2/day | 4–5/day |
| **Hypoglycaemia risk** | Higher (fixed ratios) | Lower (dose per meal) |
| **Post-prandial control** | Limited | Superior |

**NICE**: Pre-mixed only if **carb counting not feasible** and **regular meals**.

---

## 8. Carbohydrate Counting — Essentials

| Skill | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| **Read labels** | Total carb per serving (not "sugars") |
| **Weigh portions** | Digital scales for accuracy |
| **Apps/Resources** | Carbs & Cals, MyFitnessPal, DAFNE app |
| **Mixed meals** | Estimate protein/fat effect (delayed rise) |
| **Alcohol** | Carbs in beer/cider; spirits zero; hypoglycaemia risk later |

**Education**: **DAFNE principles** — refer for structured education if not competent.
