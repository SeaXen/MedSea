## 2. Definition & Epidemiology
| Feature | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Target** | Stage 2 T1DM (2+ autoantibodies + dysglycaemia) |
| **Teplizumab** | Anti-CD3 monoclonal antibody; FDA approved 2022 |
| **Efficacy** | Delays Stage 3 by median 2 years (48.4 vs 24.4 months) |
| **Eligibility** | Age ≥8; Stage 2 T1DM; not pregnant |
