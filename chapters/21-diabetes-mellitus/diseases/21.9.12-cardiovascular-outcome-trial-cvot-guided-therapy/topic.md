# Cardiovascular Outcome Trial (CVOT) Guided Therapy

> [!info]
> **FDA 2008 mandate**: All new T2DM drugs must demonstrate CV safety; **CVOTs transformed T2DM management** from glucose-centric to **comorbidity-centric**; **SGLT2i = CV/renal benefit** (EMPA-REG, CANVAS, CREDENCE, DAPA-HF, DELIVER); **GLP-1 RA = CV benefit** (LEADER, SUSTAIN-6, REWIND, FLOW); **DPP-4i = neutral** (SAVOR/EXAMINE: ↑ HF hosp); **agent selection by comorbidity**.

## 2. SGLT2 Inhibitor CVOTs — CV & Renal Benefit

| Trial | Drug | Population | Primary Outcome | Key Result |
|-------|------|------------|-----------------|------------|
| **EMPA-REG OUTCOME** (2015) | Empagliflozin | T2DM + ASCVD | 3P-MACE | **↓ 14%** (CV death 38%, HF hosp 35%) |
| **CANVAS Program** (2017) | Canagliflozin | T2DM + ASCVD/high risk | 3P-MACE | **↓ 14%** (HF hosp 33%); **↑ amputation** |
| **DECLARE-TIMI 58** (2019) | Dapagliflozin | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **Neutral MACE**; **↓ 17% CV death/HF hosp**; renal benefit |
| **VERTIS-CV** (2020) | Ertugliflozin | T2DM + ASCVD | 3P-MACE | **Neutral MACE**; ↓ HF hosp |
| **CREDENCE** (2019) | Canagliflozin | T2DM + DKD (eGFR 30–90, ACR 300–5000) | Renal composite | **↓ 30%** renal composite; ↓ CV death/HF hosp |
| **DAPA-CKD** (2020) | Dapagliflozin | CKD (eGFR 25–75, ACR 200–5000) ± T2DM | Renal composite | **↓ 39%** renal composite; ↓ CV death |
| **EMPA-KIDNEY** (2022) | Empagliflozin | CKD (eGFR 20–45 or 45–90 + ACR ≥200) ± T2DM | Renal composite | **↓ 28%** renal composite |
| **DAPA-HF** (2019) | Dapagliflozin | HFrEF (EF ≤40%) ± T2DM | CV death/HF hosp | **↓ 26%** (non-diabetic benefit) |
| **EMPEROR-Reduced** (2020) | Empagliflozin | HFrEF (EF ≤40%) ± T2DM | CV death/HF hosp | **↓ 25%** |
| **EMPEROR-Preserved** (2021) | Empagliflozin | HFpEF (EF >40%) ± T2DM | CV death/HF hosp | **↓ 21%** |
| **DELIVER** (2022) | Dapagliflozin | HFmrEF/pEF (EF >40%) ± T2DM | CV death/HF hosp | **↓ 18%** |

**Consistent message**: **SGLT2i = CV death ↓, HF hosp ↓, renal progression ↓** across spectrum.

## 3. GLP-1 Receptor Agonist CVOTs — CV Benefit

| Trial | Drug | Population | Primary Outcome | Key Result |
|-------|------|------------|-----------------|------------|
| **ELIXA** (2015) | Lixisenatide | T2DM + recent ACS | 3P-MACE | **Neutral** (no harm, no benefit) |
| **LEADER** (2016) | Liraglutide | T2DM + ASCVD/high risk | 3P-MACE | **↓ 13%** (CV death 22%, MI/Stroke neutral) |
| **SUSTAIN-6** (2016) | Semaglutide SC | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **↓ 26%** (non-fatal stroke 39%) |
| **EXSCEL** (2017) | Exenatide QW | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **Neutral** (trend CV death ↓) |
| **HARMONY** (2018) | Albiglutide | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **↓ 22%** (MACE) |
| **REWIND** (2019) | Dulaglutide | T2DM + ASCVD/risk (primary prev 31%) | 3P-MACE | **↓ 12%** (broad population incl. primary prev) |
| **PIONEER-6** (2019) | Oral Semaglutide | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **Neutral** (trend benefit) |
| **AMPLITUDE-O** (2021) | Efpeglenatide | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **↓ 27%** |
| **FLOW** (2024) | Semaglutide | T2DM + CKD (eGFR ≥50 + ACR ≥30 or eGFR <50) | Renal composite | **↓ 24%** renal composite; ↓ CV death |
| **STEP-HFpEF** (2023) | Semaglutide 2.4mg | HFpEF + obesity (BMI ≥30) ± T2DM | KCCQ-CSS + 6MWD | **↑ KCCQ-CSS 7.8pts; ↑ 6MWD 21.5m** |
| **FIGHT** (2017) | Liraglutide | HFrEF (EF ≤40%) ± T2DM | Hierarchical clinical | **Neutral** (no harm) |
| **LIVE** (2023) | Liraglutide | Post-MI + EF ≤40%/DM | CV death/HF hosp | **Neutral** |

**Key**: **GLP-1 RA = CV benefit** (liraglutide, semaglutide, dulaglutide, albiglutide, efpeglenatide); **weight loss** major driver; **HFpEF + obesity → semaglutide 2.4mg**.

## 4. DPP-4 Inhibitor CVOTs — Neutral / HF Risk

| Trial | Drug | Population | Primary Outcome | Key Result |
|-------|------|------------|-----------------|------------|
| **SAVOR-TIMI 53** (2013) | Saxagliptin | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **Neutral MACE**; **↑ 27% HF hosp** |
| **EXAMINE** (2013) | Alogliptin | T2DM + recent ACS | 3P-MACE | **Neutral MACE**; **↑ HF hosp (trend)** |
| **TECOS** (2015) | Sitagliptin | T2DM + ASCVD | 3P-MACE | **Neutral** (no HF signal) |
| **CARMELINA** (2018) | Linagliptin | T2DM + ASCVD/CKD | 3P-MACE + renal | **Neutral** (no HF signal; renal safe) |
| **CAROLINA** (2019) | Linagliptin vs Glimepiride | T2DM + ASCVD/risk | 3P-MACE | **Non-inferior** to SU; **↓ hypoglycaemia**, **↓ weight gain** |

**Key**: **DPP-4i = CV neutral**; **avoid saxagliptin/alogliptin in HF**; sitagliptin/linagliptin safe.

## 5. Other Agents

| Agent Class | Key CVOT | Result |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| **Sulfonylureas** | UKPDS (legacy), CAROLINA (vs linagliptin) | Neutral CV; ↑ hypoglycaemia, weight gain |
| **Thiazolidinediones** | PROactive (pioglitazone), RECORD (rosiglitazone) | **Pioglitazone: ↓ MACE**; **↑ HF hosp**; **Rosiglitazone: ↑ MI/CV death** (withdrawn) |
| **Insulin** | ORIGIN (glargine) | **Neutral CV** |
| **Metformin** | UKPDS (legacy) | **↓ MI, mortality** (subgroup) |
| **Bile acid sequestrants** | — | Neutral |

## 6. Comorbidity-Based Selection (CVOT Evidence)

| Comorbidity | **Preferred (CV Benefit)** | **Avoid** |
|-------------|---------------------------|-----------|
| **ASCVD** | **SGLT2i** (empagliflozin, canagliflozin) **+ GLP-1 RA** (liraglutide, semaglutide, dulaglutide) | Saxagliptin/alogliptin |
| **HFrEF** | **SGLT2i** (dapagliflozin, empagliflozin) | **TZDs (contraindicated)**, saxagliptin, alogliptin |
| **HFpEF** | **SGLT2i** (dapagliflozin, empagliflozin); **Semaglutide 2.4mg if obese** | TZDs |
| **CKD** | **SGLT2i** (dapa/empa ≥20), **GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide, dulaglutide, liraglutide), **Finerenone** | Metformin <30, SU, TZD |
| **Obesity** | **GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide, tirzepatide) | TZD, SU, insulin (weight gain) |
| **High hypoglycaemia risk** | **SGLT2i, GLP-1 RA, DPP-4i (linagliptin), TZD** | SU, insulin, meglitinides |

## 7. Algorithm Integration (ADA 2024 / KDIGO 2024)

```
T2DM + Metformin (if eGFR ≥30)
    │
    ├─► ASCVD / High CV Risk → **SGLT2i + GLP-1 RA**
    ├─► HF (HFrEF/HFpEF) → **SGLT2i** (first); add GLP-1 RA if obese HFpEF
    ├─► CKD (eGFR <60 or ACR ≥30) → **SGLT2i + GLP-1 RA + Finerenone**
    ├─► Obesity (BMI ≥30) → **GLP-1 RA** (semaglutide/tirzepatide)
    └─► No comorbidities → Individualised (cost, weight, hypo risk, preference)
```
