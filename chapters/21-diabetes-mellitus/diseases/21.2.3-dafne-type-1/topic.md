## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### DAFNE Curriculum (5 Days)
| Day | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **Day 1** | Principles of carb counting; insulin:carb ratios; correction doses |
| **Day 2** | Insulin sensitivity factor (ISF); blood glucose targets; pattern management |
| **Day 3** | Exercise; alcohol; sick day rules; hypoglycaemia management |
| **Day 4** | Eating out; travel; pregnancy; complications; pump basics |
| **Day 5** | Review; personal action plan; goal setting; follow-up |

### DAFNE-HART (Pump Users)
| Addition | Content |
|----------|---------|
| **Pump skills** | Basal rates, bolus wizard, temporary basal, suspend on low |
| **Advanced** | Dual wave/square bolus; activity settings; CGM integration |

## 7. Management

### Eligibility (NICE TA)
| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **Diagnosis** | T1DM ≥6 months |
| **Insulin regimen** | Basal-bolus (MDI) or CSII |
| **HbA1c** | >58 mmol/mol (7.5%) on MDI **OR** severe hypoglycaemia |
| **Motivation** | Willing to self-manage; attend 5-day course |

### Outcomes (Evidence)
| Outcome | DAFNE Effect |
|---------|--------------|
| **HbA1c** | ↓0.5-1.0% (5-11 mmol/mol) |
| **Severe hypoglycaemia** | ↓50-70% |
| **Quality of life** | Significant improvement |
| **Weight** | Neutral |
| **Diabetes distress** | Significant reduction |

### Quality Assurance
| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| **Educators** | Trained DAFNE educators (dietitians/nurses) |
| **Group size** | 6-8 participants |
| **Curriculum** | Standardised national curriculum |
| **Audit** | Mandatory annual outcome audit |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **DAFNE = pump training?** | NO - DAFNE = MDI skills; DAFNE-HART = pump skills add-on |
| **DAFNE = all T1DM?** | NO - only if HbA1c>58 or severe hypo; motivated |
| **DAFNE = weight loss?** | NO - weight neutral; focus on glycaemic control |
