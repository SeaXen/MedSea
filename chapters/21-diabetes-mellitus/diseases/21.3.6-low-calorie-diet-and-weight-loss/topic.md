# Low-calorie diet and weight loss

## 1. Learning Objectives
By the end of this note you should be able to:
- [ ] Apply very low-calorie diet (VLCD) and low-calorie diet (LCD) protocols

## 7. Management

### Protocol Phases
| Phase | Duration | Calories | Key Actions |
|-------|----------|----------|-------------|
| **1. VLCD/LCD** | 8-20 weeks | 800-850 kcal | Formula products; 2-3L water; multivitamin |
| **2. Reintroduction** | 2-8 weeks | Stepwise ↑ (200kcal/week) | Real food; dietitian-led |
| **3. Maintenance** | Lifelong | Individualised | Monthly review; weight monitoring |

### Contraindications
| Condition | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| **Pregnancy/lactation** | Nutrient inadequacy |
| **Severe psychiatric** | Anorexia, bulimia, depression |
| **Recent MI/stroke** | Metabolic stress |
| **Porphyria** | Trigger risk |
| **Uncontrolled gout** | Uric acid elevation |

### Complication Management
| Complication | Management |
|-------------|------------|
| **Cholelithiasis** | Ursodeoxycholic acid 500mg BD (prophylaxis) |
| **Gout** | Allopurinol if flare; hydration |
| **Hypotension** | Fluid/electrolyte replacement; reduce diuretics |
| **Hair loss** | Reassurance; adequate protein |
| **Cold intolerance** | Reassurance; adequate clothing |

## 9. Viva Questions
| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| **What is the difference between VLCD and LCD?** | VLCD <800 kcal (medical supervision); LCD 800-1200 kcal (dietitian supervision) |
| **What is the weight loss on VLCD?** | 1.5-2.5kg/week; ~15kg in 12 weeks |
| **What is the remission rate with ≥15kg weight loss?** | 86% (DiRECT) |
| **What is the cholelithiasis risk on VLCD?** | High; prophylaxis with ursodeoxycholic acid 500mg BD |
| **What is the reintroduction protocol?** | Stepwise increase 200kcal/week; dietitian-led; real food |
| **When is VLCD contraindicated?** | Pregnancy, eating disorders, recent MI/stroke, porphyria |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **VLCD = starvation?** | NO - formulated to provide adequate protein/micronutrients |
| **VLCD for everyone?** | NO - contraindications (pregnancy, eating disorders, recent MI) |
| **LCD = VLCD?** | NO - LCD 800-1200 kcal; VLCD <800 kcal |
