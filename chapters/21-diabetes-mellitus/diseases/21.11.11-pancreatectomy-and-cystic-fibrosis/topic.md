## 2. Definition & Epidemiology
| Feature | Post-pancreatectomy DM | CFRD |
|--------|------------------------|------|
| **Definition** | Diabetes after pancreatectomy (total/partial) | Diabetes in cystic fibrosis (CFTR mutation) |
| **Incidence** | Total pancreatectomy: 100%; Partial: 10-30% | 20% adolescents; 50% adults with CF |
| **Mechanism** | Surgical loss of islets + exocrine tissue | CFTR dysfunction -> thick mucus -> pancreatic destruction |

## 3. Clinical Features / Presentation

### Post-pancreatectomy DM
| Feature | Total | Partial |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Diabetes** | 100% | 10-30% |
| **Glucagon** | **Absent** (total) | Low |
| **Exocrine insuf.** | Yes | Variable |
| **Brittle** | Yes (no glucagon counter-regulation) | Less |

### CFRD
| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Age** | Increases with age; 20% adolescents, 50% adults |
| **Screening** | Annual OGTT from age 10 |
| **Diagnosis** | OGTT (FBG>=7.0, 2h>=11.1) or HbA1c>=48 (controversial) |
| **Insulin** | Only treatment (no oral agents) |
| **Exocrine insuf.** | ~85% (pancreatic insufficiency) |

## 3. Clinical Features / Presentation
| Feature | Post-pancreatectomy DM | CFRD |
|---------|------------------------|------|
| **Definition** | Post-surgical | CFTR mutation |
| **Glucagon** | Absent (total) | Low |
| **Exocrine insuf.** | Yes | ~85% |
| **Brittle** | Yes (no glucagon) | Variable |
| **Screening** | Post-op | Annual OGTT from age 10 |

## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### CFRD Classification
| Category | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **CFRD without fasting hyperglycaemia** | 2h OGTT >=11.1; FBG <7.0 |
| **CFRD with fasting hyperglycaemia** | FBG >=7.0 or 2h OGTT >=11.1 |
| **Impaired glucose tolerance** | FBG <7.0; 2h OGTT 7.8-11.0 |
| **Indeterminate** | Acute illness; on steroids |

## 5. Diagnosis & Investigations
| Test | Post-pancreatectomy | CFRD |
|------|---------------------|------|
| **OGTT** | Not used | **Gold standard** (annual from age 10) |
| **HbA1c** | Unreliable (post-op) | Falsely low (anaemia) |
| **C-peptide** | Undetectable (total) | Low |
| **Faecal elastase** | Low | Low |

## 6. Differential Diagnosis
| Condition | Distinguishing Features |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| **T1DM** | Autoantibodies +; no CFTR mutation |
| **T2DM** | Obese; insulin resistance; no CFTR |
| **Type 3c (other)** | Chronic pancreatitis, cancer |

## 7. Management

### Post-pancreatectomy DM
| Aspect | Total | Partial |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Glucagon** | **Absent** | Low |
| **Insulin** | Brittle; CSII preferred | Standard |
| **Enzyme replacement** | Essential (enteric-coated) | Variable |
| **Hypoglycaemia** | Severe (no glucagon) | Moderate |

### CFRD Management
| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Insulin only** | No oral agents |
| **Basal-bolus** | Standard; CSII common |
| **Excocrine enzymes** | Enteric-coated; dose by weight/meal |
| **Nutrition** | High calorie; high fat; unrestricted carbs |
| **Annual screening** | OGTT from age 10; HbA1c unreliable |

## 8. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary
| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Total pancreatectomy** | 100% DM; no glucagon = brittle; CSII essential |
| **CFRD** | Annual OGTT from age 10; insulin only; enzymes essential |
| **Glucagon** | Absent (total pancreatectomy); low (CFRD) |
| **Hypoglycaemia** | Severe in total pancreatectomy (no glucagon) |
| **CFRD screening** | Annual OGTT from age 10 |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **CFRD = T2DM?** | NO - insulin only, exocrine insufficiency, CFTR mutation |
| **HbA1c in CFRD?** | Falsely low (anaemia); use OGTT |
| **Total pancreatectomy = T1DM?** | Similar but no glucagon = brittle; no autoantibodies |
