## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

|--------------|----------------------|---------|-----------|----------|

| **G2** | 60-89 | 🟢 Low | 🟡 Mod | 🟠 High |

### Albuminuria Categories (ACR)
| Category | ACR (mg/mmol) | mg/g | Terminology |
|----------|---------------|------|-------------|
| **A1** | <3 | <30 | Normal/mildly increased |
| **A2** | 3-30 | 30-300 | Moderately increased (microalbuminuria) |
| **A3** | >30 | >300 | Severely increased (macroalbuminuria) |

### GFR Categories
| Category | eGFR | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| **G1** | ≥90 | Normal/high |
| **G2** | 60-89 | Mildly decreased |
| **G3a** | 45-59 | Mild-moderate |
| **G3b** | 30-44 | Moderate-severe |
| **G4** | 15-29 | Severely decreased |
| **G5** | <15 | Kidney failure |

## 5. Diagnosis & Investigations

### Screening Protocol
| Test | Method | Frequency | Action Threshold |
|------|--------|-----------|------------------|
| **ACR** | Spot urine (early morning) | Annual | A2: start ACEi/ARB; A3: max ACEi/ARB + SGLT2i |
| **eGFR** | CKD-EPI 2021 | Annual | G3b+: nephrology referral |
| **Urine microscopy** | If ACR >3 | As needed | RBC casts = non-diabetic |

### Confirmation Protocol
| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Abnormal ACR (≥3 mg/mmol) |
| **2** | Repeat 2/3 times over 3-6 months |
| **3** | Confirm persistent ACR ≥3 = DKD |

## 6. Differential Diagnosis
| Red Flag | Significance |
|----------|--------------|
| **Rapid eGFR decline** | >5 mL/min/1.73m²/year |
| **Active sediment** | RBC >10/µL, RBC casts |
| **No retinopathy** | Especially if DM >10y + A3 |
| **Systemic symptoms** | Fever, rash, arthralgia, haemoptysis |
| **Low BMI at diagnosis** | Type 2 DM usually overweight |

## 7. Management

### Proteinuria Reduction Targets
| Timepoint | Target | Action if Not Met |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| **3-6 months** | **↓ACR ≥30%** | Optimise ACEi/ARB; add SGLT2i |
| **12 months** | **ACR <30 mg/mmol (A2)** or **<3 mg/mmol (A1)** | Add finerenone (if A3 on ACEi+SGLT2i) |
| **Ongoing** | Sustained reduction | Monitor 3-12mo by KDIGO category |

### Monitoring Intervals by KDIGO Category
| Risk (Colour) | ACR Frequency | eGFR Frequency | Review |
|---------------|---------------|----------------|--------|
| **Green (Low)** | Annual | Annual | Primary care |
| **Yellow (Moderate)** | 6-monthly | 6-monthly | Primary care ± specialist |
| **Orange (High)** | 3-6 monthly | 3-6 monthly | Nephrology ± MDT |
| **Red (Very High)** | 1-3 monthly | 1-3 monthly | Nephrology + MDT |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Single ACR diagnostic?** | NO - biological variability high; need 2/3 samples in 3-6mo |
| **ACR vs PCR?** | ACR = albumin-specific (preferred for DKD); PCR = total protein |
| **HbA1c in CKD?** | Falsely LOW (↓RBC lifespan); use fructosamine/GA |
