## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **ART Metabolic Syndrome** | Protease inhibitors (lopinavir, atazanavir) → insulin resistance, dyslipidaemia, lipodystrophy · Integrase inhibitors (dolutegravir, bictegravir) → weight gain, ↑diabetes risk |
| **Insulin Resistance** | Chronic inflammation, mitochondrial toxicity, visceral adiposity, direct β-cell toxicity (older NRTIs) |
| **Screening** | ADA: Annual FPG/HbA1c in HIV on ART · Higher risk with PI, age >40, BMI >25, FH, HCV co-infection |
| **Management** | Prefer integrase inhibitors (less metabolic) · Metformin 1st line · SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA for CV/renal benefit · Monitor weight, lipids, glucose |
