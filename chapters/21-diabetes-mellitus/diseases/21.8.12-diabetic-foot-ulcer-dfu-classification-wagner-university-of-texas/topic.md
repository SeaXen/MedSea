# Diabetic Foot Ulcer (DFU) Classification (Wagner, University of Texas)

> [!info]
> **Wagner** = depth-based only (Grades 0–5); **University of Texas (UT)** = depth + ischaemia + infection (Grades 0–3, Stages A–D) — **UT superior for prognosis**; both guide management and amputation risk.

## 2. Wagner Classification (Depth-Based)

| Grade | Description | Features |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **0** | **Pre-ulcerative** | Intact skin; high risk (callus, deformity, healed ulcer) |
| **1** | **Superficial ulcer** | Involves skin/subcutaneous tissue only; no tendon/bone/joint |
| **2** | **Deep ulcer** | Penetrates to tendon, joint capsule, or deep fascia; **no bone** |
| **3** | **Ulcer with osteomyelitis** | Deep ulcer + **bone involvement** (probe-to-bone test +ve, X-ray/MRI changes) |
| **4** | **Partial foot gangrene** | Forefoot/midfoot gangrene |
| **5** | **Whole foot gangrene** | Extensive gangrene; limb-threatening |

**Limitations**: Does not account for **ischaemia** or **infection** severity — two major determinants of outcome.

---

## 3. University of Texas (UT) Classification — **Preferred**

### Grade (Depth) — Same as Wagner 0–3
| Grade | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **0** | Pre-ulcerative / healed ulcer |
| **1** | Superficial (no tendon/bone/joint) |
| **2** | Deep (tendon/joint capsule) |
| **3** | Deep + bone (osteomyelitis) |

### Stage (Ischaemia + Infection) — **Key Prognostic Factor**
| Stage | Ischaemia | Infection | Clinical Findings |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| **A** | **No** | **No** | Clean, non-ischaemic |
| **B** | No | **Yes** | Infected (purulence, erythema >2cm, warmth) |
| **C** | **Yes** | No | Ischaemic (absent pulses, low ABI/TcPO2) |
| **D** | **Yes** | **Yes** | Infected + ischaemic (highest amputation risk) |

**UT Notation**: e.g., **Grade 2, Stage D** = deep ulcer with ischaemia AND infection.

---

## 4. Why UT Superior to Wagner

| Aspect | Wagner | UT |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Ischaemia** | Not assessed | **Stage C/D = ischaemia** |
| **Infection** | Not assessed | **Stage B/D = infection** |
| **Prognosis** | Limited | **Stage predicts healing/amputation** |
| **Healing rate** | Not differentiated | Stage A: ~80%; B: ~60%; C: ~40%; D: ~20% |
| **Amputation risk** | Grade-based only | **Stage D → 50%+ major amputation** |
| **Management guidance** | Vague | Stage-based pathways |

**Evidence**: Multiple studies (Oyibo et al., 2001; Armstrong et al.) show UT stage independently predicts healing time, amputation, and resource use.

---

## 5. Combined Clinical Application

### Classification Determines Urgency
| UT Stage | Healing Probability | Amputation Risk | Referral Urgency |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **1A, 2A** | High (>80%) | Low | Routine podiatry (1–2w) |
| **1B, 2B** | Moderate (60–70%) | Moderate | MDT within 1w (infection control) |
| **1C, 2C** | Low (40–50%) | High | **Urgent vascular** (1–3 days) + MDT |
| **1D, 2D, 3D** | Very low (<30%) | **Very high (>50%)** | **Emergency MDT + vascular same day** |
| **3A, 3B, 3C** | Low | High | MDT 1w; osteomyelitis workup |

### Probe-to-Bone Test
- **Positive**: Hard, gritty bone palpable at ulcer base
- **Specificity >85%**, sensitivity ~60% for osteomyelitis
- **Grade 3** (Wagner) / **Grade 3** (UT) if positive

## 6. Other Classification Systems (Brief)

| System | Focus | Use |
|--------|-------|-----|
| **SINBAD** | Site, Ischaemia, Neuropathy, Bacterial infection, Area, Depth | Simple scoring (0–6); audit/research |
| **PEDIS** (IWGDF) | Perfusion, Extent, Depth, Infection, Sensation | **IWGDF guideline standard**; 4 grades per domain |
| **DEPA** | Depth, Extent, Phase (infection), Associated aetiology | Less common |

**PEDIS** is the **IWGDF recommended** system for international communication.

## 7. Exam Pearls (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Point |
|-------|-----------|
| **Wagner grades** | 0=pre-ulcer, 1=superficial, 2=deep to tendon/joint, 3=osteomyelitis, 4=forefoot gangrene, 5=whole foot gangrene |
| **UT grades** | Same depth 0–3 |
| **UT stages** | A=clean, B=infected, C=ischaemic, D=infected + ischaemic |
| **UT superiority** | Includes **ischaemia + infection** → predicts healing/amputation |
| **Stage D** | **Highest amputation risk** (>50% major amputation) |
| **Probe-to-bone** | +ve → osteomyelitis likely → Grade 3 |
| **IWGDF standard** | **PEDIS** classification |
| **Referral urgency** | Stage D = emergency MDT + vascular same day |
