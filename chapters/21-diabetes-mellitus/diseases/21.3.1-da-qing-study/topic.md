## 2. Definition & Epidemiology
| Feature | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Design** | Cluster RCT (33 clinics); 577 IGT participants |
| **Location** | Da Qing, China |
| **Duration** | 6 years active intervention; 20-year follow-up |
| **Population** | IGT (WHO 1985), age 25-57 |

## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### Intervention Arms
| Arm | Intervention | 6-year DM Reduction |
|-----|--------------|---------------------|
| **Diet** | Calorie restriction, vegetable ↑, alcohol ↓ | 31% |
| **Exercise** | ≥30 min/day moderate | 46% |
| **Diet + Exercise** | Combined | 42% |
| **Control** | Usual care | Reference |

### 20-Year Follow-up Outcomes
| Outcome | Intervention vs Control |
|---------|-------------------------|
| **Diabetes incidence** | 43% reduction (combined) |
| **CVD events** | 41% reduction (combined) |
| **CVD mortality** | 32% reduction (combined) |
| **All-cause mortality** | 29% reduction (combined) |

## 8. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary
| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Design** | Cluster RCT (China); 577 IGT; 6y intervention, 20y follow-up |
| **Arms** | Diet (31%), Exercise (46%), Diet+Exercise (42%) |
| **20y outcomes** | Combined: DM 43%↓, CVD events 41%↓, CVD death 32%↓, all-cause death 29%↓ |
| **Exercise alone** | Most effective single intervention (46%) |
| **Legacy effect** | Benefits persist 14y post-intervention |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Diet + Exercise = additive?** | NO - combined 42% vs exercise 46% (not additive) |
| **Da Qing = DPP?** | Different populations (China vs US); different interventions |
| **Diet alone effective?** | 31% reduction; less than exercise (46%) |
