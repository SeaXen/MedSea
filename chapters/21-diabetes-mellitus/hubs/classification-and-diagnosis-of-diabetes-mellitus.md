## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Facts |
|-------|-----------|
| **Diagnostic criteria (ADA/WHO)** | FPG ≥7.0 mmol/L · 2h-OGTT ≥11.1 mmol/L · HbA1c ≥48 mmol/mol (6.5%) · Random ≥11.1 + symptoms → **confirm with repeat unless unequivocal** |
| **Prediabetes** | IFG: FPG 6.1-6.9 · IGT: 2h-OGTT 7.8-11.0 · HbA1c 39-47 mmol/mol (5.7-6.4%) |
| **Type 1 vs Type 2** | Age, BMI, ketosis, autoantibodies, C-peptide, family history |
| **Monogenic red flags** | Age <25, non-obese, strong FH (3 generations), negative autoantibodies, non-insulin requiring |
| **GDM diagnosis** | IADPSG: 75g OGTT → FBG ≥5.1, 1h ≥10.0, 2h ≥8.5 mmol/L · NICE: risk factors + OGTT |
