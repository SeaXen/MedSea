## 4. Classification / Staging / Grading

### Screening Results Classification
| Category | Monofilament | Vibration | ANNS | Action |
|----------|--------------|-----------|------|--------|
| **Normal** | Feel all 6 sites | >10s at hallux | <3 | Annual rescreen |
| **Possible DSPN** | Miss 1–2 sites | 5–10s | 3–6 | Repeat 6 months; education |
| **LOPS (High risk)** | Miss ≥3 sites | <5s | ≥7 | **Refer podiatry/foot clinic**; IWGDF risk 2–3 |

### 10g Monofilament Technique
| Step | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Sites (6 per foot)** | Hallux pulp, 1st met head, 3rd met head, 5th met head, medial heel, lateral heel |
| **Application** | Perpendicular to skin; buckle (10g force); hold 1–2s |
| **Response** | Patient says "yes" when felt; eyes closed |
| **Pass** | Feel at all 6 sites |
| **Fail** | Cannot feel at ≥1 site |

### 128Hz Tuning Fork
| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Placement** | Bony prominence: hallux IP joint (dorsal), medial malleolus |
| **Activation** | Strike against hand/knee; do not strike hard surface |
| **Timing** | Patient indicates when vibration stops; examiner feels residual |
| **Normal** | >10 seconds at hallux IP joint |
| **Abnormal** | ≤10 seconds |

### ANNS (Annual Neuropathy Symptom Score)
| Question | Yes = 1 |
|----------|---------|
| 1. Numbness/tingling in feet? | |
| 2. Burning pain in feet? | |
| 3. Reduced sensation to touch? | |
| 4. Weakness in legs/feet? | |
| **Score ≥3** | Likely DSPN |

## 5. Diagnosis & Investigations
| Tool | Role |

## 7. Management

### Screening Algorithm
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Annual Diabetic Review] --> B[10g Monofilament (6 sites/foot)]
    B --> C{Feel all 6?}
    C -->|Yes| D[128Hz Tuning Fork (hallux IP)]
    C -->|No (≥1 missed)| E[LOPS = Missed ≥1 site]
    D --> F{>10s?}
    F -->|Yes| G[Normal — Annual rescreen]
    F -->|No (≤10s)| H[Possible DSPN — Repeat 6mo]
    E --> I[ANNS Questionnaire]
    H --> I
    I --> J{ANNS ≥3?}
    J -->|Yes| K[LOPS / High risk]
    J -->|No| L[Possible DSPN — Repeat 6mo]
    K --> M[Refer Podiatry / Foot Clinic]
    M --> N[IWGDF Risk 2-3]
    L --> O[Education + Repeat 6mo]
    G --> O
```

### Referral Pathways
| Finding | IWGDF Risk | Referral |
|---------|------------|----------|
| **Normal** | 0 | Annual rescreen |
| **Possible DSPN** (miss 1–2, vibration 5–10s, ANNS 3–6) | 1 | Podiatry; repeat 6 months |
| **LOPS** (miss ≥3, vibration <5s, ANNS ≥7) | 2–3 | **MDT Foot Clinic** |

### Patient Education (At Each Screen)
| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Daily foot inspection** | Check soles, between toes; mirror if needed |
| **Footwear** | Wide toe box, cushioned sole, no seams; avoid barefoot |
| **Skin care** | Moisturise (not between toes); no corn plasters |
| **Nail care** | Straight across; podiatry if difficult |
| **Red flags** | New lesion, colour change, swelling, heat, pain → urgent review |

## 8. FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Summary
| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Annual screen** | All DM (T1DM from Dx, T2DM from Dx) |
| **Tools** | 10g monofilament (6 sites), 128Hz tuning fork, ANNS |
| **Monofilament** | Hallux, 1st/3rd/5th met heads, medial/lateral heel; buckle = 10g |
| **Tuning fork** | 128Hz on hallux IP >10s normal; ≤10s abnormal |
| **ANNS** | 4 questions; score ≥3 = likely DSPN |
| **LOPS** | Miss ≥3 monofilament sites OR vibration <5s OR ANNS ≥7 → **refer podiatry** |
| **IWGDF risk** | 0: normal; 1: LOPS; 2: LOPS+PAD/deformity; 3: hx ulcer/amp |

## 9. Viva Questions
| Question | Expected Answer |
|----------|-----------------|
| **How do you perform 10g monofilament testing?** | Apply perpendicular to 6 sites/foot (hallux, 1st/3rd/5th met heads, medial/lateral heel); buckle to 10g; hold 1–2s; patient eyes closed says "yes" when felt |
| **What are the 6 sites for monofilament testing?** | Hallux pulp, 1st metatarsal head, 3rd metatarsal head, 5th metatarsal head, medial heel, lateral heel |
| **What is the normal vibration perception time with 128Hz tuning fork?** | **>10 seconds** at hallux interphalangeal joint |
| **What is the ANNS and how is it scored?** | 4 questions (numbness, burning, reduced touch, weakness); **score ≥3 = likely DSPN** |
| **What constitutes LOPS (loss of protective sensation)?** | Cannot feel monofilament at ≥3 sites OR vibration <5s OR ANNS ≥7 |
| **What is the referral pathway for abnormal screening?** | Normal → annual; Possible DSPN (miss 1–2, vibration 5–10s, ANNS 3–6) → repeat 6mo; LOPS → refer podiatry/MDT foot clinic |

## 10. Confusions & Mnemonics
| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Monofilament at 1 site only?** | NO — must test 6 sites/foot; single site misses patchy LOPS |
| **Tuning fork on soft tissue?** | NO — on bony prominence (hallux IP, medial malleolus) |
| **ANNS = diagnosis?** | NO — screening tool; clinical exam + NCS for diagnosis |
