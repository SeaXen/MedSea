## 1. Definition & Exam Relevance
**GAD65 autoantibodies** = antibodies against **glutamic acid decarboxylase 65 kDa isoform** (GAD2).  
**FCPS/MRCP relevance**: **Most sensitive single autoantibody for T1DM**; **primary marker for LADA**; exam questions on sensitivity, LADA diagnosis, neurological associations (stiff person syndrome).

---

## 2. Antigen Characteristics
| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Full name** | Glutamic Acid Decarboxylase 65 (GAD2 / GAD65) |
| **Function** | Catalyzes GABA synthesis from glutamate (neurotransmitter) |
| **Location** | Pancreatic β-cells, **brain (GABAergic neurons)**, adrenal medulla |
| **Molecular weight** | 65 kDa (vs GAD67 = 67 kDa, neuronal) |
| **Epitope** | Conformational (middle + C-terminal domains) |

---

## 3. Clinical Performance
| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Sensitivity (new T1DM)** | **70–80%** | Highest single Ab sensitivity |
| **Sensitivity (LADA)** | **70–80%** | **Primary LADA marker** |
| **Specificity** | 95–98% | High; low false positive rate |
| **Age distribution** | All ages | Unlike IA-2/IAA — not age-dependent |
| **Persistence** | **Years to decades** | Often persists lifelong |
| **Prevalence in healthy** | 1–2% | Low; increases with age |

---

## 4. Comparison with Other Autoantibodies
| Feature | **GAD65** | IA-2 | IAA | ZnT8 |
|---------|-----------|------|-----|------|
| **Sensitivity (T1DM)** | **70–80%** | 50–70% | 40–60% (kids) | 60–80% |
| **Sensitivity (LADA)** | **70–80%** | 20–30% | 20–30% | 40–50% |
| **Specificity** | 95–98% | >98% | 90–95% | 95–98% |
| **Age preference** | All ages | Children | Young children | All ages |
| **Persistence** | **Lifelong** | Declines | Declines | Persists |
| **Stiff person syndrome** | **High titre** | Negative | Negative | Negative |

---

## 5. LADA Diagnosis — GAD65 is Key
| Criterion | Role of GAD65 |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Age** | Typically >30 years (often 30–50) |
| **Presentation** | Non-ketotic, non-insulin requiring initially |
| **Autoantibodies** | **GAD65 positive in 70–80%**; IA-2/IAA/ZnT8 often negative |
| **Progression** | Insulin requirement within 6 months to 6 years |
| **HLA association** | DR3-DQ2 / DR4-DQ8 (similar to T1DM) |

**NICE/ADA**: Test GAD65 in all adults with new diabetes **not typical for T2DM** (lean, <50y, personal/family autoimmunity).

---

## 6. Neurological Associations
| Condition | GAD65 Titre | Clinical Link |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Stiff Person Syndrome (SPS)** | **Very high** (>100× diabetes titre) | Anti-GAD65 inhibits GABA synthesis → hyperexcitability |
| **Cerebellar ataxia** | High | GABAergic cerebellar dysfunction |
| **Limbic encephalitis** | High | Often paraneoplastic (thymoma, SCLC) |
| **Epilepsy** | Elevated | GABAergic inhibition loss |

**Exam pearl**: High-titre GAD65 + neurological symptoms → **think SPS / paraneoplastic** — screen for thymoma, SCLC.

---

## 7. Assay Methodology
| Method | Principle | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Radiobinding Assay (RBA)** | ³⁵S-methionine labelled GAD65 + precipitation | Gold standard; conformational epitopes preserved |
| **ELISA** | Recombinant human GAD65 (baculovirus/HEK293) coated plate | High throughput; may miss conformational epitopes |
| **Luciferase Immunoprecipitation (LIPS)** | Luciferase-GAD65 fusion + luminescence | High sensitivity; wide range |

**Standardization**: WHO International Standard (NIBSC 97/550); expressed in **U/mL** or **WHO units/mL**.

---

## 8. Interpretation Algorithm
```
New diabetes diagnosis
    ↓
GAD65 POSITIVE
    ├─ + IA-2 / ZnT8 / IAA → **T1DM confirmed** (multiple Abs)
    ├─ Adult, lean, non-ketotic, slow progression → **LADA** (GAD65 alone often sufficient)
    ├─ High titre + neuro symptoms → **Stiff Person Syndrome / Paraneoplastic** → screen malignancy
    └─ Elderly, overweight, typical T2DM → **False positive?** (1–2% healthy elderly); repeat + clinical correlation

GAD65 NEGATIVE
    ├─ IA-2/ZnT8/IAA positive → **T1DM still possible** (GAD65 neg in 20–30% T1DM)
    └─ All Abs negative → T2DM, monogenic, secondary diabetes
```

---

## 9. FCPS/MRCP Exam Pearls
- **GAD65 = most sensitive single Ab for T1DM** (70–80%)
- **GAD65 = primary marker for LADA** (70–80% positive; others often negative)
- **Persists lifelong** — unlike IA-2/IAA which decline
- **All ages** — not age-restricted like IA-2/IAA
- **High titre + neuro symptoms** = Stiff Person Syndrome (not just diabetes)
- **Paraneoplastic**: Thymoma, small cell lung cancer — screen if neuro signs
- **Not insulin-dependent** — can test anytime (unlike IAA)
- **Assay**: RBA gold standard; ELISA routine; conformational epitopes critical
- **False positive in elderly** (1–2%) — always correlate clinically
