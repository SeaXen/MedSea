## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Classification** | Level 1: <3.9 mmol/L (alert) · Level 2: <3.0 mmol/L (clinically significant) · Level 3: severe (requires assistance) |
| **Glucagon** | 1mg IM/SC adult · 0.5mg <25kg child · Nasal 3mg (Baqsimi) · Auto-injector (Gvoke) · IV dextrose 10-20% if IV access |
| **Hospital hypoglycaemia** | Common (insulin errors, NPO without dose adjustment, renal failure) · Insulin passports · Dose review protocols · CGM in hospital |
