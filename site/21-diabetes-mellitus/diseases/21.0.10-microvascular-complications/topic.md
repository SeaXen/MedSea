## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Topic | Key Facts |
|-------|-----------|
| **Retinopathy screening** | Type 1: 5y post-dx · Type 2: at dx · Annual if no DR · ETDRS grading (10-12 no DR, 20-35 mild/mod NPDR, 43-53 severe NPDR, 61+ PDR) |
| **DMO** | Centre-involving macular oedema · Anti-VEGF 1st line (ranibizumab/aflibercept) · Laser if non-centre |
| **DKD staging** | KDIGO: GFR G1-G5 × ACR A1-A3 (A1<3, A2 3-30, A3>30 mg/mmol) · Heat map for prognosis |
| **DKD management** | ACEi/ARB max tolerated · SGLT2i (eGFR ≥20) · Finerenone (eGFR ≥25, K+ ≤4.8) · GLP-1 RA |
| **DSPN** | Stocking-glove sensory loss · Painful (10-20%) · Duloxetine/pregabalin/gabapentin/amitriptyline |
| **Autonomic neuropathy** | CV: resting tachycardia, orthostatic hypotension, silent MI · GI: gastroparesis · GU: ED, bladder |
| **Foot risk (IWGDF)** | 0: no LOPS/PAD · 1: LOPS · 2: LOPS+PAD/deformity · 3: hx ulcer/amputation |
| **DFU classification** | Wagner 0-5 (depth) · UT Grade 0-3 × Stage A-D (infection/ischaemia) |
| **DFU management** | TCC gold standard offloading · PEDIS infection grading · Revascularisation if PAD · MDT |
| **Charcot** | Eichenholtz 0-3 · Hot swollen foot, preserved pulses · Immobilisation (TCC) · Bisphosphonates? |
