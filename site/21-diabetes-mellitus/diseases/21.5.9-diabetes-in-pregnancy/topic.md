## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **GDM Screening** | IADPSG: Universal 75g OGTT 24-28wk (FBG≥5.1, 1h≥10.0, 2h≥8.5) · NICE: Risk factors → OGTT · ADA: Universal or risk-based |
| **GDM Targets** | FBG <5.3, 1h <7.8, 2h <6.4 mmol/L · HbA1c not recommended for monitoring |
| **GDM Management** | Diet 1st → Metformin (if FBG >5.5 or 1h >7.0) → Insulin · Glyburide alternative |
| **Pre-existing DM** | Pre-conception HbA1c <48mmol/mol (6.5%) · Folic acid 5mg OD to 12wk · Stop ACEi/ARB/statin/SGLT2i/GLP-1 RA |
| **Postpartum** | OGTT 6-13 weeks · Annual HbA1c · Lifetime T2DM risk 50% |
