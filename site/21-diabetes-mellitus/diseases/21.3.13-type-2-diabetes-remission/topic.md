## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **DiRECT Trial** | Low-calorie diet (825-853 kcal/d) 12-20wk → food reintroduction → maintenance · 46% remission at 1y, 36% at 2y, 23% at 5y |
| **Remission Criteria** | HbA1c <48mmol/mol (6.5%) off ALL glucose-lowering medications for ≥3 months (ADA/Diabetes UK consensus 2021) |
| **Low-Calorie Diet** | Formula meal replacements (soups/shakes) · ~15kg mean weight loss → remission · Structured support essential |
| **Predictors** | Shorter duration (<6y), lower baseline HbA1c, greater weight loss, C-peptide preserved |
| **Maintenance** | Continued weight maintenance, regular physical activity, monthly support, self-monitoring · Relapse if weight regain |
