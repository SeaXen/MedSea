## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **HbA1c in CKD** | Falsely LOW in CKD/ESRD (↓RBC lifespan, ESA use) · Use fructosamine (2-3wk avg) or glycated albumin (3wk) · Target HbA1c may be misleading |
| **Drug Dosing (eGFR)** | Metformin: ≥45 full, 30-45 half, <30 contraindicated · SGLT2i: initiate ≥45/30, continue ≥20/15 · GLP-1 RA: no adjustment · DPP-4i: adjust (linagliptin no adjust) · SU: avoid glibenclamide, gliclazide OK · Insulin: ↓25-50% |
| **Dialysis** | Glucose-free dialysate → hypoglycaemia risk · Insulin requirements variable (pre/post dialysis) · Monitor pre/post dialysis glucose · Peritoneal dialysis: glucose absorption from dialysate |
