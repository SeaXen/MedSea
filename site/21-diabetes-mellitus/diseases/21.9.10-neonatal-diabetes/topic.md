## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Definition** | Diabetes diagnosed <6 months of age |
| **Genetics** | KCNJ11 (Kir6.2) ~40% · ABCC8 (SUR1) ~10% · 6q24 imprinting (transient) · INS, GCK, PDX1, PTF1A, FOXP3, EIF2AK3 (Wolcott-Rallison) |
| **Transient (TNDM)** | Remission by 18 months, relapse in adolescence/adulthood · 6q24 methylation defect (70%) · KCNJ11/ABCC8 activating mutations |
| **Permanent (PNDM)** | Lifelong · KCNJ11/ABCC8 (most common) · INS, GCK, PDX1, PTF1A, EIF2AK3 |
| **Sulfonylurea Response** | **KCNJ11/ABCC8: 90% switch successfully** (tolbutamide/gliclazide) · Dose 0.5-1.0mg/kg/day · Neurological features may persist (DEND syndrome) |
