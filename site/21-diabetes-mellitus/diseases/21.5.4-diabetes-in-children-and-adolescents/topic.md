## High-Yield Exam Points (FCPS/MRCP)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **T1D in Children** | Peak 10-14y · DKA at dx 25-30% · HbA1c target <53mmol/mol (7.0%) · Family-centred care, school plan |
| **T2D in Youth** | Rising with obesity · Age 10-19y, puberty, FH, acanthosis nigricans · Metformin 1st line · GLP-1 RA/SGLT2i if HbA1c >75 |
| **Monogenic in Children** | Neonatal DM <6mo (KCNJ11/ABCC8) · MODY (GCK: no rx, HNF1A: SU responsive) · Genetic testing if probability >6% |
| **Transition** | Structured programme age 16-18 · Joint paediatric-adult clinics · DAFNE-Youth · Psychosocial support |
