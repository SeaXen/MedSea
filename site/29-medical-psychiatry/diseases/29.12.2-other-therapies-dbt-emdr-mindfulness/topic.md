# Other therapies (DBT, EMDR, mindfulness)

---

## Definition

Other evidence-based psychotherapies include dialectical behaviour therapy (DBT) for BPD, eye movement desensitisation and reprocessing (EMDR) for PTSD, mindfulness-based therapies, and motivational interviewing for substance use.

## Core Clinical Content

### DBT for BPD
Individual + group therapy + phone coaching; 4 modules: mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, interpersonal effectiveness

### DBT duration
1-3 years; weekly individual + group sessions

### DBT key skills
DEAR MAN (interpersonal), TIPP (distress tolerance), PLEASE (emotion regulation)

### EMDR
Eye movement desensitisation and reprocessing; for PTSD; 8 phases

### EMDR mechanism
Bilateral stimulation (eye movements) while recalling traumatic memory; debated

### Mindfulness-based stress reduction (MBSR)
8-week program; for chronic stress, anxiety, depression relapse

### Motivational interviewing (MI)
For substance use; collaborative, evocative, autonomy-supporting

### MI principles
Express empathy, develop discrepancy, roll with resistance, support self-efficacy

### Exposure therapy for PTSD
Prolonged exposure (PE), cognitive processing therapy (CPT)

### EMDR vs exposure
EMDR includes bilateral stimulation; PE is pure exposure; both effective for PTSD
