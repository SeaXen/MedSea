## Key Themes for FCPS/MRCP

- **Mnemonics**: SIG E CAPS (depression), DIG FAST (mania), 4 A's (capacity), SAD PERSONS (suicide risk)
- **Investigations**: MSE, capacity assessment, risk assessment
- **Pharmacology**: SSRIs/SNRIs first-line; lithium for bipolar; clozapine for treatment-resistant schizophrenia
- **Emergencies**: NMS, serotonin syndrome, rapid tranquilisation
- **Legal**: MHA, MCA, capacity, consent
- **Special populations**: perinatal, elderly, adolescent, medically ill
