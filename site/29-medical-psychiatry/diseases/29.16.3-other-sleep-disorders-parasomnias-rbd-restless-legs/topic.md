# Other sleep disorders (parasomnias, RBD, restless legs)

---

## Definition

Other sleep disorders include parasomnias (abnormal behaviours during sleep — NREM and REM), REM sleep behaviour disorder (RBD), and restless legs syndrome (RLS), each with distinct features and management.

## Core Clinical Content

### Parasomnias
Abnormal behaviours/emotions during sleep (NREM: sleepwalking, terrors; REM: REM behaviour disorder)

### Sleepwalking (somnambulism)
NREM parasomnia; complex behaviour during deep sleep; amnesia

### Sleep terrors
NREM parasomnia; screaming, autonomic arousal, no memory; common in children

### REM sleep behaviour disorder (RBD)
Loss of normal REM atonia; acting out dreams; risk of injury

### RBD association
Strong marker for α-synucleinopathies (Parkinson's, DLB, MSA)

### RBD treatment
Clonazepam, melatonin; bed safety

### Restless legs syndrome (RLS)
Urge to move legs, worse at rest/evening, relieved by movement

### RLS associations
Iron deficiency (low ferritin), pregnancy, CKD, peripheral neuropathy

### RLS treatment
Iron replacement if ferritin <75; dopamine agonists (pramipexole, ropinirole); gabapentin

### Night terrors vs nightmares
Night terrors: NREM, no recall; nightmares: REM, vivid recall
