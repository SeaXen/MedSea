## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Focus
- **SLE**: 2019 EULAR/ACR criteria (ANA entry + weighted domains), anti-dsDNA/anti-Sm specific, lupus nephritis (ISN/RPS class), antiphospholipid syndrome overlap
- **Systemic sclerosis**: limited (CREST) vs diffuse; autoantibodies (centromere=limited, Scl-70=diffuse); pulmonary fibrosis, PAH, renal crisis (ACEi!)
- **Sjögren's**: sicca (dry eyes/mouth), Ro/La, lymphoma risk (MALT), focus score on lip biopsy
- **MCTD**: high-titre anti-U1-RNP, overlap SLE/SSc/PM, pulmonary hypertension, Raynaud's
- **Polymyositis/Dermatomyositis**: proximal weakness, CK/AST/ALT, anti-Jo1 (antisynthetase), heliotrope/Gottron's, malignancy screen (esp DM)
- **Antiphospholipid syndrome**: clinical (thrombosis, pregnancy morbidity) + lab (LA, aCL, anti-β2GPI); warfarin target INR 2.5-3.5; obstetric APS needs LMWH + aspirin
- **Adult-onset Still's**: quotidian fever, salmon-pink rash, arthralgia, ferritin >> normal, glycogenated fraction <20%
- **Relapsing polychondritis**: ear/nose chondritis, airway involvement, autoimmune associations
