## FCPS/MRCP High-Yield Topics (Priority Order)

### Must Know (Core)
1. Rheumatoid Arthritis — diagnosis, classification, DMARDs, biologics
2. Systemic Lupus Erythematosus — classification, organ involvement, management
3. Osteoarthritis — diagnosis, management, red flags
4. Gout — acute attack, prophylaxis, urate-lowering therapy
5. Osteoporosis — DEXA, FRAX, bisphosphonates, denosumab
6. Septic Arthritis — urgent diagnosis, empiric antibiotics
7. Polymyalgia Rheumatica / Giant Cell Arteritis — diagnosis, steroids
8. Vasculitis — ANCA patterns, GPA vs MPA vs EGPA, PAN
9. Ankylosing Spondylitis / Spondyloarthritis — HLA-B27, ASAS criteria
10. Psoriatic Arthritis — CASPAR criteria, domains

### Should Know (High Yield)
11. Crystal arthropathies — CPPD, basic calcium phosphate
12. Systemic Sclerosis — limited vs diffuse, organ involvement
13. Sjögren's Syndrome — sicca, lymphoma risk
14. Polymyositis/Dermatomyositis — malignancy association
15. Antiphospholipid Syndrome — criteria, pregnancy morbidity
16. Osteomalacia / Rickets — vitamin D deficiency
17. Paget's Disease — ALP, bisphosphonates
18. Reactive Arthritis / Post-infectious
18. PMR vs GCA overlap
19. Kawasaki Disease — coronary aneurysms

### Nice to Know (Advanced)
20. Behçet's Disease
21. Relapsing Polychondritis
22. Adult-onset Still's Disease
23. IgG4-related Disease
24. Complex Regional Pain Syndrome
25. Fibromyalgia / Chronic Widespread Pain

## Study Sequence (Recommended)

### Phase 1: Foundations (Week 1-2)
- Clinical Approach (History, GALS, Investigations, Drugs)
- RA, SLE, OA, Gout, Osteoporosis

### Phase 2: Inflammatory & Crystal (Week 3-4)
- Spondyloarthritis (AS, PsA, ReA, Enteropathic)
- Crystal (CPPD, BCP)
- Autoimmune (SSc, Sjögren's, MCTD, Myositis, APS)

### Phase 3: Vasculitis & Bone (Week 5)
- GCA/PMR, ANCA vasculitides, PAN, Kawasaki
- Osteomalacia, Paget's, Renal osteodystrophy

### Phase 4: Infections & Soft Tissue (Week 6)
- Septic arthritis, Rheumatic fever, Osteomyelitis
- Fibromyalgia, Regional soft tissue, CRPS

### Phase 5: Integration & Revision (Week 7-8)
- Mixed cases, Viva practice, SBA banks
- DEXA, X-ray, US, MRI interpretation
- Drug charts (DMARDs, biologics, steroids)
