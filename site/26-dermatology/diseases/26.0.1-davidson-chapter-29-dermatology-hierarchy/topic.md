### 6. Skin Infections (6 Heading Hubs, 22 Disease Topics)

#### 6.1 Bacterial Infections
- Impetigo (non-bullous, bullous)
- Ecthyma

- Erysipelas

- Necrotising fasciitis
- Staphylococcal scalded skin syndrome (see 4.4)
- Erythrasma
- Cutaneous tuberculosis
- Leprosy (Hansen's disease)

#### 6.2 Viral Infections
- Herpes simplex (HSV-1, HSV-2)
- Herpes zoster (shingles)
- Varicella (chickenpox)
- Eczema herpeticum (Kaposi varicelliform eruption)
- Molluscum contagiosum
- Warts (verrucae - common, plantar, plane, genital)
- Hand-foot-mouth disease (coxsackie)

- Gianotti-Crosti syndrome

#### 6.3 Fungal Infections
- Dermatophytoses (tinea capitis, corporis, cruris, pedis, unguium)
- Candidiasis (intertrigo, oral, vulvovaginal, chronic mucocutaneous)
- Pityriasis (tinea) versicolor
- Majocchi granuloma
- Deep fungal infections (sporotrichosis, chromoblastomycosis)

#### 6.4 Parasitic Infestations
- Scabies
- Cutaneous larva migrans
- Tungiasis
- Myiasis

- Onchocerciasis

#### 6.5 Sexually Transmitted Infections (Cutaneous)

- Lymphogranuloma venereum (LGV)

- Genital herpes (see 6.2)
- Genital warts (see 6.2)

#### 6.6 Atypical & Opportunistic Infections
- Atypical mycobacteria (M. marinum, M. ulcerans)
- Nocardia
- Immunocompromised host skin infections
- Post-transplant skin infections

### 10. Dermatological Manifestations of Systemic Disease (4 Heading Hubs, 14 Disease Topics)

#### 10.1 Connective Tissue Diseases
- Systemic lupus erythematosus (cutaneous)
- Dermatomyositis (heliotrope, Gottron, mechanic's hands, Shawl/V-sign)
- Systemic sclerosis (limited, diffuse, calcinosis, telangiectasia)
- Mixed connective tissue disease
- Sjögren syndrome (cutaneous)
- Antiphospholipid syndrome (cutaneous)

#### 10.2 Vasculitis Syndromes (see also 3.4)
- Polyarteritis nodosa (cutaneous)
- Granulomatosis with polyangiitis (cutaneous)
- Eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis
- Microscopic polyangiitis (cutaneous)
- Cryoglobulinaemic vasculitis (see 3.4)
- IgA vasculitis (see 3.4)

#### 10.3 Metabolic, Nutritional & Endocrine
- Diabetes mellitus (necrobiosis lipoidica, diabetic dermopathy, acanthosis nigricans)
- Thyroid disease (myxoedema, pretibial myxoedema, thyroid acropachy)
- Cushing syndrome
- Acromegaly
- Porphyria cutanea tarda
- Ehlers-Danlos syndromes (cutaneous)
- Marfan syndrome (striae)
- Nutritional deficiencies (zinc, biotin, essential fatty acids, vitamin C)

#### 10.4 Other Systemic Associations
- Sarcoidosis (cutaneous)
- Amyloidosis (cutaneous)
- Paraneoplastic dermatoses (acanthosis nigricans, dermatomyositis, Leser-Trélat, Sweet syndrome, Bazex)
- Internal malignancy skin signs
