## 📝 Exam Answer Templates

### Viva: "Describe the diagnostic reasoning process"
1. **History + Exam** → Clinical hypothesis generation (pattern recognition vs analytical)
2. **Pre-test probability** (epidemiology, risk factors, clinical gestalt)
3. **Test selection** (SnNout for rule-out, SpPin for rule-in)
4. **Apply Likelihood Ratios** → Calculate post-test probability (Fagan nomogram)
5. **Compare to thresholds**: Test threshold vs Treatment threshold
6. **Cognitive debiasing**: Consider alternatives, seek disconfirming evidence, use checklists
7. **Document** reasoning and uncertainty

### Viva: "How do you appraise an RCT?"
**Validity**: Randomisation (sequence generation, allocation concealment), Blinding (participant, personnel, outcome assessor), ITT analysis, Follow-up completeness (>80%), Selective reporting
**Results**: Effect size (RR/OR/HR), Absolute measures (ARR, NNT), Precision (95% CI), Subgroup analysis (pre-specified)
**Applicability**: Population (PICO match), Intervention feasibility, Outcomes relevance, Harms vs Benefits

### Viva: "SPIKES for breaking bad news"
**S**etting: Privacy, time, no interruptions, support person present
**P**erception: "What do you understand about your condition?"
**I**nvitation: "How much detail would you like?"
**K**nowledge: Warning shot → Information in chunks → Check understanding → Avoid jargon
**E**motions: Observe, name, validate, empathise ("I can see this is devastating")
**S**trategy/Summary: Agree next steps, offer follow-up, provide written info, involve team

### SAQ: "QI Project: Reduce time to antibiotics in sepsis"
**PDSA**: Plan (Driver diagram → Aim: 100% antibiotics <1h; Primary: Time to abx; Secondary: Mortality, LOS; Process: Sepsis 6 completion), Do (Pilot on 1 ward), Study (Run chart → median ↓ from 90 to 45 min), Act (Spread to all wards)
**Measures**: Outcome (30-day mortality), Process (Time to abx, Lactate drawn), Balancing (C. difficile, antibiotic resistance)
**Sustainability**: Embed in EHR order set, Staff education, Audit schedule

### Viva: "Mental Capacity Act — 2-stage test"
**Stage 1**: Impairment of mind/brain? (Dementia, delirium, LD, brain injury, intoxication)
**Stage 2**: Unable to: (a) Understand, (b) Retain, (c) Use/weigh, (d) Communicate decision
→ If lacks capacity → Best interests (past wishes, beliefs, consult family/IMCA, least restrictive)
→ **DoLS** if continuous supervision + not free to leave → Authorisation required
→ **IMCA** if serious medical treatment / accommodation change + no family/friends
