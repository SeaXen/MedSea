> [!tip] **FCPS/MRCP Priority: HIGH**
> **Metastasis = Cause of 90% Cancer Deaths**; **Invasion-Metastasis Cascade**: **Local Invasion (EMT, Proteolysis, Motility) → Intravasation → Survival in Circulation (CTCs, Platelet Cloaking, Immune Evasion) → Extravasation → Micrometastasis → Colonisation (MET, Angiogenesis, Stemness)**; **Seed & Soil Hypothesis** (Paget); **Organotropism**: Lung (Breast, Colon, Sarcoma), Liver (GI, Pancreas, Breast), Bone (Breast, Prostate, Lung, Thyroid, Kidney), Brain (Lung, Breast, Melanoma, RCC); **Lymphatic Spread**: Sentinel Node, Nodal Mets; **Haematogenous Spread**: Portal Vein (GI → Liver), Systemic Arterial; **Dormancy**: Cellular (G0/G1 Arrest) vs Micrometastatic (Angiogenic Switch); **Tumour Budding** = Poor Prognosis.

## 2. 2. Invasion-Metastasis Cascade

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Primary Tumour] --> B[**1. Local Invasion**]
    B --> B1[EMT: E-cadherin ↓, N-cadherin ↑, Vimentin ↑]
    B --> B2[Proteolysis: MMPs, uPA, Cathepsins]
    B --> B3[Motility: Rho/ROCK, Integrins, FAK, Src]
    B1 --> C[**2. Intravasation**]
    B2 --> C
    B3 --> C
    C --> C1[Basement Membrane Breach]
    C --> C2[Endothelial Transmigration]
    C --> C3[**CTCs in Circulation**]
    C1 --> D[**3. Survival in Circulation**]
    C2 --> D
    C3 --> D
    D --> D1[Platelet Cloaking (TF, P-selectin)]
    D --> D2[Immune Evasion (NK, CD8+ T)]
    D --> D3[Shear Stress Resistance]
    D1 --> E[**4. Extravasation**]
    D2 --> E
    D3 --> E
    E --> E1[Endothelial Adhesion (Selectins, Integrins)]
    E --> E2[Transendothelial Migration]
    E --> E3[**Micrometastasis**]
    E1 --> F[**5. Colonisation**]
    E2 --> F
    E3 --> F
    F --> F1[MET: Mesenchymal → Epithelial]
    F --> F2[Angiogenesis (VEGF)]
    F --> F3[Cancer Stem Cells (CSCs)]
    F --> F4[Organ-Specific Adaptation]
    F1 --> G[**Macrometastasis**]
```

### 1. Step-by-Step Breakdown

| Step | Key Events | Molecular Mediators |
|------|------------|---------------------|
| **1. Local Invasion** | EMT, ECM Degradation, Motility | **EMT-TFs**: SNAIL, SLUG, TWIST, ZEB1; **E-cadherin loss**, **N-cadherin/Vimentin gain**; **MMPs (2, 9, 14)**, uPA, Cathepsins; **Rho/ROCK**, Integrins, FAK, Src |
| **2. Intravasation** | Basement Membrane Breach, Endothelial Crossing | **VEGFR2**, ANGPT2, **CXCR4/CXCL12**, **ICAM-1/VCAM-1**, **VE-Cadherin** |
| **3. Circulation Survival** | Platelet Cloaking, Immune Evasion, Shear Stress | **Platelets**: TF, P-Selectin, TGF-β; **CTC Clusters** (Higher Metastatic Potential); **NK Evasion** (MHC-I Downregulation, PD-L1); **Anoikis Resistance** (Integrin Switching, PI3K/AKT) |
| **4. Extravasation** | Endothelial Adhesion, Transmigration | **Selectins (E/P-Selectin)**, **Integrins (α4β1, αLβ2)**, **ICAM-1/VCAM-1**, **CXCL12/CXCR4** |
| **5. Colonisation** | MET, Angiogenesis, CSC Expansion, Niche Adaptation | **MET**: E-cadherin Restoration; **VEGF**, FGF, Angiopoietins; **CSCs** (CD44, CD133, ALDH1); **Organ-Specific Factors** |

## 3. 3. Epithelial-Mesenchymal Transition (EMT) & MET

### 1. EMT: Molecular Hallmarks

| Feature | Epithelial | Mesenchymal |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **Adhesion** | **E-Cadherin High** | **E-Cadherin Low**, **N-Cadherin High** |
| **Cytoskeleton** | **Cytokeratins** | **Vimentin**, Fibronectin |
| **Polarity** | Apical-Basal | Front-Rear |
| **Motility** | Immobile | Migratory, Invasive |
| **TFs** | — | **SNAIL (SNAI1)**, **SLUG (SNAI2)**, **TWIST1**, **ZEB1**, **ZEB2**, **FOXC2** |
| **Signalling** | — | **TGF-β**, **Wnt/β-catenin**, **Notch**, **HIF-1α**, **NF-κB**, **RAS/ERK**, **PI3K/AKT** |

### 2. EMT Spectrum & Plasticity

| State | Description | Clinical Relevance |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Epithelial** | Full E-cadherin, Polarised | Primary Tumour Core |
| **Partial/ Hybrid EMT** | Co-expression (E-cad + Vimentin) | **Highest Metastatic Potential**, **Stemness**, **Therapy Resistance** |
| **Mesenchymal** | Full Vimentin, Migratory | Circulating Tumour Cells (CTCs) |
| **MET (Mesenchymal-Epithelial Transition)** | Reversion to Epithelial | **Colonisation**, **Macrometastasis Formation** |

> **EMT is Reversible & Dynamic** — **Plasticity** enables adaptation; **Partial EMT** = **Most Aggressive**

## 4. 4. Seed & Soil Hypothesis & Organotropism

### 1. Paget's Seed & Soil (1889)

> **"When a plant goes to seed, its seeds are carried in all directions; but they can only live and grow if they fall on congenial soil."** — **Stephen Paget**

- **Seed** = **Cancer Cell** (Intrinsic Properties: Mutations, EMT, Stemness)
- **Soil** = **Target Organ Microenvironment** (Permissive Factors: Growth Factors, ECM, Immune Landscape, Vascular Architecture)

### 2. Organotropism: Common Metastatic Patterns

| Primary Cancer | Common Metastatic Sites | Mechanism |
|----------------|------------------------|-----------|
| **Breast** | **Bone (>70%)**, Lung, Liver, Brain | **CXCR4/CXCL12** (Bone Marrow), **Integrin αvβ3**, **PTHrP** (Osteolysis) |
| **Prostate** | **Bone (>90%)**, Lymph Nodes, Lung | **PSA**, **Bone Sialoprotein**, **CXCR4**, **ETS Fusion** |
| **Lung (NSCLC)** | **Brain, Bone, Liver, Adrenal, Contralateral Lung** | **EGFR/ALK** → Brain; **Integrin αvβ3** → Bone |
| **Colorectal** | **Liver (Portal Vein) > Lung > Peritoneum** | **Portal VEnous Drainage** → Liver First Pass; **CDX2**, **CXCR4** |
| **Melanoma** | **Lung, Liver, Brain, Bone, Skin/Subcut** | **Integrin α4β1 (VCAM-1)**, **CXCR4**, **MCAM/MUC18** |
| **Renal (RCC)** | **Lung, Bone, Liver, Brain, Adrenal** | **VHL Loss → HIF/VEGF**, **CXCR4**, **CAIX** |
| **Gastric** | **Peritoneum, Liver, Lung, Bone**, Ovary (Krukenberg) | **Transcoelomic Spread** (Peritoneal), **Haematogenous** |
| **Ovarian** | **Peritoneum (Transcoelomic) > Liver, Lung** | **Transcoelomic**, **MUC16/CA125**, **HER2** |
| **Thyroid (DTC)** | **Lung, Bone** | **TSH Receptor**, **Thyroglobulin** |
| **Neuroblastoma** | **Bone Marrow, Liver, Skin, Bone** | **GD2**, **Integrin α4β1** |

### 3. Modern Organotropism Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Exosome Integrins** | **α6β4/α6β1 → Lung**, **αvβ5 → Liver**, **αvβ3 → Brain**, **α2β1 → Bone** |
| **Chemokine Receptors** | **CXCR4/CXCL12** (Bone Marrow, Lymph Node), **CCR7/CCL19/21** (Lymph Node), **CXCR3/CXCL9-11** (Inflamed Tissues) |
| **Adhesion Molecules** | **Integrin αvβ3** (Bone, Brain), **α4β1/VCAM-1** (Bone Marrow, Lung), **E-Selectin/CD44** (Liver, Lung) |
| **Organ-Specific Growth Factors** | **PTHrP/TGF-β** (Bone Osteolysis), **HGF/c-MET** (Liver), **BDNF/TrkB** (Brain) |

## 5. 5. Routes of Spread

### 1. Lymphatic Spread

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Pattern** | **Sequential**: Primary → Sentinel Node → Regional Nodes → Thoracic Duct → Venous Circulation |
| **Sentinel Lymph Node (SLN)** | **First Draining Node**; **Mapping**: Technetium-99m + Blue Dye; **Biopsy** → Staging, Prognosis |
| **Tumour Types** | **Carcinomas** (Breast, Melanoma, H&N, Thyroid, Gastric, Colorectal, Gynae, Lung); **Sarcoma** (Rare) |
| **Pattern** | **Skip Metastasis** (Bypass SLN), **Interval Nodes**, **Skip Metastasis** = Poor Prognosis |

### 2. Haematogenous Spread

| Route | Cancers | Target Organs |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| **Portal Venous** | **GI Cancers** (Colorectal, Gastric, Pancreatic, Hepatocellular, Neuroendocrine) | **Liver (First Pass)** → Lung → Systemic |
| **Systemic Arterial** | **Most Carcinomas, Sarcomas, Melanoma, RCC, Thyroid** | **Lung (First Filter)** → Brain, Bone, Liver, Adrenal |
| **Pulmonary Venous** | **Pulmonary Capillaries** (Lung Primaries) | **Systemic** (Paradoxical Emboli) |
| **Caval/IVC** | **Renal, Adrenal, Hepatic, Retroperitoneal** | **Lung, Liver** |
| **Batson's Plexus** | **Prostate, Breast, Pelvic** | **Vertebral Column (Bone Mets)** |

## 6. 6. Circulating Tumour Cells (CTCs) & ctDNA

### 1. CTCs

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Definition** | **Tumour Cells in Peripheral Blood** (≥1 CTC/7.5mL = Unfavourable in Metastatic Breast/Prostate/Colorectal) |
| **Detection** | **CellSearch (EpCAM+ CK+ CD45- DAPI+)** — **FDA Cleared**; **Microfluidics**, **Size-Based (ISET)**, **CTC-iChip**, **RNA-Seq** |
| **Prognosis** | **≥5 CTCs/7.5mL (Breast/Prostate)** = Poor OS; **Baseline + Serial Monitoring** |
| **CTC Clusters** | **2-50 Cells** → **23-50x Higher Metastatic Potential** vs Single CTCs |
| **Phenotyping** | **EMT Markers** (Vimentin, Twist), **Stemness** (CD44, CD133, ALDH1), **PD-L1**, **HER2**, **AR-V7** (Prostate) |

### 2. ctDNA (Circulating Tumour DNA)

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Definition** | **Tumour-Derived DNA Fragments** in Plasma (160-180bp, <1% of cfDNA) |
| **Detection** | **NGS (Targeted Panels, WES, WGS)**, **Digital PCR (dPCR, BEAMing)**, **Methylation-Based** |
| **Applications** | **MRD Detection** (Post-Surgery, Post-Chemo), **Resistance Mutations** (EGFR T790M, ESR1, KRAS), **Tumour Fraction**, **TMB**, **MSI**, **Early Detection (Screening)** |
| **Advantages** | **Minimally Invasive**, **Real-Time**, **Spatial/Temporal Heterogeneity**, **MRD Sensitivity 10⁻⁴-10⁻⁶** |

## 7. 7. Metastatic Dormancy

### 1. Types of Dormancy

| Type | Mechanism | Triggers for Reactivation |
|------|-----------|---------------------------|
| **Cellular Dormancy** | **Single Disseminated Tumour Cell (DTC)** in **G0/G1 Arrest**; **p38high/ERKlow**, **NR2F1**, **TGF-β2**, **BMP**, **Gas6/AXL** | **Inflammation**, **Angiogenic Switch**, **Fibrosis**, **Hormonal Changes**, **Immune Decline**, **Surgery/Trauma** |
| **Micrometastatic Dormancy** | **Micrometastasis** (Balanced Proliferation/Apoptosis); **Angiogenic Dormancy** (Thrombospondin-1 > VEGF) | **Angiogenic Switch** (VEGF ↑, TSP-1 ↓), **Inflammation**, **Metabolic Changes** |
| **Immune-Mediated Dormancy** | **Equilibrium Phase** (Immunoediting); **CD8+ T Cells, IFN-γ** Control | **Immune Evasion** (MHC-I Loss, PD-L1), **Immunosenescence**, **Checkpoint Upregulation** |

> **Clinical**: **Late Recurrence** (Years-Decades) = Dormancy Escape; **Breast (ER+), Melanoma, Prostate, RCC** Prone to Dormancy.

## 8. 8. Tumour Budding

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Definition** | **Single Cells or Small Clusters (<5 Cells)** at **Invasive Front** of Tumour |
| **Mechanism** | **EMT at Invasive Front** → Detachment of Single Cells/Clusters |
| **Assessment** | **H&E**, **Cytokeratin IHC**; **Count at 20x Field** (10 Highest Budding Areas) |
| **Scoring (ITBCC)** | **Bd1 (Low): 0-4 buds**, **Bd2 (Intermediate): 5-9 buds**, **Bd3 (High): ≥10 buds** |
| **Prognosis** | **High Budding (Bd3) = Lymph Node Mets, Lymphovascular Invasion, Poor OS/DFS** |
| **Cancers** | **Colorectal (Validated)**, **Oral SCC**, **Gastric**, **Oesophageal**, **Pancreatic**, **Lung**, **Breast**, **Gynae** |

## 11. 11. Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **EMT vs MET** | **EMT**: Epithelial → Mesenchymal (Invasion, Dissemination); **MET**: Mesenchymal → Epithelial (Colonisation, Outgrowth) |
| **CTCs vs ctDNA** | **CTCs**: Whole Cells (Rare, Viability Issues, Functional Studies); **ctDNA**: DNA Fragments (Abundant, Sensitive, Mutation Tracking) |
| **Cellular vs Micrometastatic Dormancy** | **Cellular**: Single DTC, Quiescent (G0); **Micrometastatic**: Micrometastasis, Balanced Turnover, Angiogenic Dormancy |
| **Budding vs LVI** | **Budding**: Single Cells at Invasive Front (EMT); **LVI**: Tumour Cells Inside Lymph/Vascular Spaces |
| **Exosome Integrins vs Chemokines** | **Integrins**: Organotropism (Targeting); **Chemokines**: Recruitment, Survival (CXCR4/CXCL12 = Bone Marrow) |
| **Portal vs Systemic Haematogenous** | **Portal**: GI Cancers → Liver First; **Systemic**: Lung First Filter → Systemic |

**Mnemonic: METASTASIS-CASCADE**
- **M**etastasis: **90% Cancer Deaths**
- **E**MT: **SNAIL/SLUG/TWIST/ZEB** → E-cad ↓, Vimentin ↑
- **T**ransit: **Intravasation → Circulation (CTCs) → Extravasation**
- **A**noikis Resistance: **PI3K/AKT, Integrin Switching**
- **S**eed & Soil: **Paget** (Seed + Soil = Organotropism)
- **T**ransport: **Lymphatic (Sequential) vs Haematogenous (Portal/Systemic)**
- **A**ngiogenesis: **VEGF/HIF-1α** (Colonisation)
- **S**tem Cells: **CSCs (CD44, CD133, ALDH1)** → Colonisation
- **I**ntravasation: **EMT + MMPs + Motility**
- **S**temness: **Partial EMT = Highest Stemness**
- **C**TCs: **CellSearch (EpCAM/CK/CD45)**, **Clusters = High Risk**
- **A**ngio Switch: **VEGF > TSP-1** (Colonisation)
- **D**ormancy: **Cellular (NR2F1) / Micro (TSP-1/VEGF) / Immune**
- **E**xosomes: **Integrins → Organotropism (α6β4 Lung, αvβ5 Liver, α2β1 Bone)**
- **S**oil: **Target Organ Microenvironment**
