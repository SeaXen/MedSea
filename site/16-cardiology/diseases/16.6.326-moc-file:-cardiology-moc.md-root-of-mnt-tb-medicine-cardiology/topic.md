## 3. Section Components
Each section includes:
- **Checklist** of subtopics
- **Progress tracker** (% complete)
- **High-yield tables** (differential, classification)
- **Viva topics** with sample questions
- **Cross-links** to related MOCs (Medicine, Pediatrics)

## 4. Usage
- **Navigation**: Wikilinks `[[Topic]]` and `[[Section/Topic]]`
- **Daily review**: Tick off completed items

- **Viva prep**: All MRCP/FCPS cardiology topics in one place
