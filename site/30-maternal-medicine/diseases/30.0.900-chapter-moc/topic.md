## Key Themes for FCPS/MRCP

- **Mnemonics**: PRE-ECLAMPSIA (risk factors), H-E-L-L-P (severe PE variant), M-M-R-V (vaccines in pregnancy)
- **Investigations**: BP, urinalysis, OGTT, growth scans, uterine artery Doppler, sFlt-1/PlGF, bile acids
- **Pharmacology**: Labetalol, nifedipine, methyldopa (safe antihypertensives); insulin first-line; LMWH for VTE
- **Avoid in pregnancy**: ACEI/ARB, warfarin (1st trimester), valproate, methotrexate, MMF, NSAIDs (3rd trimester)
- **Emergencies**: Eclampsia, HELLP, AFLP, AFE, PPH, sepsis, pulmonary HTN crisis
- **Special populations**: pre-existing heart/kidney/diabetes, multiple pregnancy, adolescent, advanced maternal age
- **Drug safety**: paracetamol, penicillins safe; avoid ACEI/ARB, warfarin, valproate, methotrexate, tetracyclines, quinolones
