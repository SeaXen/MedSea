# Antenatal care and booking

---

## Definition

Antenatal care is the structured care pathway from confirmation of pregnancy to delivery. The booking visit establishes risk, performs initial screening, and initiates a care plan. Continuity of care and timely screening improve outcomes.

## Core Clinical Content

### First contact
GPs/booking visit ideally by 10 weeks; comprehensive history and examination

### Confirm pregnancy
Urine β-hCG; dating by last menstrual period (LMP); first-trimester USS for crown-rump length (CRL)

### History
Past obstetric (mode of delivery, complications, GDM/PE); past medical; family (thrombosis, diabetes); social; mental health

### Examination
BMI, BP, urine, breast; abdominal examination later

### First-trimester screening
Combined test (nuchal translucency + β-hCG + PAPP-A) at 11-13+6 weeks; cfDNA for aneuploidy if high risk

### Booking bloods
FBC, blood group + antibody screen, haemoglobinopathy, HIV, syphilis, hepatitis B, rubella, MSU

### Anomaly scan
18-20+6 weeks; structural survey; soft markers

### Fetal growth
Serial symphyseal fundal height (SFH); third-trimester growth scans if risk factors

### Visits
Nulliparous — 10 visits; parous — 7 visits; schedule depends on risk

### Lifestyle
Smoking cessation; alcohol/illicit drug avoidance; diet (listeria); exercise; work adjustments

## Confusions & Mnemonics

- **Mnemonic for Antenatal care and booking** — see key points in Core Clinical Content
- **'PRE-ECLAMPSIA' risk factors** — Previous PE, Renal disease, Extremes of age, Chronic HTN, Ethnicity, Antiphospholipid, nulliparity, Multiple, Obesity, Smoking
- **'Mnemonic: H-E-L-L-P'** — Haemolysis, Elevated Liver enzymes, Low Platelets
- **'CHAP' trial target** — Chronic HTN treated to <140/90 mmHg
- **'M-M-R-V' safe vaccines in pregnancy** — Measles-Mumps-Rubella (MMR, avoid), inactivated influenza, COVID-19, Whooping cough (dTpa)
