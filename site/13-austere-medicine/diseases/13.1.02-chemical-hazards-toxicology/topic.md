## 2. 📖 Heavy Metals — Major Toxicities

| Metal | Sources | Target Organs | Key Clinical Features | Biomonitoring | Treatment |
|-------|---------|---------------|----------------------|--------------|-----------|
| **Lead (Pb)** | Paint, batteries, plumbing, gasoline (historical), smelting, ceramics, spices, toys | **CNS (neurodev), Haematopoietic (anaemia), Renal, CV, GI** | **Children**: IQ loss, behavioural, abdominal pain, anaemia<br>**Adults**: Neuropathy, hypertension, gout, infertility | **Blood Pb (BLL)**: **<5 µg/dL (CDC reference)**<br>**ZPP** (Zinc Protoporphyrin) | **Chelation**: **Succimer (DMSA) PO**, **CaNa₂EDTA IV**, **D-penicillamine**<br>**Threshold**: **BLL ≥45 µg/dL** (symptomatic), **≥20 µg/dL** (asymptomatic child) |
| **Mercury (Hg)** | **Elemental**: Thermometers, dental amalgam, gold mining<br>**Inorganic**: Skin lightening, disinfectants<br>**Methylmercury (MeHg)**: Fish (predatory), rice | **Elemental**: CNS (tremor, erethism), renal, respiratory<br>**Methylmercury**: **Neurodevelopmental (fetal)**, sensory, motor | **Blood Hg** (MeHg), **Urine Hg** (Inorganic), **Hair Hg** (MeHg, long-term) | **Chelation**: **DMPS/DMSA** (Inorganic), **No proven chelator for MeHg** (Supportive) |
| **Arsenic (As)** | **Groundwater (Geogenic)**, Pesticides (historical), Mining, Smelting, CCA Wood | **Skin (Hyperkeratosis, Cancer)**, **CVD**, **Diabetes**, **Cancer (Skin, Lung, Bladder)**, **Neuro** | **Urine As (Inorganic + Metabolites)**: **<10 µg/L** (Speciated best) | **Chelation**: **DMPS/DMSA** (Acute), **Supportive** (Chronic) |
| **Cadmium (Cd)** | **Batteries, Plating, Fertilisers (Phosphate), Smelting, Tobacco** | **Renal (Tubular Proteinuria, Itai-Itai), Bone (Osteomalacia), Lung Cancer, Prostate** | **Blood Cd** (Recent), **Urine Cd** (Body Burden), **β2-Microglobulin** (Renal) | **No Effective Chelation** → **Source Reduction**, **Iron/Zinc Supplementation** |
| **Manganese (Mn)** | Welding, Mining, Batteries, Fuel Additive (MMT) | **Neuro (Manganism: Parkinsonism)**, **Lung** | **Blood Mn**, **Urine Mn**, **MRI (Basal Ganglia)** | **Supportive**, **Iron Repletion** (Competitive Absorption) |

> [!tip] **Heavy Metal Toxicity = Chronic > Acute**. **Biomonitoring = Blood (Recent), Urine (Inorganic/Recent), Hair (Long-term/MeHg)**.

## 3. 📖 Pesticides

### Classification & Toxicity

| Class | Examples | Mechanism | Acute Toxicity | Chronic Effects |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Organophosphates (OPs)** | Malathion, Parathion, Chlorpyrifos, Diazinon, Dichlorvos | **Irreversible AChE Inhibition** → **Cholinergic Crisis** (SLUDGE: Salivation, Lacrimation, Urination, Defecation, GI cramps, Emesis + Bradycardia, Miosis, Bronchorrhoea, Muscle Fasciculations, Weakness, Respiratory Failure) | **High** (LD50 Low) | **Neuropathy (OPIDN)**, **Neurodevelopmental**, **Carcinogenicity** (Some) |
| **Carbamates** | Carbaryl, Aldicarb, Carbofuran, Methomyl | **Reversible AChE Inhibition** | **Moderate-High** | **Less Neurodevelopmental than OPs** |
| **Organochlorines (OCs)** | **DDT, Dieldrin, Aldrin, Endrin, Heptachlor, Chlordane, Mirex, Lindane (γ-HCH)** | **Na⁺ Channel Modulation** (Prolonged Depolarisation), **GABA Antagonism** | **Low Acute** | **Persistent, Bioaccumulative, Endocrine Disruption, Cancer (DDT), Neurotoxicity** |
| **Pyrethroids** (Type I/II) | Permethrin, Cypermethrin, Deltamethrin, Cyfluthrin | **Na⁺ Channel Modulation** (Type I: Transient, Type II: Prolonged) | **Low (Mammals)**, High (Insects) | **Endocrine Disruption, Neurodevelopmental** |
| **Neonicotinoids** | Imidacloprid, Clothianidin, Thiamethoxam | **nAChR Agonism** (Insects) | **Low (Mammals)** | **Bee Colony Collapse**, **Neurodevelopmental?** |
| **Glyphosate** | **Herbicide (Roundup)** | **EPSPS Inhibition** (Shikimate Pathway) | **Low Acute** | **IARC Group 2A (Probable Carcinogen)**, Microbiome, Endocrine |

### Acute Pesticide Poisoning Management

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1. Decontamination** | **Remove Clothing, Wash Skin/Hair (Soap/Water)**, **Ocular Irrigation** |
| **2. Resuscitation** | **ABCs**, **Atropine 2mg IV** (Repeat q5-10min until Dry Secretions/HR>80), **Oxime (Pralidoxime 1-2g IV)** if OP **<24-48h** |
| **3. Supportive** | **Ventilation** (Intubation), **Seizure Control** (Benzodiazepines), **Fluids/Electrolytes** |
| **4. Enhanced Elimination** | **Gastric Lavage (If <1h, Protected Airway)**, **Activated Charcoal 50g**, **Alkaline Diuresis** (Some), **Haemodialysis** (Severe, Dialyzable) |
| **5. Monitor** | **Cholinesterase Activity (RBC/Plasma)**, **ECG, LFT, Renal, Coagulation** |

> [!warning] **Atropine = Life-Saving in OP Poisoning**. **Oxime (Pralidoxime) = Only Effective if Given Early (<24-48h)**. **Carbamates = Reversible → Atropine Usually Sufficient, Avoid Oximes**.

## 4. 📖 Industrial Chemicals — Key Toxicities

| Chemical | Use / Source | Target Organ / Toxicity |
|----------|--------------|-------------------------|
| **Asbestos** (Chrysotile, Amphibole) | Insulation, Roofing, Tiles, Brake Pads | **Pleural Plaques, Asbestosis, Mesothelioma, Lung Cancer** (Latency 20-40y) |
| **Silica (Crystalline Quartz)** | Mining, Sandblasting, Construction, Ceramics | **Silicosis (Nodular Fibrosis)**, **Lung Cancer**, **Autoimmune (SLE, RA, Scleroderma), TB Risk** |

| **Dioxins (TCDD)** / **Furans / PCBs** | Waste Incineration, Industrial Accidents (Seveso), Transformers | **Chloracne, Endocrine Disruption, Immune Suppression, Cancer (TCDD Group 1)** |
| **PFAS (PFOA, PFOS, GenX)** | Non-stick, Firefighting Foam, Textiles | **Immune Suppression, Thyroid Disruption, Cancer (Kidney, Testicular), Developmental** |

## 5. 📖 Dioxins, Furans, PCBs — POPs (Persistent Organic Pollutants)

| Compound | Source | Key Toxicity | Stockholm Convention |
|----------|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| **TCDD (2,3,7,8-Tetrachlorodibenzo-p-dioxin)** | Waste Incineration, Herbicide Production (Agent Orange), Pulp Bleaching | **Most Toxic Synthetic Chemical**, **Carcinogen (Group 1)**, **Chloracne, Endocrine, Immune, Developmental** | **Listed (Elimination)** |
| **PCBs (Polychlorinated Biphenyls)** | Transformers, Capacitors, Hydraulic Fluids, Paints | **Neurodevelopmental, Endocrine, Immune, Cancer (Liver, Melanoma)** | **Listed (Elimination)** |
| **PCDD/Fs (Dioxins/Furans)** | Combustion, Chlorine Bleaching, Metal Smelting | **Similar to TCDD**, **TEQ (Toxic Equivalency) Based on TCDD** | **Listed** |

> [!tip] **TEQ (Toxic Equivalency) = Sum of (Concentration × TEF)** for each Congener. **TCDD = Reference (TEF=1)**. **WHO-TEQ Used for Risk Assessment**.

## 6. 📖 PFAS (Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances) — "Forever Chemicals"

| Compound | Use | Health Concerns | Regulation |
|----------|-------|-----------------|------------|
| **PFOA / PFOS** | Non-stick (PTFE), Firefighting Foam (AFFF), Textiles, Food Packaging | **Immune Dysfunction, Thyroid Disruption, Cancer (Kidney, Testicular), Developmental, Cholesterol** | **Stockholm Convention (PFOS Listed, PFOA Under Review)**, **EU/US Drinking Water Limits (4-70 ng/L)** |
| **GenX / Short-Chain** | **Replacements** | **Similar Toxicity Concerns** | **Under Scrutiny** |

> [!warning] **PFAS = "Forever Chemicals" (C-F Bond Stability)**. **Bioaccumulate, Persistent, Mobile**. **Drinking Water = Major Exposure Route**.

## 7. 📖 Radiation Hazards

| Type | Source | Health Effects | Protection |
|--------|--------|----------------|------------|
| **Ionising** | **Radon** (Indoor, #2 Lung Cancer Cause), **Medical (CT, X-ray, Nuclear Med)**, **Occupational (Miners, Nuclear Workers)**, **Nuclear Accidents (Chernobyl, Fukushima)** | **Cancer (Leukaemia, Thyroid, Breast, Lung, Solid Tumours)**, **Cataracts**, **Hereditary Effects**, **Acute Radiation Syndrome (ARS)** | **ALARA (As Low As Reasonably Achievable)**, **Time, Distance, Shielding**, **Dose Limits (Public 1 mSv/yr, Workers 20 mSv/yr)** |
| **Non-Ionising (UV)** | **Sun, Tanning Beds** | **Skin Cancer (Melanoma, NMSC), Cataracts, Photoaging, Immune Suppression** | **Sun Protection (Slip, Slop, Slap, Seek, Slide)** |
| **Non-Ionising (RF/EMF)** | **Mobile Phones, Base Stations, WiFi, Power Lines** | **IARC 2B (Possible Carcinogen)**, **Thermal Effects (High Power)**, **EHS (Controversial)** | **Precautionary, SAR Limits, Distance** |

> [!warning] **Radon = #2 Cause of Lung Cancer** (After Smoking). **Test Homes in High-Radon Areas**. **Medical Radiation = Largest Man-Made Source** → **Justification & Optimisation (ALARA)**.

## 8. 📖 Risk Assessment & Biomonitoring

### Human Health Risk Assessment (HHRA) Steps

| Step | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **1. Hazard Identification** | **Can the chemical cause harm?** (Animal, Human, Mechanistic Data) |
| **2. Dose-Response Assessment** | **NOAEL/LOAEL → BMD → RfD (Non-Cancer) / CSF (Cancer)** |
| **3. Exposure Assessment** | **Source → Pathway → Receptor** (Concentration × Intake Rate × Duration × Frequency) |
| **4. Risk Characterization** | **Risk = Hazard × Exposure** (Qualitative/Quantitative), **Uncertainty Analysis** |

### Biomonitoring — Key Matrices

| Matrix | Reflects | Half-Life / Window | Examples |
|--------|----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Blood** | **Recent Exposure** (Hours-Months) | **Pb (30d), Hg (MeHg 50d), Cd (Months), POPs (Years)** | **Lead, Mercury, POPs, VOCs** |
| **Urine** | **Recent/Excreted** (Hours-Days) | **As (Inorganic), Cd, Pesticides, Phthalates, BPA, PAH Metabolites** | **Arsenic, Pesticides, Phthalates** |
| **Hair** | **Long-Term (Months-Years)** | **MeHg (Hair Growth ~1cm/month), Metals** | **Methylmercury, Chronic Metal Exposure** |
| **Nails** | **Long-Term (Months)** | **As, Se, Heavy Metals** | **Arsenic** |
| **Breast Milk** | **Lipophilic POPs** | **Lactation Period** | **PCBs, Dioxins, OCPs, BFRs** |
| **Exhaled Breath** | **Volatile Organics** | **Real-Time** | **VOCs, Solvents** |

| Framework | Scope | Key Provisions |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| **REACH (EU)** | **Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation, Restriction of Chemicals** | **Industry Responsibility**, **SVHC (Substances of Very High Concern)**, **Authorisation, Restriction** |

| **IARC Monographs** | **Cancer Classification (Group 1, 2A, 2B, 3, 4)** | **Carcinogen Identification** |

## 11. ❓ Viva Questions

1. **What are the clinical features of acute organophosphate poisoning?**
   - **SLUDGE**: Salivation, Lacrimation, Urination, Defecation, GI cramps, Emesis; **Miosis, Bradycardia, Bronchorrhoea, Muscle Fasciculations, Weakness, Respiratory Failure**.

2. **What is the antidote for organophosphate poisoning and when should it be given?**
   - **Atropine 2mg IV** (Repeat q5-10min until dry secretions/HR>80); **Pralidoxime 1-2g IV** (Within 24-48h).

3. **What are the health effects of chronic arsenic exposure?**
   - **Skin lesions (Hyperkeratosis, Hyperpigmentation), Cancer (Skin, Lung, Bladder), CVD, Diabetes, Neurotoxicity**.

4. **Why is cadmium particularly toxic to the kidneys?**
   - **Accumulates in Proximal Tubules** → **Tubular Proteinuria (β2-Microglobulin), Fanconi Syndrome, Osteomalacia (Itai-Itai Disease)**.

5. **What is the mechanism of benzene-induced leukaemia?**
   - **Metabolites (Benzene Oxide, Quinones) → DNA Damage, Chromosomal Aberrations (Aneuploidy, Translocations) → AML**.

6. **What is the significance of dioxins (TCDD) and how are they measured?**
   - **Most Toxic Synthetic Chemical**, **Carcinogen Group 1**, **Measured as TEQ (Toxic Equivalency) relative to TCDD**.

6. **What are PFAS and why are they called "Forever Chemicals"?**
   - **Per/Polyfluoroalkyl Substances**, **C-F Bond Extremely Stable** → **Persistent, Bioaccumulative, Mobile** → **Drinking Water Major Exposure**.

7. **How is radiation risk assessed for radon exposure?**
   - **Radon = #2 Lung Cancer Cause**, **Indoor Accumulation**, **Test Homes in High-Risk Areas**, **Mitigation (Sub-Slab Depressurisation)**.

7. **What are the key biomarkers for lead exposure?**
   - **Blood Lead Level (BLL) = Recent Exposure**, **Zinc Protoporphyrin (ZPP) = Chronic Effect**, **Bone Lead (XRF) = Cumulative Burden** (Research).

8. **What is the management of acute arsenic poisoning?**
   - **Chelation: DMPS/DMSA** (IV/PO), **Supportive (Fluids, Electrolytes, Cardiac Monitoring)**, **Source Removal**.

8. **What is the significance of biomonitoring in environmental health?**
   - **Measures Internal Dose (Integrates All Routes)**, **Reflects Biologically Effective Dose**, **Guides Risk Assessment & Public Health Action**.

## 12. 🧠 Confusions & Mnemonics

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Organophosphates vs Carbamates** | **OP = Irreversible AChE Inhibition (Requires Oxime)**; **Carbamate = Reversible (Atropine Usually Sufficient)** |
| **Acute vs Chronic Heavy Metal** | **Acute = GI, Shock, CNS**; **Chronic = Neuro, Renal, Cancer, Endocrine** |
| **Asbestos vs Silica** | **Asbestos = Mesothelioma, Pleural Plaques**; **Silica = Silicosis, Lung Cancer, Autoimmune** |
| **Dioxins vs PCBs** | **Both POPs, Chlorinated**, **TCDD = Most Toxic Dioxin**, **PCBs = Transformers, Neurodevelopmental** |
| **PFAS vs Other Chemicals** | **PFAS = C-F Bond (Forever), Persistent, Bioaccumulative, Ubiquitous, Multi-Organ Toxicity** |
