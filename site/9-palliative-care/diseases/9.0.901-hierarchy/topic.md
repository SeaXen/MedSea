# Davidson Chapter 8: Pain and Palliative Care - Topic Hierarchy

> **Source:** Davidson's Principles and Practice of Medicine, 24th Edition, Chapter 8: Pain and Palliative Care (LA Colvin, M Fallon)
> **Target:** FCPS / MRCP Part 1, Part 2, PACES

## Hierarchy Structure

### 1. Pain Physiology & Assessment (4 Topics)

#### 1.1 Pain Physiology
- Pain physiology and pathways (nociception, transmission, modulation)
- Nociceptive pain (somatic, visceral)
- Neuropathic pain
- Total pain concept
- Pain threshold and tolerance
- Central sensitisation and wind-up

#### 1.2 Pain Assessment
- Pain history (SOCRATES + oncology-specific)
- Pain examination
- Pain assessment tools (VAS, NRS, VRS, FLACC)
- Pain in non-communicating patients
- Pain in dementia and cognitive impairment

### 2. Non-Opioid Analgesics (4 Topics)

#### 2.1 Paracetamol
- Mechanism (COX inhibition, central)
- Dosing
- Toxicity (hepatocellular necrosis, NAC)

#### 2.2 NSAIDs
- Classification (traditional vs COX-2)
- Mechanism (COX-1/COX-2)
- Efficacy
- Side effects (GI, renal, CV)
- Drug interactions

#### 2.3 Topical Analgesics
- Topical NSAIDs (diclofenac, ibuprofen)
- Topical capsaicin
- Topical lidocaine

### 3. Opioid Analgesics (5 Topics)

#### 3.1 Weak Opioids
- Codeine
- Tramadol
- Dihydrocodeine

#### 3.2 Strong Opioids
- Morphine
- Oxycodone
- Hydromorphone
- Methadone
- Tapentadol

#### 3.3 Transdermal Opioids
- Fentanyl patch
- Buprenorphine patch

### 4. Neuropathic Pain (5 Topics)

#### 4.1 Neuropathic Pain — Overview
- Definition, mechanism, epidemiology
- Assessment (LANSS, DN4 questionnaires)
- Treatment approach

#### 4.2 Specific Neuropathic Pain Conditions
- Trigeminal neuralgia
- Post-herpetic neuralgia
- Painful diabetic neuropathy
- Central post-stroke pain
- Complex regional pain syndrome (CRPS)

### 5. Cancer Pain Syndromes (4 Topics)

#### 5.1 Bone Pain
- Metastatic bone pain
- Multiple myeloma
- Bone metastases (breast, prostate, lung, kidney, thyroid)

#### 5.2 Visceral Pain
- Liver capsule pain
- Pancreatic cancer
- Bowel obstruction

#### 5.3 Neuropathic Cancer Pain
- Brachial plexus infiltration
- Lumbosacral plexus infiltration
- Spinal cord compression
- Leptomeningeal disease

#### 5.4 Incident and Breakthrough Pain
- Breakthrough pain (spontaneous, incident)
- End-of-dose failure

### 6. Adjuvant Analgesics (5 Topics)

#### 6.1 Antidepressants
- TCAs (amitriptyline, nortriptyline)
- SNRIs (duloxetine, venlafaxine)

#### 6.2 Anticonvulsants
- Gabapentin
- Pregabalin
- Carbamazepine (trigeminal neuralgia)

#### 6.3 NMDA Antagonists
- Ketamine
- Magnesium

#### 6.4 Other Adjuvants
- Corticosteroids (dexamethasone)
- Bisphosphonates (bone pain)
- Capsaicin (topical)
- Lidocaine (topical and IV)

### 7. Opioid Management (5 Topics)

#### 7.1 Opioid Side Effects
- Constipation
- Nausea and vomiting
- Respiratory depression
- Sedation and cognitive impairment
- Pruritus
- Opioid-induced hyperalgesia

#### 7.2 Opioid Rotation / Switching
- Equianalgesic table
- Incomplete cross-tolerance
- When to rotate
- Practical steps

#### 7.3 Opioid Tolerance and Dependence
- Physiological tolerance
- Dependence vs addiction
- Pseudo-addiction

### 8. Non-Pain Symptoms in Palliative Care (8 Topics)

#### 8.1 Gastrointestinal
- Nausea and vomiting
- Constipation
- Bowel obstruction (malignant)
- Anorexia-cachexia
- Dysphagia
- Hiccups

#### 8.2 Respiratory
- Breathlessness / dyspnoea
- Cough
- Respiratory secretions (death rattle)
- Pleural effusions (symptomatic)

#### 8.3 Neuropsychiatric
- Delirium
- Anxiety
- Depression
- Insomnia
- Terminal agitation / restlessness

#### 9.1 Malignant Spinal Cord Compression (MSCC)
- Presentation, diagnosis, emergency management

#### 9.2 Superior Vena Cava Obstruction
- Causes, presentation, management

#### 9.3 Hypercalcaemia of Malignancy
- Causes, presentation, management

#### 9.4 Catastrophic Haemorrhage
- Terminal care, anticipatory prescribing

#### 9.5 Other Emergencies
- Seizures
- Acute pain crisis
- Acute dyspnoea

### 10. End-of-Life Care (5 Topics)

#### 10.1 Care of the Dying
- Recognition of dying
- Priorities of care
- Symptom management in last days

#### 10.2 Syringe Drivers (Continuous Subcutaneous Infusion)
- Indications
- Common drug combinations
- Practical aspects

#### 10.3 Terminal Agitation and Palliative Sedation
- Assessment
- Management
- Ethical considerations

#### 10.4 Last Days of Life
- Symptom control
- Family support
- Place of care and death

### 11. Communication and Ethics (5 Topics)

#### 11.1 Breaking Bad News
- SPIKES protocol
- Practical aspects

#### 11.2 Advance Care Planning
